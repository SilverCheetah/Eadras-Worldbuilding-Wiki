Arathis

Ancient, pre-breaking civilization powerful in magic and magitech. Race of same name were humanoid reptiles.

**Pre-Worldbreaker Arathi Culture**

**Society** Arathi society was the first true civilization on Eadras. They were highly organized, hierarchical, and completely dominated the world for at least ten thousand years. Their culture emphasized mastery, order, and the harmonious blending of magic and technology.

**Culture** The Arathi were masters of magitek, crystalmancy, and nearly every civilized art. They built their entire civilization around the flow of Eldritch energy. Raw Eldritch radiated from the sun, struck the planet’s bedrock, reflected in a usable form, and flowed through ley lines into seven massive **Eldritch Conduits** — magical poles that pierced the world and returned the energy to the planet’s core to be recycled.

Their crystalmancy allowed them to capture and store Eldritch inside crystals, using them like batteries to power magitek devices.

**Leadership** They were ruled by a powerful council of archmages and crystalmancers. The center of their power was **Altea**, the heart of the entire world’s magic network.

**Appearance & Dress** Arathi were tall, elegant insectoid-reptilian hybrids with iridescent scales and colorful feathered crests. They adorned themselves with intricate crystal jewelry and glowing magitek ornaments that floated around their bodies.

**Cities** Their greatest cities were built atop highland plateaus directly over the Eldritch Conduits. They constructed enormous crystal structures on top of these conduits to harvest and store vast amounts of Eldritch energy. These cities featured towering crystal spires, floating platforms, and impossible magitek architecture that pulsed with arcane power.

**Remnants of the Arathi Civilization**

**Deep Vaults** Almost all of the Arathi’s legendary underground repositories were destroyed during the Cataclysm. The few that remain are incredibly rare. Three of these vaults have been opened in recorded history: the first by **Darkhoof**, the same unicorn who would later wield the **Spear of Crystallized Entropy**, the second by a Velrothi archmage, and most recently by a Gnome. No one knows how many more may still exist, still sealed and untouched after ten million years. They are believed to contain pristine magitek, stored Eldritch crystals, and lost knowledge from the Golden Age.

**Crystal Conduit Ruins** The seven great Eldritch Conduits were shattered during the Cataclysm. Only small fragments of these once-mighty poles still exist — known as **Conduit Ends**. These jagged remnants jut from mountainsides or sea floors and continue to leak dangerous amounts of raw Eldritch energy, creating deadly wild magic zones and mutating any life that lingers nearby.

**Floating Crystal Shards** A handful of city districts and crystal towers somehow survived the Cataclysm and still float in the sky. These pitiful remnants drift slowly across the world. Some have crashed onto the land or into the ocean, at depths ranging from shallow waters to the deepest trenches.

**Artifacts** Arathi artifacts are the rarest and most powerful relics on Eadras. Three of the most significant surviving pieces are:

*   **Orichalcum Ring of Command** — Forged from orichalcum, this ring allows the wearer to summon and command an army of up to five thousand orichalcum golems from **anywhere on the Prime Material Plane**.
*   **Rod of Transmutation** — A powerful crystal-powered artifact with no known limits. It can transmute one substance into another, powered by a crystalline energy source.
*   **Crystalline Forge** — Appears as a necklace with a large crystal pendant. When thrown to the ground and the command word is spoken, it rapidly expands into a fully functional magitek factory made of crystalline and metallic components. It uses magitek to produce whatever the user commands it to create, with no known limits on size or complexity.
