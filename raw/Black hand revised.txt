Black hand revised

The black hand is a shadowy secretive organization. To non-members, its goals are obscure, its scope is unknowable, and its agents either linger in every shadow or they don't exist (telling themselves it is a boogeyman story to scare the gullible)

Those better informed, they know that the Black hand seeks power through information gathering, blackmail, espionage, infiltration, and less savory means. Power is the goal.

No one outside the top level rulers know about the Black Hand's connection to Xarvion or the search for the Spear.

To most of its members joining is about gaining personal power.

Unfortunately, this means that a great deal of what is currently written is wrong.
