The Flesh-Eaters
The Flesh-Eaters are a small, secretive cabal of dragons who proudly call themselves traditionalists. They believe that the only true and proper state of the world is when dragons ruled over all other races as apex predators. They openly reject the reforms and philosophies that emerged from the Great Exchange and the later Council of Ninety-Nine.
These dragons engage in the systematic hunting, consumption, and terrorization of smaller sapient races. They are particularly notorious for targeting the young of these species, considering their flesh the greatest delicacy. Among their most hated prey are unicorns, whom they still view as nothing more than exceptionally magical livestock.
Even among their own kind, the Flesh-Eaters are reviled. They are known to raid the nests of other dragons, stealing eggs to raise the hatchlings in isolation according to their own brutal ideology. These stolen young are indoctrinated from the moment they hatch to believe in the supremacy of dragonkind and the divine right of predation.
While the Council of Ninety-Nine officially denounces the Flesh-Eaters and has declared them enemies of dragonkind, the cabal has proven extremely difficult to eradicate. They operate in small, highly mobile cells, often hiding in remote wilderness or among the ruins of ancient draconic empires. Their continued existence remains one of the darkest stains on modern dragon society.
Note: Most dragons consider even speaking the name "Flesh-Eaters" in polite company to be in extremely poor taste.
