Axes of Creation

The Creator & Creatrix decided on four opposing principles in the Mythos they built.

**Masculine/Feminine axis** From full masculine (drive, structure, conquest) to full feminine (receptivity, intuition, creation). A war-god might sit pure masculine—unbreakable, aggressive. A spirit-weaver pure feminine—endless, flowing. No rule says you can't. But extremes? Masculine alone turns brittle; feminine alone turns vague. The axis tracks it—nothing's "supposed" to be center.

**Altruism/Self Interest axis** Full altruist: gives till empty. Full self-interest: takes till hated. Both exist—saints, tyrants, everything between. No cosmic pull-back. Just... live with the fallout. Pure give? You fade. Pure take? You isolate. The mythos lets it happen.

**Obedient/Independent axis** Total obedience: slave-mind, no will. Total independence: lone wolf, no bonds. Heroes might hover middle; fanatics or anarchists hit the ends. No enforcement—just results. Obey too hard? You're tool. Rebel too hard? You're chaos.

**Wisdom/Foolishness axis** Full wisdom: ancient, cautious, deep. Full foolishness: wild, impulsive, fresh. Sages wisdom-side; jesters foolishness-side. No "should." Wisdom can stagnate; foolishness can innovate. The axis just marks where you stand—consequences follow.

Everything was created aligning to these axes.
