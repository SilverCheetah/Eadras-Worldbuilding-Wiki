DIVINE BUREAUCRACY AND CELESTIAL ADMINISTRATION
===============================================

The Divine Bureaucracy is real.

Functions include:
- prayer routing
- miracle authorization
- doctrinal classification
- inter-domain conflict management
- continuity of administration

Major themes:
- accumulated abstraction
- procedural exhaustion
- empathy erosion
- institutional inertia
- delayed intervention

HELL'S DANGER
--------------
Hell's danger is not merely cruelty.
Its danger is administrative clarity.

The Infernal Bureaucracy optimizes extraction rather than flourishing.

OVERSIGHT GAP
-------------
The gods are not continuously aware of all actions performed in their names.

This oversight gap contributed directly to:
- the Sorcerer Crusade
- delayed intervention events
- institutional theological drift

PRAYER TAXONOMY
---------------
Four broad forms:
- Asking
- Thanking
- Witnessing
- Releasing

Only Asking heavily burdens divine channels.

SHRINE OF FAILED SIMPLIFICATIONS
--------------------------------
A semi-comic Celestian site preserving failed attempts to streamline divine administration.
