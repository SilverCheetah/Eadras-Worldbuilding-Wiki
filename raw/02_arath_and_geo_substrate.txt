## I. What the Arathi Actually Were

**Vos:**  
Modern scholarship often imagines the Arathi as cold. That is wrong. They were disciplined, hierarchical, and precise, yes. But not loveless. Their cities sang. Their transit platforms carried workers, philosophers, nestlings, magistrates, soldiers, gardeners, and the dying. Their crystal archives preserved lullabies beside constitutional law.

**Nara:**  
Their households were communal and layered. Not like most modern families. Arathi kinship was organized through **brood-lines**, **memory houses**, and **civic parentage**. A child belonged to its biological clutch, its instructional hall, and its city-oath.

**Raja:**  
Too many parents.

**Nara:**  
Sometimes. But it meant no child was supposed to be abandoned. Their shame was not neglect. Their shame was calibration.

**Sylph:**  
A terrible thing, to be loved by a committee.

**Nara:**  
Yes.

**Lirra:**  
Their species’ insectoid-reptilian biology mattered. They did not experience individuality exactly as most modern peoples do. They thought in nested obligations: self, brood, function, city, conduit, world. Their architecture reflected this. No house was fully private. No public space was fully impersonal.

**Vos:**  
Which modern ruin-delvers misunderstand constantly. They find a chamber and ask, “Was this temple, office, nursery, archive, or transit node?” The answer is often yes.

**Nox:**  
Arathi categories bled into each other because their civilization believed separation was inefficiency.

**Qoxli:**  
Nature separates. Skin from air. Root from stone. Nest from hunting ground. Season from season.

**Vos:**  
Nature also integrates.

**Qoxli:**  
Integration is not the same as total system.

**Ioa:**  
That distinction should be carved above every Deep Vault.

## II. Crystalmancy: Beauty as Infrastructure

**Lirra:**  
Crystalmancy must not be treated as “old magic with crystals.” That is insulting and dangerous. It was a complete civilizational grammar.

Arathi crystals were not dead minerals. They were cultivated lineages: grown, sung, tuned, cut, healed, bred, and sometimes retired. A transit crystal, a memory crystal, and a judicial resonance lattice were as different as a horse, a library, and a court.

**Raja:**  
Good. That is useful. Modern artificers keep treating fragments like interchangeable parts.

**Lirra:**  
And losing hands.

**Raja:**

**Lirra:**

**Sylph:**

There were recognized crystal lineages. We should name a few.

**Lirra:**  
Carefully.

**Vos:**  
Carefully, then.

**Lirra:**  
The **Saelith Choirs** were used in communication arrays. They carried harmonic impressions across vast distances, not words exactly, but structured meaning. Modern “speaking crystals” are crude descendants or imitations.

The **Veyruun Bones** stabilized architecture. They were grown through foundations, towers, bridges, and floating platforms. They distributed stress through magical resonance.

The **Ishkara Veins** regulated civic energy flow. They connected districts to conduit-derived power without allowing direct surge.

The **Thamrite Mirrors** stored memory, law, image, and sensory record. Most surviving archive shards are degraded Thamrite derivatives.

The **Oru-Sha Blades** were military crystals, adaptive and extremely restricted. If one is found intact, the proper action is not study. It is evacuation.

**Lirra:**

**Nox:**

**Raja:**  
Were the crystal lineages alive?

**Lirra:**

**Qoxli:**  
If you have to ask, you have already done something rude.

**Lirra:**  
They grew, reacted, adapted, degraded, and in some cases resisted misuse. They did not possess souls in the ordinary sense. But Arathi cultivation blurred organism, tool, and spell.

**Shani:**  
Did they suffer?

**Lirra:**  
Some did, near the end.

**Shali:**  
Because everything suffers when made to deny its nature.

Lirra:
The Arathi built under assumptions of planetary continuity. Their architecture, conduit routing, deep vault placement, and geo-substrate cultivation all presumed a crust that did not crawl away from itself.

# Tome Notes for the Worldbuilder

## 1. New Canon-Worthy Ideas Generated

- **Arathi kinship structure:**  
  Arathi children belonged simultaneously to biological **brood-lines**, educational **memory houses**, and civic parentage through the city-oath. Their society blurred family, institution, and state.

- **Major crystal lineages of Arathi crystalmancy:**
  - **Saelith Choirs:** communication and meaning-transmission crystals.  
  - **Veyruun Bones:** architectural stabilization crystals.  
  - **Ishkara Veins:** civic energy regulation crystals.  
  - **Thamrite Mirrors:** memory, legal, sensory, and archival storage crystals.  
  - **Oru-Sha Blades:** military/adaptive weapon crystals; extremely dangerous.

- **Key Arathi phrase:**  
  **“What is harmonized is entrusted.”**  
  Originally a stewardship phrase, later corrupted toward control and mastery.

- **Post-Arathi proverb:**  
  **“The conduit does not know your crown.”**  
  Used to humble rulers, archmages, engineers, and conduit-states.

- **Context-Bound Relics:**  
  Arathi artifacts that are not broken or cursed but require vanished civic, biological, environmental, magical, or institutional contexts to function properly.

- **Deep Vault classification system:**
  - **Green Vaults:** inert/minimally active; supervised archaeology possible.  
  - **Blue Vaults:** archive or civic systems; translation and activation risk.  
  - **Gold Vaults:** intact infrastructure tied to conduits or life-supporting systems; quarantine recommended.  
  - **Red Vaults:** military, autonomous, biological, or reality-altering systems; evacuate and seal.  
  - **Black Vaults:** entropy contamination, boundary damage, alien resonance, or unknown hostile self-repair; no entry.

- **The Vault Concordat:**  
  A possible international agreement governing Deep Vault exploration, relic quarantine, unsealing rights, and containment obligations.

- **The Unsealing Oath:**  
  A ritual/legal oath requiring three witnesses before opening a sealed Arathi system:
  1. Witness for knowledge.  
  2. Witness for consequence.  
  3. Witness for the unborn.

- **The Fallback Doctrine:**  
  Anti-Arathi civilizational principle: no life-supporting system should exist without a mortal-scale fallback.

- **Witness Before Use / Understand Before Repair:**  
  Practical principles for Arathi archaeology and relic handling.

- **The Numbered Mourning:**  
  A death-prayer tradition for unnamed masses lost in the Cataclysm, involving counting breaths for the unknown dead until the priest can continue no longer.

- **Absence Cults:**  
  Early post-Cataclysm traditions that venerated places where maps failed and loss could not be repaired. Some became nihilistic; others preserved psychologically healthy mourning.

- **Festival of Broken Certainties:**  
  A civic ritual in which a city deliberately disables or interrupts one public system to practice resilience and humility.

- **Open Root Laws:**  
  Anti-totalization laws requiring cities to preserve ecological corridors, migration paths, water movement, and unbuilt spaces.

- **Hand-Tool Requirement:**  
  Guild law requiring essential systems to retain non-magical or low-magical backup methods.

- **Dead Switch Doctrine:**  
  Military/engineering rule requiring autonomous defenses or war-machines to have multiple mortal shutdown methods.

- **Entropy Taboo:**  
  A widespread magical prohibition against entropy abolition, permanent stasis, or decay-nullification research.

## 2. Open Questions

- Who sponsors or enforces the **Vault Concordat**? Orden? A neutral divine-backed order? A coalition of conduit states?
- Which modern powers refuse to classify vaults honestly?
- Are there known Black Vaults with names and legends?
- How many of the seven conduits have nearby Arathi vault-complexes?
- Do any modern crystal lineages descend directly from Arathi cultivated crystals, or are all modern versions crude recreations?
- Can Thamrite Mirrors contain emotionally dangerous sensory records of the Cataclysm?
- Are Absence Cults still active, or have they evolved into death-priest, anti-progress, or contemplative orders?
- What is the relationship between the Fallback Doctrine and Bold’s conduit-based infrastructure?
- Does the Entropy Taboo have divine enforcement, mortal enforcement, or both?

## 3. Possible Contradictions to Check

- The Arathi are extinct with no hidden survivors; any vault AI, crystal memory, or construct must not become “secret Arathi continuity” unless deliberately framed as incomplete context-bound remnants.
- Altea must remain gone. No true Altean ruins, royal vaults, buried capital, or preserved central archive.
- Deep Vaults can preserve Arathi material, but they should be peripheral, specialized, provincial, or fragmentary—not the answer to everything.
- The conduits predate the Arathi. Arathi mastery of them must not imply Arathi creation of them.
- Arathi artifacts may seem alive or responsive, but this should not automatically mean they possess souls in the same way mortals do.
- The gods should not know hidden author-level secrets not available to them.
