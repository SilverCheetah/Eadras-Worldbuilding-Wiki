Seraph are the common rank-and-file spirits of Celestia. They make up the vast majority of the staff of the Divine Bureaucracy.
Their daily work consists of the endless bureaucratic tasks required to keep the celestial administration running — processing paperwork, managing divine requests, filing forms, and maintaining the vast machinery of the Divine Bureaucracy. They are the clerks, functionaries, and middle management that keep the system operational.
In addition to their bureaucratic duties, Seraph also serve as the traditional "angels" of the setting. When the gods need their will enacted in the mortal world or elsewhere, it is usually a Seraph who is dispatched to carry it out. This is simply another part of their role within the bureaucracy.
While the Divine Bureaucracy is often slow, overly complicated, and bogged down in red tape, the Seraph themselves are dedicated and dutiful. They are not particularly malicious or benevolent — they are simply doing their jobs as part of a much larger celestial system.
Seraph are the celestial mirror to the Devils of Nyxar. Where Devils staff a terrifyingly efficient Infernal Bureaucracy, Seraph staff a sprawling, often cumbersome Divine Bureaucracy. Both are bureaucratic creatures first and foremost.
