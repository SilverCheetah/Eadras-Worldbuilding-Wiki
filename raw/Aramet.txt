**Aramet, Lesser Goddess of Hidden Things**

**Overview** Aramet is the subtle patron of concealed things: lost paths, buried secrets, enemy weak spots, forgotten runes. She guides, never fights—gods are barred from direct combat now, except during apocalyptic situations, like the Demon War. Call her, and she'll whisper where to look, what to avoid, how to slip through.

**Origin** Born Elfen. Eldritch pulsed through her from birth—like breathing. By her teens, she cast like a master—Eldritch bent to her will, spells came natural.

**Ascension** During the Demon War, scouting a secondary gate, Aramet faced a guardian she couldn't beat. In the clash, she ascended—demon obliterated, gate sealed.

**Symbol** Silver leaf with a compass etched in the veins—points to what's lost.

**Role at Table** When players call: "Aramet, please help me find it." She might answer softly: "It's there—behind the crag. Careful."
