Necromancy is treated as a **technical discipline** rather than a moral one:

*   It requires three things:

    *   A dead body (or suitable vessel)
    *   An animating spirit (which can be the original soul, a different intelligent spirit, or even a nature/elemental spirit)
    *   The correct spell or ritual

*   Low-level or non-sentient undead are common and often neutral (used for labor, guarding, etc.).

*   Sentient undead are rarer and more complex, but still not automatically evil.
