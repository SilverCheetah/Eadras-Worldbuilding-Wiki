POST-ARATHI CIVILIZATIONAL TRAUMA
=================================

NUMBERED MOURNING
-----------------
Ritual counting breaths for unnamed masses of dead until the officiant collapses.

ABSENCE CULTS
-------------
Traditions centered around irrecoverable loss and failed geography.

Core phrase:
"This does not return."

FALLBACK DOCTRINE
-----------------
No life-supporting system should exist without mortal-scale fallback.

HAND-TOOL REQUIREMENT
---------------------
Every essential process must retain:
- manual methods
- low-magical alternatives
- non-specialist redundancy

FESTIVAL OF BROKEN CERTAINTIES
------------------------------
Cities intentionally disable public systems periodically to preserve resilience and humility.

OPEN ROOT LAWS
--------------
Urban laws preserving:
- migration corridors
- water flow
- ecological passages
- unbuilt space

VAULT CONCORDAT
---------------
International framework for Deep Vault governance.

DEEP VAULT CLASSIFICATION
-------------------------
Green Vaults:
Low-risk archaeological sites.

Blue Vaults:
Archive or civic systems.

Gold Vaults:
Infrastructure-level systems.

Red Vaults:
Military or reality-altering systems.

Black Vaults:
Entropy contamination, hostile self-repair, alien resonance, or unknown catastrophic systems.

THE UNSEALING OATH
------------------
No sealed Arathi system may be opened without:
1. Witness for knowledge
2. Witness for consequence
3. Witness for the unborn
