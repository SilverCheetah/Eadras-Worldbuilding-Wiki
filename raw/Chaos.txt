Chaos
Where nothing makes sense

Imagine a place where nothing is stable. Not time, not dimensions, not definitions or forms. This barely begins to describe Chaos. One thing rapidly shifts into another, sanity is broken here, You are broken here. Left might become Tuesday, Right might become Yellow, and the very concept of You might become a cow that gives milk in the chord of G-sharp.

Then things shift again. And again. Shredding everything you know, or think you know. And destroying you, if you enter.

But from the outside (inside gaia) Chaos is hypnotic...
