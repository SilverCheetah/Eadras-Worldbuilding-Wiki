Devils do not have distinct biological varieties in the way mortals assume. Their base species is a relatively unremarkable medium-sized red-skinned humanoid, standing roughly five to five and a half feet tall. They have smooth crimson or deep red skin, small horn nubs, and relatively plain facial features. Their build is lean and athletic but not particularly imposing.
When completely out of uniform, devils look surprisingly ordinary. In the locker rooms of Dreadharrow, one can see groups of these red-skinned humanoids casually talking with one another, stripping off their departmental uniforms, throwing them into lockers, and heading to the showers while complaining about their day.
It is only when they don their official uniforms that they undergo significant physiological change. The uniform itself, issued by the Infernal Bureaucracy, magically reshapes their body — adding mass, spikes, wings, additional horns, armor, and other dramatic features appropriate to their rank and department. What mortals perceive as entirely different "types" of devils (imps, barbed devils, horned devils, pit fiends, etc.) are actually the same base species wearing different uniforms.
