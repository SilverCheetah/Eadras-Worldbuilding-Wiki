### Ioa – Head of the Elder Gods, Leader of the Pantheon

*   **Core Domain**: Oversees the entire pantheon, settles disputes, maintains balance among the gods.
*   **Personality**: Wise, calm, authoritative, and patient. He rarely raises his voice but commands instant respect. He has a dry, understated sense of humor.
*   **Relationships**: Acts as the neutral mediator. Closest to Tara and Raja (the parental pair). Keeps a careful eye on Nox and Sylph. Respects Lirra’s importance but worries about her independence.

### Lirra – Goddess of Magic, the Weave, Raw Magic, Sorcery, and Magic Items

*   **Core Domain**: All forms of magic and the structure that contains it (the Weave).
*   **Personality**: Wise, patient, deeply caring toward sorcerers, but stern when the Weave is threatened. Has a quiet, motherly intensity.
*   **Relationships**: Works closely with Vos (structure). Protective of her Spinners. Dislikes Nox for corrupting magic. Has a soft spot for Sylph’s chaos because it prevents stagnation.

### Nara – Goddess of Motherhood, Femininity, Women, Domesticity, Home and Hearth

*   **Core Domain**: Family, nurturing, home life, childbirth, and emotional warmth.
*   **Personality**: Warm, compassionate, protective, and fiercely loving. She can become terrifyingly stern when her children (literal or metaphorical) are threatened.
*   **Relationships**: Deeply bonded with Raja (her counterpart). Good friends with Lirra. Mildly disapproves of Sylph’s wildness but tolerates her. Often acts as a peacemaker alongside Ioa.

### Nox – God of Night, Hidden Wisdom, Secrets, Treachery, Darkness, Undead, and the Loathsome

*   **Core Domain**: Shadows, deception, forbidden knowledge, and corruption of death.
*   **Personality**: Smooth, sarcastic, cynical, and dangerously charming. He knows everyone’s secrets and enjoys playing both sides.
*   **Relationships**: Disliked by almost everyone except Sylph (who finds him entertaining). Strong rivalry with Shani. Grudging respect for Lirra.

### Qoxli – Goddess of the Wild, Animals, the Bow, Forests, Freedom, and the Chase

*   **Core Domain**: Untamed nature, instinct, hunting, and personal freedom.
*   **Personality**: Fierce, exuberant, restless, and bluntly honest. She lives for the thrill and has little patience for rules.
*   **Relationships**: Best friends with Sylph. Natural rival of Vos. Wary but respectful toward Raja. Strongly dislikes Nox.

### Raja – God of Masculinity, Men, Craftsmanship, Hunting, Victory, and Fatherhood

*   **Core Domain**: Strength, skill, provision, competition, and fatherly duty.
*   **Personality**: Honorable, straightforward, proud, and competitive. He values hard work and excellence but can be stubborn.
*   **Relationships**: Perfect counterpart to Nara. Strong ally of Daj and Vos. Has a respectful rivalry with Qoxli (hunt vs. wild freedom). Dislikes treachery (Nox).

### Sylph – Goddess of Foolishness, Indulgence, Instinct, Self-Interest, and Independence

*   **Core Domain**: Whim, pleasure, chaos, and unbridled freedom. Patron of all Fae.
*   **Personality**: Playful, mischievous, selfish, and surprisingly clever. She lives for the moment and loves causing trouble.
*   **Relationships**: Best friends with Qoxli. Loves teasing Nox. Annoys Vos and Tara. Has a flirtatious rivalry with Daj.

### Vos – God of Cities, Wisdom, Order, Law, Justice, Wealth, Commerce, and Technology

*   **Core Domain**: Civilization, structure, progress, and lawful prosperity.
*   **Personality**: Methodical, intellectual, precise, and somewhat aloof. He has a dry wit and little tolerance for foolishness.
*   **Relationships**: Close allies with Raja and Tara. Frequent clashes with Qoxli and Sylph. Respects Lirra but finds her magic chaotic.

### Shali & Shani – The Twin Goddesses of Death

**Core Domain**: Death and the judgment of souls. Shali gently escorts worthy souls to peace and rest. Shani drags the wicked to eternal torment.

**Personality & Relationship**: They are two halves of the same original soul, split at creation because each half interpreted the divine decree regarding death differently. Shali is compassionate, patient, and merciful. Shani is cold, ruthless, and unforgiving. They **argue and compete about almost anything**, constantly bickering over whose approach to death is superior. Their rivalry creates real tension in the pantheon. Their tension with Nox stems from his liberal use of undeath and necromancy. Shani sees it as meddling with what should be final. Shali views it as distasteful and a mockery of death. They do not view it as inherently evil, because it is not, just a violation of their domain.

**Flavor & Mortal Perception**: Mortals know there are two goddesses of death. Shali is respectfully called **Mistress Death** or **Goddess Death**. Shani is so feared that most people refuse to speak her name aloud, simply calling her **Death Itself**.

Because her name is uttered so rarely, every time someone does speak Shani’s name, she pays very close attention and quietly appreciates it.

**Ioa’s Role**: Ioa frequently tries to keep the twins apart and occupied to prevent their constant arguments from disrupting the pantheon. He will sometimes assign them deliberately menial or absurd tasks, such as personally reaping every fly that dies in the southern wetlands on a given day, just to keep them busy and separated.
