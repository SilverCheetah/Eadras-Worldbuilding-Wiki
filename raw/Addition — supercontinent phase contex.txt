Addition — supercontinent phase context:

At the time of the unicorn Sourceflare event, the planet existed in a supercontinent configuration characterized by large-scale environmental stability and interconnected ecosystems.

This unified landmass allowed ecological and magical systems to operate across continuous regions, contributing to the emergence of unicorns as a species aligned with balance and systemic continuity.

The later fragmentation of the supercontinent reduced the scale at which such balance could be maintained.
