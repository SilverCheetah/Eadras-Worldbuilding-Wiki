Elder Gods

Ioa, Tara, Raja, Vos, Qoxli, Daj, Shali, Shani, Nox, Sylph, and Lirra.

Ioa leads the greater gods in their duties and acts as the head of the pantheon. God of Sovereign Order.

Nara is the goddess of femininity, women, domesticity, Hearth, Home, and motherhood.

Raja is the god of masculinity, men, craftsmanship, hunting, Víctory, and fatherhood.

Vos is the god of cities, wisdom, law, order, justice, wealth, commerce, and technology.

Qoxli is the goddess of the Wild, animals, the bow, forests, freedom, the Chase.

Daj is the god of war, strife, conflict, disagreements, armies, fortresses, and weapons.

Sylph is the goddess of foolishness, indulgence, instinct, self interest, and independence. She is the patron goddess of all Fae creatures.

Shali and Shani are the twin goddesses of death. Shali brings souls to the good place and Shani takes souls to eternal torment.

Nox is the God of Night, hidden wisdom, secrets, treachery, darkness, undead, and the loathsome.

Lirra is the goddess of Magic, the Weave, raw magic, sorcery, and magic items.

Elderborn

1\. **Erendor** — God of Time, cycles, prophecy, aging. Jolly, round guy with silver curls and pajamas. Hops timelines, borrows his own body, gets side-eyed by Timekeeper on big moves. Keeps the clock ticking without cheats.

2\. **Handwright** — God of Craftwork. Gruff master of all hand-made trades—smithing, weaving, carving, barrels, you name it. No specialty, just raw know-how. Growls at sloppy work, warms up if you nail it.

3\. **Agron** — God of Agriculture. Tall, root-handed, smells like wet soil. Nudges crops to grow, walks fields at night. No temples—just hope he notices your planting.

4\. **Elyria** — Goddess of Love. Passion, bonds, heartbreak, romance, unions. Rose-wreathed, golden eyes, sad-hot vibe. Handles weddings, feuds, family ties. Prayers for romance or revenge.

5\. **Vaelor** — Sky Lord. Winds, storms, eagle omens. Moody cloud-wings, lightning veins. Drops thunder on liars, priests read bird flights.

6\. **Ignara** — Fire Mother. Volcanoes, wildfires, raw flame. Conjures anything from fire—beasts, dancers, weapons on demand. Fiery temper, unpredictable attention.

7\. **Thalira** — Sea Sovereign. Tides, fish, shipwrecks. Flowing robes, coral crown. Calms or drowns, no mercy for fools.

8\. **Morveth** — Underworld Warden. Judges souls, rules the depths. Bone throne, lantern of regrets. No guiding—that's the twin death goddesses. Fair, cold, shadowy.

9\. **Aureon** — Martial God of Goodness & Purity. Silver-white armor, dawn-sword. Purges corruption, no rage—just clean strikes.

**The Game of the Gods:** It was created by Dao in secret, one day it just appeared in Solarion. All important mortals are pieces on a giant board in the divine realm. The gods play it like a game of D\&D—taking turns moving tokens. It’s addictive, so most gods stay in the divine realm playing it.
