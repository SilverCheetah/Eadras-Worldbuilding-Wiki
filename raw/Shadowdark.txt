Shadowdark - dark reflection of prime material plane, influenced by negative energy from below. Home of many spirits tainted by darkness, including restless dead. Home of what would be called the Underdark in other Mythoi, the Shadowdark is larger than the Prime Material and the farther one travels from the Prime, the stranger things become. Has a system of Portals (called Dark Paths) to quickly traverse the nearly infinite plane, but they are corrupt and dangerous.
