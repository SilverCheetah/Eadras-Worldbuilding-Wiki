DOCTRINE OF THE OPEN PALM
=========================

Core Principle:
The Open Palm is a reformist reinterpretation of the Doctrine of the Unheld Hand.

It argues:
- The gods must not dominate mortal destiny.
- Mortal agency remains sacred.
- Divine restraint must not become abandonment.
- Systems exist to preserve flourishing, not procedural purity.

Key phrase:
"Do not fill the space we opened."

THREE BELLS OF INTERVENTION
---------------------------
1. Mortals recognize the crisis and act.
2. Mortal institutions fail despite good-faith effort.
3. The crisis threatens:
   - Weave stability
   - soul-cycle continuity
   - species survival
   - reality integrity
   - irreversible civilizational collapse

At the Third Bell:
divine intervention becomes permissible.

HEARTH-WITNESS MANDATE
----------------------
Major divine offices maintain mortal or recently mortal auditors capable of evaluating divine administration through lived mortal consequence.

PETITIONS OF ABANDONED CIRCUMSTANCE
-----------------------------------
Afterlife petitions arguing that divine negligence contributed materially to suffering or death.

MORTAL THEOLOGICAL SCHOOLS
--------------------------
- Orthodox Unheld
- Empty Palm Lament
- Stewardship Compact
- Wild Refusal
- Bold Revision

KEY CONCLUSION
--------------
Mercy without discipline becomes indulgence.
Discipline without mercy becomes Hell.
