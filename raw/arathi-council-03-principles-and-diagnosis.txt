# HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

This document extracts reusable setting principles from the Arath and geo-substrate council material. These principles should guide future stories, wiki updates, political design, magical archaeology, and civilization-scale risk.

---

# PRINCIPLE 1: THE OLD WORLD'S MEDICINE MAY POISON THE LIVING WORLD

[DIAGNOSIS] Arathi systems were designed for pre-tectonic Eadras. Modern Eadras is a different world-condition.

A system that was stabilizing in the old world may be catastrophic in the new one. This principle applies beyond geology: old political, magical, biological, and institutional solutions may be dangerous if restored without their vanished context.

Key maxim:

"Do not dose the living world with medicine for the dead."

---

# PRINCIPLE 2: LIVING INFRASTRUCTURE CREATES ETHICAL DEBT

[DIAGNOSIS] A civilization that builds with living or semi-living infrastructure must answer questions ordinary engineering avoids.

Does the infrastructure suffer?
Can it consent?
Can it be retired?
Can it be healed?
Can it be inherited?
Who speaks for it after the civilization that cultivated it dies?

Arathi geo-substrate makes these questions unavoidable.

---

# PRINCIPLE 3: CONTEXT IS PART OF FUNCTION

[DIAGNOSIS] Some technologies do not merely require components. They require a whole civilization to make sense.

This applies to Arathi relics, divine bureaucracy, unicorn memory, dragon knowledge, and magical orders. Removing a thing from its context can make a functioning system behave like a broken or dangerous one.

---

# PRINCIPLE 4: IRREVERSIBLE DEPENDENCY IS ARATHI DANGER

[DIAGNOSIS] The point at which a civilization becomes Arathi-like is not when it uses powerful tools. It is when ordinary survival depends on systems no living authority fully understands and no mortal-scale fallback can replace.

This principle should guide modernity, magitek, urban planning, military systems, food production, and conduit policy.

---

# PRINCIPLE 5: ABSENCE CAN BE MORE HONEST THAN FALSE MEMORY

[DIAGNOSIS] Because no immediate post-Cataclysm intelligent mourners existed, later cultures must not pretend to remember Arath directly.

Formal Absence Theology is powerful because it honors the impossibility of memory. It refuses the comfort of invented continuity.

Key prayer:

"We do not remember, and this too is true."

---

# PRINCIPLE 6: DISCOVERY DOES NOT CREATE OWNERSHIP

[DIAGNOSIS] Deep Vault discovery is not entitlement. Finding a system does not grant the moral right to activate, possess, dissect, sell, or weaponize it.

This principle applies to ruins, living substrate, dragon-hoarded knowledge, unicorn warnings, divine secrets, and inherited conduits.

---

# PRINCIPLE 7: SUCCESS CAN MAKE CORRECTION FEEL PRESUMPTUOUS

[DIAGNOSIS] Arath lasted so long and worked so well that even gods hesitated to correct it. This is a major civilizational danger.

Institutions that endure for long enough begin to look legitimate simply because they have endured.
