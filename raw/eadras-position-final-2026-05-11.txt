Eadras's position in the multiverse — final canon, 2026-05-11

(Session-capture: Josh untangled two properties I had been conflating
across multiple retcons today, and clarified what's unique about
Eadras vs other Mythoi.)

== Two distinct properties ==

INTRINSIC MAGICAL RICHNESS is a property of the MYTHOS ITSELF -- its
substance, its laws, its cosmological architecture, what's woven into
the fabric. Eadras IS intrinsically magically rich. This is a
Mythos-level property, not a function of Well contents at any given
moment.

WELL SIZE is the reservoir of Creative Power that gods tap. It
regulates TOTAL POWER AVAILABLE to the gods of a Mythos -- how much
they can draw.

These two properties are INDEPENDENT. They can vary separately. I had
been conflating them across the (fourteenth), (sixteenth), and
(seventeenth) log entries; that was the persistent error.

Specifically:
- Eadras: intrinsically magically rich. Small current Well. Always full
  (Bastion feed).
- Other Mythoi: varying intrinsic richness; larger Wells; not refilled
  by Bastion (not adjacent to Concordance).

== Well size matters when not refilled ==

The critical mechanic:

> "Size of well would regulate total power available to Gods, but not
> when it is always full!"

For Mythoi without continuous feed: Well size = total godpower budget.
A larger Well = more power available; a smaller Well = less. Wells
drain over time as gods tap them.

For Eadras: the small Well is always full (Bastion feed). The size-as-
cap doesn't apply. Gods can keep drawing forever. The "budget" framing
doesn't exist for Eadras's pantheon -- they have effectively unlimited
access.

== The Primordial sacrifice -- unique to Eadras ==

EADRAS IS UNIQUE in the Omniverse for ONE specific event:

The Primordial Duo (Evye and Fryr) SACRIFICED themselves into the
Mythos. They dissolved their essence into the Well of Creation and the
substance of the world, paying their own existence as the price of
creating Eadras.

OTHER MYTHOI in the Omniverse: their primordial creators
- did NOT sacrifice
- are STILL PRESENT in their Mythos
- still RULE as the top tier of divinity
- have NOT consumed their Wells in the same way Eadras was consumed

Consequences for Eadras:
- Top-tier rulership is one tier lower than it would have been
  elsewhere -- the Elder Gods of Eadras are the highest active
  divinity, with the Primordials gone
- The Well is small (because the sacrifice + creation consumed most
  of the substantial original allotment plus the Primordial essence)
- Bastion feed compensates for the depleted Well, since Eadras happens
  to be in the privileged location

Consequences for other Mythoi:
- Primordial-tier divinity at the top (their primordials still rule)
- Larger Wells (not consumed by sacrifice-and-creation in the same
  way)
- No Bastion feed (not adjacent to Concordance)
- Gods operate within a finite power budget

== The Eadras advantage, summed up ==

Eadras's pantheon is exceptionally powerful because:
1. The Mythos is intrinsically magically rich (saturated substance)
2. The Well is small but ALWAYS FULL (no bottleneck on godpower)
3. The Primordials are gone (no tier above the active pantheon)
4. The Bastion of Light continuously feeds the Well (compensates for
   the consumed allotment and Primordial essence)

This combination is unique. Other Mythoi have at most some of these
features. None have all four.
