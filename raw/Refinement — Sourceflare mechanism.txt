Refinement — Sourceflare mechanism:

Sourceflares are natural stellar events that produce bursts of high-intensity radiation across the entire spectrum from above radio to visible light to x-rays including magical radiation within the Eldritch spectrum.

Under normal conditions, these events do not significantly change life. However, under specific circumstances, they can result in discrete, finite manifestations of new species.

Exceptionally high intensity flares (X-class flares), can have influence over the planetary ecology, magnetic field, etc.

The form of these manifestations is determined by environmental conditions at the time of the event, including planetary stability, ecological maturity, and existing systemic structure.
