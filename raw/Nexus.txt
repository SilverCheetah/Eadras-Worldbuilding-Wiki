Nexus is the megacity at the center of the Omniverse. For 1000 km outward in any direction from the Bastion of Light, you will find Nexus, with its many distinct districts populated by every culture in the omniverse somewhere. And governed by the One Law.

Do No Violence in Nexus.

The Archons are the enforcers of the One Law, and they don't ask questions, they just use their maces or spells.

Since Nexus is so large, the most common means of long distance travel is by air, either a flying mount or an enchanted object. So directions tend to be oriented looking down. "Go four districts down, pass the Silver fox statue, the massive abomination, the Beastman and come to the Elf Girl, then turn spinward and count three streets, turn coreward and go one street, turn anti-spinward and count three houses on the anti-coreward side and you are there."
