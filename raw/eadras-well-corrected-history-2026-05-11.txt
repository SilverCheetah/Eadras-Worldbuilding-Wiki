FINAL CANON (this entry):
- Eadras IS intrinsically rich. The OC put a substantial allotment of
  Creative Power into it at making.
- The Primordial Duo, when they sacrificed themselves into the Mythos,
  added their essence to the Well, deepening it further.

  reservoir.

  continuously feeds it through Concordance-proximity.

== Decomposed canon ==

Question 1: Did the Primordial Duo's sacrifice add to Eadras's Well?
Answer: YES. The Duo's essence went into the Well, deepening it.

Question 2: Was most of that essence consumed in creation?
Answer: YES. Most of the Duo's essence was used to create everything
in Eadras.

Question 3: Was most of the Well consumed in creation?
Answer: YES. "Most of essence and most of well" -- both depleted.

Question 4: Is Eadras intrinsically rich?
Answer: YES. The Mythos was made with a substantial allotment from
the start; it was not made poor.

Question 5: But the present-day Well is small?
Answer: YES. After expenditure in creation, what remains is small.

Question 6: Why does the pantheon still operate at exceptional power?
Answer: Bastion of Light continuously feeds the small remainder. The
flow rate compensates for the depleted depth.

== The history of Eadras's Well, in order ==

T0  -- OC creates Eadras's Mythos with a substantial Creative Power
       allotment in the Well of Creation. Eadras is intrinsically rich.
T1  -- Primordial Duo (Evye and Fryr) sacrifice themselves; their
       essence is added to the Well. Deepest the Well ever is.
T2  -- Creation of everything: planes, laws, substance, life conditions.
       Most of the Primordial Duo's essence and most of the Well's
       contents are expended.
T3  -- Mythos is now made. Small remainder Well remains.
T4  -- Bastion of Light, through Eadras's proximity to the Concordance,
       continuously feeds the Well from T3 onward. The small Well never
       empties.
T-now -- Pantheon taps the small-but-replenishing Well via direct link.

== Implications ==

- The Eadras pantheon is mighty NOT by what the Mythos still has, but
  by what it CONTINUES to receive. Both depletion AND replenishment
  are part of the story.
- Eadras's intrinsic richness explains why so much creation was possible
  in the first place -- nine planes, multiple eras of civilizations,
  the engineered races, the cosmological architecture, etc. Other
  Mythoi with lower allotments could not produce a creation of this
  scale.
- The Primordial Duo's sacrifice is part of WHY Eadras is rich. Their
  essence was a top-up to the OC's already-substantial allotment.
- The current small Well is therefore not a sign of cosmic stinginess;
  it is the sign of an enormous creative project completed with most
  of the available essence.
