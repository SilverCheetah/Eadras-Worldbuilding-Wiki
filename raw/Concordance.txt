Concordance is the vast outer plane at the center of the omniverse. The massive city of Nexus sits at the center of Concordance. The Bastion of Light is at the center of Nexus.

Concordance contains every environment imaginable, and many that aren't. Sections of the plane shift on an unpredictable schedule, but if people live there the shift is always to a place with similar resources (say farming to fishing).

There are millions of beings that live on Concordance, providing most of what the residents of Nexus need. Still, unimaginable volumes of trade pour into and out of Nexus hourly through the incredible number of portals in Concordance.

All the other Outer Planes encircle Concordance. The presence of the Creator (or at least his Bastion of Light) keeps the Chaos back far enough that there is no need for an enclosing shell, and while the planes are spiritual they also contain enough physical reality to allow physical bodies to exist comfortably.
