# Worldbuilding Session Ingest — Eadras Cosmology, Cultures, and Metaphysics

## Meta-worldbuilding philosophy

The setting is intentionally built as a synthesis of influences from many fantasy traditions, recombined into a coherent mythic framework rather than a patchwork homage setting.

Core design goals:

* No objective good/evil axis.
* Races are psychologically and philosophically distinct rather than morally coded.
* Cosmology is structured around coherence vs dissolution rather than morality.
* History matters biologically, culturally, and metaphysically.
* Civilization leaves psychological and ontological consequences.
* Mythic ambiguity is intentionally preserved.

The setting should feel:

* ancient
* layered
* partially understood
* historically reconstructed
* emotionally real
* cosmologically coherent

# Core Metaphysical Themes

## Coherent existence

A major cosmological principle emerging across the setting:

Existence requires:

* structure
* truth
* identity
* continuity
* memory
* lawful coherence

Chaos represents:

* unreality
* absence of coherent structure
* dissolution of stable existence

The Omniversal Creator represents:

* maximal coherent existence
* absolute ontological stability
* perfect self-consistency

The phrase:

"I AM"

is understood as:

* ontological certainty
* perfect coherent selfhood
* reality fully stabilized

Everything else in existence is referential approximation.

# True Language vs Referential Language

Only the Omniversal Creator possesses:

* True Language
* perfect identity-expression equivalence
* direct reality speech

Everything else uses:

* derivative language
* symbolic approximation
* referential structures

## Generational degradation model

### Omniversal Creator

* True Language
* direct reality expression

### Primordial Creator Deities

* First derivative language
* near-perfect reality approximation
* capable of creating Mythoi
* imperfect expression leads to flawed worlds

### Elder Gods

* Second derivative language
* partial conceptual inheritance
* incapable of fully understanding creation architecture

### Mortals

* heavily degraded symbolic systems
* magic through approximation and structured resonance

This explains:

* imperfect Mythoi
* linguistic drift
* magical difficulty
* limits of truenaming
* the necessity of precision
