Godpower and the Well of Creation, 2026-05-11

(Session-capture: Josh established the universal omniversal mechanism
by which all gods, in all Mythoi, derive their power. The mechanism
underlies and explains the "Eadras pantheon is positionally powerful"
canon committed earlier today.)

== Canon ==

GODPOWER (in all Mythoi) taps the leftover primordial power in the
WELL OF CREATION that exists in every Mythos as the CREATIVE POWER
the Omniversal Creator put into every Mythos.

Decomposed:

1. The Omniversal Creator (OC) puts CREATIVE POWER into every Mythos
   at its making. This is universal -- every Mythos receives an
   allotment.

2. Most of that Creative Power is spent creating the Mythos itself
   (planes, laws, substance, conditions for life). What remains is
   LEFTOVER -- it sits in the Mythos after creation is finished.

3. The leftover Creative Power lives in a WELL OF CREATION -- a
   reservoir of the OC's creative substance, present in every Mythos.

4. GODPOWER -- the power gods exercise -- is a TAP on the local Well.
   Every god in the Omniverse, regardless of Mythos, draws on their
   local Well in the same fundamental way.

5. Variation between Mythoi (magic-rich vs thin, mighty pantheons
   vs marginal) is variation in HOW MUCH leftover Creative Power
   each Well holds.

== Universal architecture, not Eadras-specific ==

This applies across the entire Omniverse. Every god, every Mythos,
every pantheon -- same underlying mechanic. Eadras's pantheon being
exceptionally powerful (canon committed earlier today) is now
mechanically explained: Eadras has one of the largest Wells in the
Omniverse, so its leftover Creative Power is correspondingly vast,
so its gods are correspondingly mighty.

== Implications ==

- Gods are NOT the source of their own power. They are channels --
  taps -- on the OC's prior creative act, mediated through the local
  Well. Whatever a god creates, builds, threatens, or grants is
  ultimately a borrowing.
- Pantheons that grasp this fact are likely to be quieter / humbler
  than pantheons that do not. (Conjecture by author; not committed
  to canon for any specific pantheon.)
- The Eadras pantheon's understanding of this is mixed and varies by
  individual god. They do not spend much time on the question while
  the Game is available -- which is part of the Game's containment
  function.

== Open canon ==

- Is the Well of Creation REFILLABLE, or FINITE? Does godly tapping
  drain it over time, or does it replenish?
- Does MORTAL MAGIC also tap the Well (in some attenuated form),
  or does mortal magic come from elsewhere? Mortal scholars who
  have studied metaphysics conjecture a connection, but the
  full relationship is unestablished.
- The Eadras-specific magical mechanisms (the Weave, Pillar Magic,
  Netharim, Lexanari, true language, etc.) -- which of these are
  Well-derived, and which are something else? See magic-in-eadras.md
  for the magic-systems canon.
- The Primordial Duo's sacrifice ("dissolved themselves into the
  world itself") -- did their essence ADD to Eadras's Well? Is
  Eadras's exceptional richness partly OC-baseline and partly
  Primordial-Duo-contribution? Or is the OC's baseline alone what
  makes Eadras rich?
- Whether any Mythos has ever DRAINED its Well, and if so what
  happens to it.
- Whether Dao's macro-balance role (Role 2) includes ensuring no
  Mythos's Well becomes catastrophically imbalanced relative to
  its neighbours.
