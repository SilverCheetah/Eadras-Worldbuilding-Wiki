# HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

This document extracts setting-wide principles from the Demon War material. These principles apply to war memory, anti-magic politics, death theology, magical geography, and institutional trauma.

---

# PRINCIPLE 1: FEAR CAN CREATE THE THING IT CLAIMS TO PREVENT

[DIAGNOSIS] The Anti-Magic Crusade caused the Thousand-Year Dimming by attacking magical practitioners and Windspeakers.

This is a general Eadras principle: panic can destroy the stabilizers that keep panic's object from becoming real.

---

# PRINCIPLE 2: ANNIHILATION IS DIFFERENT FROM DEATH

[DIAGNOSIS] Death is part of the soul-cycle. Demonic consumption steals beings from the known cycle.

This distinction matters for theology, grief, war memorials, and anti-demonic ethics. Not all losses are the same kind of loss.

---

# PRINCIPLE 3: MAGIC IS NOT INNOCENT, BUT NEITHER IS FEAR

[DIAGNOSIS] Magic is dangerous because reality is alive. Magic is sacred because reality answers. Fear becomes evil when it destroys the hands that know how to heal the wound.

This principle should govern post-Crusade magical law.

---

# PRINCIPLE 4: DIFFERENT ORDERS SUFFER DIFFERENTLY

[DIAGNOSIS] The Anti-Magic Crusade did not affect all magical traditions equally.

Visible urban wizards burned. Windspeakers were strategically targeted. Priests and Clerics split into victims and perpetrators. Netharim became terrifying symbols. Lexanari became rumor-myths. Druids survived through wild isolation and Fey protection.

A believable catastrophe has uneven geography.

---

# PRINCIPLE 5: TRUTH MUST SOMETIMES BE TIERED, BUT TIERING CREATES ROT

[DIAGNOSIS] Xar'vion disclosure cannot be total without risk, but secrecy created the Black Hand's interpretive monopoly.

This tension applies broadly: some truths are too dangerous to broadcast, but every burial creates a habitat for those who dig.

---

# PRINCIPLE 6: HUMOR CAN BE INFRASTRUCTURE

[DIAGNOSIS] Beast-Folk laughter-houses can function as defensive infrastructure because the enemy feeds on despair.

This principle legitimizes non-martial cultural defenses: songs, festivals, jokes, mourning forms, cookfires, masks, absurdity, and communal rituals can be strategic tools.

---

# PRINCIPLE 7: BROKEN CIRCULATION CREATES UNEVEN GEOGRAPHY

[DIAGNOSIS] The Dimming was broken Eldritch circulation, not uniform darkness. Catastrophes in Eadras often create patchwork consequences: barrens beside overbright zones, silence beside screaming abundance.

This principle can guide map design, settlement patterns, magical economies, and pilgrimage.
