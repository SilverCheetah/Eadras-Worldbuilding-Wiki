The Order of Ben Liroth is the ancient and most prestigious order of sorcerers who swear direct service to the goddess Lira. Based in the Towers of Lira, they serve as both the foremost academy of magic and the primary enforcers of Lira’s will in the mortal world.
Their duties include training new sorcerers, conducting magical research, seeking out and testing potential initiates, lending their power to the border guard during times of crisis, and various other tasks.
Most trained sorcerers in the world either belong to the Order or received their training from them.Graduates of their rigorous program are offered the chance to swear the Oath of Service and formally join the Order, binding themselves to Lira’s service for life.

Demons

Demons are an invasive alien race that comes from a completely different Mythos — a reality so alien to our own that their very existence is poisonous to this Mythos.
They are energivores. They do not eat food; they consume raw energy — mana, life force, raw matter, anything that contains energy. The more they consume, the larger, stronger, and more intelligent they become. A young demon might appear as an insubstantial shadow, but an ancient one that has fed for centuries can grow to colossal size, sprouting dozens of limbs, thick armored plates, razor-sharp bone weapons, or even completely new organs designed for killing.
Their bodies are incredibly mutable. They can reshape their flesh at will, inventing new biological weapons or defensive structures on the spot. They are also capable of spontaneously creating new spells, often twisting the laws of magic in ways that native mages cannot replicate or even comprehend.
While they can be killed or banished in battle, they are extraordinarily difficult to truly destroy. Banishing them only sends them back to their home Mythos, from which they can eventually return.
Many scholars consider demons to be an existential threat to the entire omniverse. The greatest mystery in creation is why the Omniversal creator allows these beings to exist at all.

Demons
Origin
Demons come from an entirely different Mythos. Their Prime Material Plane is hellish and Jupiter-like — a realm of never-ending electrical storms, crushing atmospheric pressure, toxic clouds, and violent chaos. The conditions there are so extreme that no being from our reality could survive for even a single moment.

Physiology & Abilities
Demons are energivores. They consume raw energy — mana, life force, raw matter, or any form of power — instead of food. The more energy they absorb, the larger, stronger, and more intelligent they grow. Their bodies are extremely mutable, allowing them to rapidly grow extra limbs, natural weapons, armor plating, or any other biological structures they need. They can also spontaneously invent new spells on the spot, twisting magic in ways that native mages cannot replicate or understand.

Threat Level
Demons are an existential threat to the omniverse. While they can be temporarily banished or killed, banishing them only sends them back to their nightmarish Prime Material Plane, from which they can eventually return.

The Great Mystery
No one truly knows why the Omniversal creator permits these beings to exist, but some scholars speculate they are used to encourage growth and advancement.
