"The Three" refer to Mark , Rand , and Melania . They created the Elfen , Velrathi , and Taran races.

The Three are the adopted children of Dao, living with him while he forged the Runeswords in the second age. When they reached maturity, the three wanted to create on their own, and each chose to create kindred, based on their own humanoid frame.

Note to Self: The Three That Are One withdrew from the world sometime between 20,000 and 19,000 years ago. Their withdrawal gave the Tarran several centuries to grow arrogant and power-hungry without any direct divine oversight or correction. This directly enabled them to declare the Firethorn Empire at 18,000 years ago.
