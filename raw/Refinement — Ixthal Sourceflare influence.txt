Refinement — Ixthal Sourceflare influence:

Ixthal did not create life directly, but instead amplified and subtly shaped natural Sourceflare events to produce specific outcomes.

These actions were performed in a manner that remained indistinguishable from natural phenomena, preventing detection by divine entities or systemic oversight. The resulting species, including dragons and unicorns, are therefore believed to have emerged naturally.

This intervention represents a violation of cosmic constraints carried out through concealment rather than direct defiance.
