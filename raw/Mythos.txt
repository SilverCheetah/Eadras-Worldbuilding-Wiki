A Mythos is a closed shell or bubble connecting an interconnected series of planes within the Omniverse, including a Prime Material Plane, some sort of lower plane, some sort of connective plane, and some sort of upper plane.

Each mythos began with a creator deity or deities, created by the Omniversal Creator, and what that creator deity did was up to them.

In most mythos, the creator deity used the power to create prime material planes, lower planes, upper planes (from which they have an avatar rule), and a transitional (or connective) plane. Some even create elemental planes, and even outer planes. But they are the only deity.

In other mythos, the creator deity creates lesser deities to share the load, and together they create a set of planes. With multiple minds, outcomes vary considerably.\
In one particular Mythos, the Creator Deity created a hellish prime material plane, and dark and deep lower planes. The transitional plane was corrupted and the upper plane was a beacon of hope. This is the Mythos Demons come from, and they want to conquer all other Mythos.
