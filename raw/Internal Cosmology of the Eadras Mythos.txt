Internal Cosmology of the Eadras Mythos

Eadras is composed of its Prime Material Plane and seven additional internal planes. Celestia, Nyxar, Shadowdark, Feywild, Limina, Fire, Water, Earth, and Air.

The Prime Material Plane (Eadras physical reality) sits in the middle. Occupying the same space but dimensionally shifted are the Feywilds (a bright reflection) and the Shadowdark ( a dark reflection). Dimensionally below is Nyxar, and Dimensionally above is Celestia. The Elemental Planes form a continuous ring around the Prime Material and are constantly rotating, in tune with the seasons. Limina is the transitory plane within the Eadras Mythos, connecting all the planes together and the medium in which the planes exist, being the "air" that fills the mythos bubble.
