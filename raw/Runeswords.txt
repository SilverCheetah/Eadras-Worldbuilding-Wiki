### Runeswords

Dao created nine Runeswords outside of any Mythos to keep balance in the Omniverse. They are sentient blades with the soul of an ancient warrior race from a Mythos that no longer exists willingly bound inside forever.

There are three Runeswords of Neutrality, three of Good, and Three of Evil, Plus two created after by Melania for her children the Taran.

The Runeswords are all bound by the Runes of Power and the Omniversal Laws, laws created by the Omniversal Creator to govern all Mythos in the Omniverse.

Sword Laws

1.  A sword cannot lie to its wielder, but there are things that are forbidden to reveal.
2.  A sword cannot cause its wielder to lose their life through action or inaction, nor cause the wielder harm.
3.  A sword may not reveal the location of another sword unless it is diametrically opposed.
4.  Swords cannot act on their own except in moments of extreme danger to their wielder.

All Runeswords (except Deathbringer) are only conscious when being actively wielded. When returned to their stone, they sleep, and their memories of their previous master are purged, except the swords of neutrality.

Starmetal — The Material of the Runeswords

All eleven Runeswords are forged from **Starmetal**, a material Dao personally extracted from the remains of the Worldbreaker falling star. Only he, as an avatar of the Ultimate Creator, could harvest it, render it stable enough to work with, and make it compatible with the mythos.

Starmetal is **foreign** to Eadras/Andros. It is vastly harder and more resistant than any native metal or material. It is highly resistant to magic, extremely difficult to damage, and requires god-level heat (Dao intensified volcanic fire tenfold) to forge.

There are three distinct types of Starmetal, each tied to moral alignment (even though good and evil are not cosmic absolutes in the mythos, they are intrinsic to sentient morality):

*   **White Starmetal** — Used for the **Swords of Good**. It gleams with a pure, clean white shine (not a glow, but a clear, bright luster).
*   **Silver Starmetal** — Used for the **Swords of Neutrality**. It is highly reflective, almost mirror-like, with a chrome or polished silver appearance.
*   **Black Starmetal** — Used for the **Swords of Evil**. It is deep, absolute black that seems to swallow light rather than reflect it.

##### The Runeswords

Deathbringer, the Hand of Death, the Sword of Doom

The first runesword is Deathbringer. Deathbringer is above the natural laws of the universe, space and time do not have dominion over Deathbringer and its wielder. It would only be bound by one law, a law that it would be charged to uphold: 'Death comes for us all in the end.'

**Taran Runeswords** (Forged by Melvina for the Fireheart Empire)

*   **Brightthorn** – Sword of the First Princess of Fire (White Starmetal) Can sunder any ward or magical barrier and strike straight and true on command. Grants increased combat effectiveness and eldritch augmentation. **Restriction**: Can only be wielded by the **First Princess of Fire** (firstborn daughter of the legitimate monarch of the Firethorn Empire).
*   **Darkthorn** – Sword of the First Prince of Fire (Black Starmetal) Soul Drinker — Absorbs souls and converts them into raw eldritch energy for the wielder. Only Shali and Shani know the ultimate fate of these souls. **Restriction**: Can only be wielded by the **First Prince of Fire** (firstborn son of the legitimate monarch of the Firethorn Empire).
