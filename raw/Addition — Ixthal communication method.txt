Addition — Ixthal communication method:

Ixthal communicates through direct conceptual transmission rather than spoken language.

This method conveys meaning, intent, and emotional context without the use of words, allowing recipients to understand him without requiring verbal explanation.

The process is limited in its ability to convey complex sequences or detailed causal relationships, and is therefore used primarily for guidance rather than instruction. It also serves to maintain his silence by avoiding explicit statements that could reveal forbidden knowledge.
