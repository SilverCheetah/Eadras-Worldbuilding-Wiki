== Canon: sacrifice method ==

Most Primordial creators across the Omniverse use ONLY the energy in
their Mythos's Well of Creation. They draw on the Creative Power the
OC put into the Mythos at its making, and leave their own essence
intact. Their Wells deplete a bit (or sometimes a lot), but they
themselves remain to rule.

Evye and Fryr were unusual: they DID BOTH.
- They drew on the Well of Creation extensively (depleting it).
- They poured out their own essence on top of that (sacrificing
  themselves).

Combined effect:
- Severely depleted Well (small remainder)
- Both creators completely dissolved into creation
- One of the most magically powerful Mythoi in the multiverse
- Eadras placed among the seven Mythoi closest to the Bastion of
  Light (existing canon, now mechanistically explained)

The extra-richness of Eadras's intrinsic magic is the consequence of
this double-pour: Well-energy + Primordial-essence both went into
the substance of the Mythos.

This is unusual even among the seven closest Mythoi where primordial
sacrifice happens. The "do both" method is what makes Eadras
exceptional even within the rare-sacrifice cluster.

== Canon: planewalker strength ==

PLANEWALKERS FROM EADRAS ARE FUNDAMENTALLY 2-4X STRONGER THAN
PLANEWALKERS FROM OTHER MYTHOI.

Breakdown:
- 2-4x multiplier in MOST WAYS -- strength, magical capacity,
  endurance, depth/quality of exercised powers.
- "Fundamentally" -- this is structural, not situational. It is
  a property of being-from-Eadras.
- Applies to ALL planewalkers from Eadras, regardless of whether
  they are divine, mortal, ascended, or artifact-bound.

Mechanism (implied):
- Eadras's intrinsic magical richness infuses every being born or
  made within the Mythos.
- They carry that saturation with them when they cross Mythos
  boundaries.
- The Bastion-fed Well keeps the saturation at its source replenished.

Implications:
- Cross-Mythos contact involving Eadras is STRUCTURALLY ASYMMETRIC.
  An Eadras being visiting another Mythos arrives overpowered; a
  being from another Mythos visiting Eadras does not get the inverse
  boost.
- Mortal scholarship has largely not parsed this -- it operates
  invisibly to most peoples.
- The advantage is enduring, not transient. The saturation was
  woven in at substrate level when the planewalker came into being;
  they do not "lose" it by being away from Eadras.
