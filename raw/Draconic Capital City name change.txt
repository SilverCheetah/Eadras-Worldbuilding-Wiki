Draconic Capital City name change

Official Draconic Name
Rhtrakhadara
Full root form: RhTrKh a Rh a Dr a
Pronunciation (draconic):
Ruh-TRAKH-ah-DAH-rah
(Heavy emphasis on the second syllable, with a deep rumbling “kh” and rolling “r”. When spoken by an elder dragon it resonates like distant thunder.)
Meaning & Etymology

RhTrKh = Eternal / Ancient / Unyielding Memory
Rh (second root) = Radiant / Noble / Gathering
Dr = Guard / Stone / Enduring Place
Vowel pattern gives it the sense of “a place where many come together in peace”

Literal Translation:
“The Eternal Radiant Gathering”
or more poetically among dragons:
“The Place Where All Fires Burn as One Without Consuming”
Usage in Draconic

Full formal name: Rhtrakhadara
Common shortened form among dragons: Rhtrakha or simply Rhtrakh
In formal Council speech: Rhtrakhadara, Seat of the Ninety-Nine
Poetic / ritual form: Rhtrakhadara RhTrKh (“Eternal Convergence of the Fire”)

How Dragons Refer to It

“I fly to Rhtrakhadara” = neutral, factual
“I seek the Fire at Rhtrakhadara” = when going for Council business or treaty
“The Flames of Rhtrakhadara do not devour their own” = proverb about the city’s peace injunction
