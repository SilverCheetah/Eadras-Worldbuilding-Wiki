== Canonical term: shell, not uniform ==

The term "uniform" is useful shorthand, but the more accurate canonical
term is SHELL.

A shell is a physical and magical construct that fundamentally CHANGES
WHAT THE SPIRIT IS while they are wearing it. The shell is not clothing
the spirit puts on; it is an identity-state the spirit is placed into.

When a spirit is assigned a new role, they are placed into a different
shell. The assignment is literal:

  "You are now a nymph."
  "You are now a brownie."
  "You are now the genius loci of this forest."

Putting on a new shell transforms the spirit into what is functionally a
different type of fey/spirit, granting them an entirely new appearance,
new powers, AND a new set of responsibilities. The role is the bundle.

== Bundle: appearance + powers + responsibilities ==

A shell carries three things at once:
- Appearance (the body the spirit wears)
- Powers (the capabilities of the role)
- Responsibilities (the duties owed by that role)

Reassigning a shell changes all three simultaneously. A spirit reassigned
from a stream-nymph shell to a hearth-brownie shell is no longer the same
kind of being in any functional sense — different body, different powers,
different duties, different binding.

== Standardised vs bespoke shells ==

Two distinct flavours of shell exist across the four spirit realms:

STANDARDISED SHELLS — used in the bureaucratic realms (Celestia, Nyxar).
Issued from a code, organised by department and rank, mass-produced for
the workforce. A pit-fiend shell is structurally similar to every other
pit-fiend shell. The Divine and Infernal Bureaucracies maintain shell
catalogues like they maintain everything else: meticulously, with
revisions tracked. In these contexts "shell" doubles as "uniform" with
rank insignia, and the "uniform" shorthand is precise.

BESPOKE SHELLS — used in the Feywild and (presumably) Shadowdark realms.
Bound to SPECIFIC landscapes, hearths, streams, groves, or relationships.
A nymph is not just "a nymph"; she is the nymph of THIS stream. A brownie
is the brownie of THIS household. A genius loci is, by definition, the
spirit of one specific named place. The shell binds the role, the place,
and the relationship as a single unit. Reassignment changes all three
together. The "uniform" shorthand fits worse here -- a shell tied to one
specific forest isn't a uniform; it's a vessel for a specific role in a
specific place.

== Term usage going forward ==

- "Shell" is the canonical term.
- "Uniform" remains valid shorthand, especially in bureaucratic-realm
  contexts where standardised shells double as institutional rank
  markers.

  seraphim.md) can continue using "uniform" with a cross-reference to
