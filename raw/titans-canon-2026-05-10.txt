Titans — canon clarifications, 2026-05-10

(Session capture from Claude Code session in which Josh resolved the
engineering-doctrine asymmetry between Velrathi and Tarran via a five-whys
probe on the Orcs page, then expanded Titan canon to fill a gap in the wiki.)

== Progenitor social-role doctrines ==

Each of the three progenitor races covers one fundamental aspect of created
order, and engineers a servant-race cluster sized to that role:

- Tarran  = Protectors        -> engineer a martial cluster
- Velrathi = Deep Learning    -> engineer a scholarly civilizational ecosystem
- Elfen   = Nature Guardians  -> engineer preservation specialists

Tarran martial cluster:
- Aetherin -- shock troops against chaos and demonic incursions
- Titans   -- heavy front-line war engines against chaos
- Mitans   -- miners / subterranean / tunnel warriors

Velrathi scholarly ecosystem:
- Rathar -- scholars and archivists
- Grug   -- makers and maintainers
- Orug   -- internal guardians of Velrathi work (NOT war-against-chaos)

The Orug were engineered with deliberate non-aggression. The Velrathi did not
want their internal guardians starting fights with peer-engineered war creatures
(Aetherin, Titans). Inter-progenitor lane-discipline: the Tarran handle
war-against-chaos, the Velrathi handle the scholarly civilization, the Elfen
handle natural balance. Each cluster respects the others' jurisdiction.

== Titans specifically ==

Size:
- Adult height: 25-30 meters
- Child height: 10 meters
- "Mindblowingly huge -- living war machines."

Function:
- Bred for war against chaos.
- Heavy front-line obliteration -- moved into the path of demonic and chaotic
  incursions to crush them by sheer mass and reach.

Diet / metabolism:
- Eat almost constantly due to scale.
- Civilization built around managed Titans had to solve the calorie problem as
  part of fielding them: provisioning, livestock, grain-stores at scales most
  other peoples never need.

Civilized vs uncivilized:
- Civilized Titans = raised under Tarran discipline (or successor structure);
  full sapient beings with names, language, oaths, culture.
- Uncivilized Titans = feral; sapient still but reduced to predatory simplicity
  by the calorie demand. Eat what they catch, including sapient prey, alive.

Demon War status:
- Most Titans died (Demon War / Battle of the Broken Conclaves / surrounding
  actions). Pure Titan population decimated.
- Not all died -- a remnant survived. From this remnant descend both modern
  feral Titans AND, through natural devolution, the Giants -> Goliath.

Devolution chain (preserved from existing canon):
Titans -> Giants -> Goliath. (Note: existing goliath.md states Titans were
3m; that is wrong per this session's correction. The true devolution is far
more dramatic: 25-30m -> intermediate Giant scale -> ~3m Goliath.)

== Engineering register (style rule for any page referencing this canon) ==

"Engineering" in Eadras canon means:
- Spellcraft (ritual magic influencing offspring traits across gestations)
- Mendelian inheritance (multi-generational selective breeding, controlled
  lineages, accumulating modifications)

NOT:
- In-vitro genetic manipulation
- Viral vectors / gene splicing / CRISPR-style biotech
- Vat-grown bodies, constructs, or clones

The mechanism is biological and lineage-based, magically-influenced, and
multi-generational. Write in fantasy register (shaped, wrought, bred,
blood-crafted, lineage-shaping, spellcraft of lineage). Avoid biotech
vocabulary (gene, DNA, modify, splice, viral, vat, sequence, in-vitro).
