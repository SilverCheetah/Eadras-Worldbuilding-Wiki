# HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

This document extracts broader setting principles from the unicorn material. These principles are useful for memory systems, trauma cultures, nonhuman psychology, and ancient-knowledge design.

---

# PRINCIPLE 1: PERSONHOOD CAN BE TEMPORARY WITHOUT BEING FALSE

[DIAGNOSIS] A unicorn outside the herd is truly itself. Its choices, love, suffering, and errors matter. But that selfhood is not meant to be permanent.

This principle allows Eadras to treat personhood as real without assuming all personhood must be stable, permanent, isolated individuality.

---

# PRINCIPLE 2: HEALING IS INTEGRATION, NOT ERASURE

[DIAGNOSIS] Unicorn herds heal trauma by integrating individual experience into the herd. The memory remains, but its pain changes shape.

This principle contrasts with Beast-Folk sacred forgetting. Both can be valid trauma responses:
- Beast-Folk survive by forgetting enough;
- unicorns survive by sharing enough;
- death-priests survive by naming correctly;
- dragons survive by hoarding memory;
- mortals improvise.

---

# PRINCIPLE 3: DEEP MEMORY IS NOT THE SAME AS HISTORICAL RECORD

[DIAGNOSIS] A memory system can be ancient, accurate in pattern, and still not provide dates or schematics.

This principle protects mythic memory from being reduced to archive and protects scholarship from assuming all truth is citation-shaped.

---

# PRINCIPLE 4: DANGEROUS KNOWLEDGE CAN INFECT THE KNOWER

[DIAGNOSIS] Unicorn memory-quarantine exists because some experiences are not merely sad or frightening; they can become contaminants inside a collective mind.

This applies to:
- Black Vaults;
- demonic memory;
- entropy-warp;
- Primordial Language exposure;
- true names;
- certain divine judgments;
- Arathi sensory records.

---

# PRINCIPLE 5: THE MOST VISIBLE MEMBERS OF A SPECIES MAY BE THE LEAST REPRESENTATIVE

[DIAGNOSIS] Mortals mostly encounter Seasonal and Long Strayers, not Herd-Bound unicorns. Therefore mortal ideas about unicorns overemphasize solitary individuality.

This principle applies broadly: outsiders build false species models from the members who leave home.

---

# PRINCIPLE 6: TRANSLATION IS NOT TRANSCRIPTION

[DIAGNOSIS] Herd-Listeners do not copy unicorn memory into mortal language. They translate pressure, aversion, symbol, resonance, and pattern into usable warning.

This preserves ambiguity and allows mistranslation, politics, and humility to matter.
