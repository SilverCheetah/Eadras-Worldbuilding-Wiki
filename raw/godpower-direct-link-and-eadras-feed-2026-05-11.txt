Godpower — direct link, Spear's link, and Eadras's Bastion-feed retcon, 2026-05-11

(Session-capture: Josh sharpened the Godpower canon with two
additions and one major retcon to the earlier "Eadras has a large
Well" framing.)

== Canon: direct link ==

ALL GODS HOLD A DIRECT LINK to their Mythos's Well of Creation. The
connection is unmediated -- no intermediaries between deity and the
Creative Power, no priestly conduit, no permission needed beyond
divinity itself. A god IS, by definition, something with a direct
link to its local Well.

== Canon: Spear of Crystallized Entropy also has a direct link ==

One notable NON-DEITY entity in Eadras also holds a direct link to
the Well: the SPEAR OF CRYSTALLIZED ENTROPY. This is the canonical
reason the Spear operates at god-tier power and is so catastrophic
when wielded -- it has the same kind of unmediated tap that a god
has. In this very specific sense, the Spear is a god, though a
THING rather than a PERSON.

The existing dao.md page describes Dao having cursed the Spear; the
direct-link canon explains WHY the Spear required Dao's personal
intervention -- it operates at god-tier and only a higher-than-godly
power (Dao) could touch it.

== Canon: Eadras Well retcon ==

CORRECTION TO EARLIER FRAMING:

Earlier today's session canon: "Eadras is one of the most magically
rich Mythoi" + "the Eadras pantheon are among the most powerful
deities in the multiverse." I extrapolated this to mean "Eadras has
one of the largest Wells in the Omniverse." That extrapolation was
WRONG.

CORRECTED CANON:

- Eadras has the SMALLEST Well in the multiverse (raw allotment).
- The Omniversal Creator put very little Creative Power into Eadras
  at its making.
- What little Creative Power was put in was largely spent during
  creation.
- By raw-allotment measure, Eadras should be among the THINNEST
  Mythoi in the Omniverse, with one of the WEAKEST pantheons.

== Canon: Bastion-of-Light feed mechanism ==

Eadras's exceptional pantheon power is preserved by LOCATION rather
than allotment:

- Eadras sits IMMEDIATELY ADJACENT to the Concordance (the vast
  outer plane at the center of the Omniverse).
- Through that proximity, the BASTION OF LIGHT continuously feeds
  Eadras's Well.
- The Bastion is the absolute center of the Omniverse, home of the
  Omniversal Creator. It radiates Creative Power.
- The radiation passes through the Concordance and seeps into the
  nearest Mythos. That nearest Mythos is EADRAS.

Mechanism:

- Eadras's Well is small AND continuously topped up.
- Other Mythoi's Wells are large but slowly deplete (no comparable
  feed source).
- Eadras is magic-rich by RATE OF REPLENISHMENT, not by DEPTH OF
  WELL.
- The Eadras pantheon's strength is fundamentally A GIFT OF GEOGRAPHY.

== Implications ==

- A god in Eadras with a god of similar nature in a more distant
  Mythos: the distant god dwindles as their Mythos's Well drains;
  the Eadras god is constantly topped up.
- Eadras may be COVETED by other Mythoi for its proximity-to-Bastion.
- Demonic invasion of Eadras (per Demon War canon) is structurally
  WORSE at the cosmic scale: demons aren't just disrupting a powerful
  Mythos, they're potentially disrupting the closest seep point from
  the Bastion to the rest of the Omniverse.
- The Eadras pantheon's existence is CONDITIONAL on the continued
  feed. A pantheon that is fed-fed-fed by Bastion radiation is not
  the same kind of pantheon as one that owns a deep reservoir.
  Existential dependency on cosmic geography.
- The Spear's direct link makes it a "thing-god." It is the only
  non-person currently understood to hold this kind of connection.
