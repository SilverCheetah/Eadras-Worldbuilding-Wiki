Addition — Sourceflare manifestation (dragons):

The Sourceflare event that produced dragons resulted in nine distinct eggs, each corresponding to a unique archetype. These eggs were not scattered randomly but emerged as a structured manifestation, suggesting a controlled or constrained outcome rather than uncontrolled generation.

Each egg represented a different expression of draconic nature, establishing the foundational diversity of dragonkind from the moment of their emergence. All later dragons are descended from these original nine lineages.

This event occurred approximately 2,000,000 years after the Worldbreaker catastrophe, during a period when the world remained partially unstable and in recovery.
