================================================================
1. OVERVIEW
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] Draconic is the native language of dragons in Eadras. Dragons hatch already in possession of it — it is not acquired culturally but emerges with the species. This makes Draconic simultaneously biological, metaphysical, and cultural: effectively species firmware.

The language is structured around three interlocking layers:
- a triconsonantal root system (abjad)
- a vowel-template layer encoding operational stance
- agglutinative suffixes encoding status, register, and relationship

Together these layers produce a language of extreme semantic density, in which a single word can carry an object, its history, the speaker's relationship to it, ritual status, emotional stance, and political implication simultaneously.


================================================================
2. DRACONIC COGNITION — THE PRESSURES THAT SHAPED THE LANGUAGE
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] Draconic cognition is shaped by pressures unfamiliar to human linguistics:

- Immense lifespan
- Exceptional memory
- Hierarchical social nuance
- Metaphysical speech traditions
- Ritual precision
- Long historical continuity
- Vast conceptual time horizons

A language optimized for compressed, layered meaning is what such beings would evolve. Dragons think less in isolated words and more in semantic relationship structures.


================================================================
3. THE TRICONSONANTAL ROOT SYSTEM
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] The root system produces conceptual clustering. Each three-consonant root encompasses a family of related concepts that are not separate ideas in draconic cognition but facets of a single underlying pattern.

Example root: RhTrKh

This root encompasses:
- eternal
- enduring
- memory
- ancient continuity

A dragon does not parse these as four distinct concepts. They are one concept, viewed from four angles.


================================================================
4. THE VOWEL-TEMPLATE LAYER
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] The vowel-template layer operates on top of the consonantal roots:

- Consonants define core conceptual territory.
- Vowels define operational stance toward that concept.

A draconic listener parses, almost simultaneously:
- category
- intent
- relationship
- grammatical role
- philosophical framing

This information density means a draconic utterance encodes far more than its surface meaning.


================================================================
5. AGGLUTINATION
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] Stacked suffixes encode:
- status
- relational nuance
- tense
- ritual register
- authority
- emotional weight

A single draconic word may contain:
- the object
- its history
- the speaker's relationship to it
- ritual status
- emotional stance
- political implication

Dragons do not mind linguistic length the way humans do. They have neither breath constraints nor conversational impatience. Their words become semantic architecture rather than merely sounds.


================================================================
6. PHONOLOGY — PHONEME INVENTORY
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] Draconic phonology favors deep throat articulation, rear-mouth resonance, rolling vibration, fricative airflow, and chest-supported projection.

Example phonemes: Kh, Gh, Rh, Rr, Shr, Zr, Ng

The phoneme inventory has reduced reliance on delicate labials (p, b, m, f, v) because the large reptilian mouths producing Draconic have long jaws, heavy dentition, and different lip structure than humans. Dragon speech leans on:
- throat shaping
- tongue-root articulation
- airflow resonance
- chest harmonics


================================================================
7. PHONOLOGICAL SCALING ACROSS DRAGON AGES
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] The phonology works equally well for a hatchling, a mature dragon, or a mountain-sized elder, because it relies on resonance and airflow rather than fine precision articulation.

The sounds are physically scalable. An elder dragon saying "Rhtrakhadara" produces a word that can occupy enormous acoustic space.


================================================================

================================================================

[IN-WORLD] Sounds like Rr, Rh, Kh, Gh imply:
- sustained airflow

- resonance coupling

This fits dragon physiology directly. Dragons are fire-breathing creatures whose bodies already function as pressure-and-resonance systems.

When an elder dragon speaks formal Council Draconic inside a basalt chamber built for resonance, the language physically inhabits the space:

10. WHY MORTALS STRUGGLE WITH DRACONIC
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] The mortal difficulty with Draconic is not pronunciation alone. It is simultaneous semantic compression.

Mortals can produce phonetic approximations of draconic sounds, but they cannot parse the layered meaning in real time. Dragons hear layers mortals cannot.

The phonological difficulty is anatomical: humans lack the lung volume, throat depth, resonance chambers, jaw structure, and chest vibration to produce the deeper sounds correctly.

Even Weavespinners — mortals who reach further than most — only approximate the deeper resonances.

11. NEUROLOGICAL FOUNDATION IN DRAGONS
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] Because dragons hatch already speaking Draconic, the language is neurologically foundational to them.

A dragon hearing certain formal roots, ancestral cadence, or ritual resonance may respond viscerally rather than intellectually. The language reaches beneath conscious thought.

12. THE FLESH-EATER INVERSION
================================================================

[IN-WORLD] The Flesh-Eaters use flawless Council-formal Draconic during their kills.

This is not mockery. It is the use of civilization's highest sacred linguistic form within acts of deliberate anti-civilization. Council-formal Draconic — the deepest and most resonant register — becomes associated with terror, violation, and contamination for some dragons.

Some dragons may not be able to attend Council without flinching at certain root cadences.

================================================================

[IN-WORLD] Draconic has multiple registers, including:

These registers are distinguished by agglutinative complexity and by the phonological precision required.
