Inki
Summary: Inki is the Ascended Mortal God of invention, craftsmanship, and discovery — the divine patron of gnomes and all who seek to understand the hidden workings of the world. He was the first gnome to ever breach an Arathi vault.
Domain: Invention, mechanical genius, the unlocking of secrets, and the pursuit of knowledge through cleverness.
Appearance: Inki is usually depicted as a small, wiry figure with wild, snow-white hair and ink-stained fingers, surrounded by floating tools, half-built mechanisms, and glowing runes.
Personality: Curious, restless, and endlessly tinkering. He has little patience for tradition that stands in the way of progress, but he deeply respects well-made things and the satisfaction of solving a difficult problem.
Blessings: Gnomes born with naturally white hair are considered especially blessed by Inki. These individuals are almost always gifted with exceptional intelligence and inventive talent — true prodigies who seem to have received a direct spark of his divine genius.
Legend: Inki is famous for being the first gnome to successfully breach an Arathi vault, an achievement still regarded as the ultimate act of cleverness and daring in gnomish culture.
Symbol: A six-pointed gear with a single glowing rune in the center, or a hammer striking an anvil that creates sparks shaped like runes.
