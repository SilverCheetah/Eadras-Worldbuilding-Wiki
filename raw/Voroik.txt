Voroik

The Weavers of Fate, the the Talespinners, the Loom Sisters. Three deific beings in a dimensional space outside of the omniverse (outside of time, space, and fate) who weave the Omniversal Tapestry of Reality. They weave from strands composed of fate, each strand beloinging to an essential concept somewhere in the omniverse, with local areas of the tapestry for each Mythos.

Danvi - The Maiden, the puller

Erd - The Mother, the measurer

Skal - The Matron, the cutter
