Archons

Archons are the direct servants of the Omniversal Creator. There are three classes of Archons that interact with the sapient beings of the Omniverse: Deva, Planetar, and Solars.

Archons can be rarely seen carrying out the will of the OC throughout the multiverse. Whenever you see one, it is there by the will of the OC. They tend to be rather humorless, and mission focused.

Deva are the warriors, hands, spies, messengers, and grunts. Planetar are the NCO's, guiding squads platoons, or companies of Deva. And Solars are the Generals, warleaders, and champions.

Solars carry two of the most potent artifacts in the omniverse. A Solar's bow has a single, incredible enchantment - True Strike, but anywhere in the Omniverse. It cannot be fled, but it might be caught (very unlikely).

A solar's arrow has an even more potent enchantment - Eliminate. Whatever it touches will be killed, even if it cannot be killed.
