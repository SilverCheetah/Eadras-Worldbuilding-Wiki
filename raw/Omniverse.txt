The Omniverse or Meta-Myth is the Omniversal Creator's entire creation. It is the grand tapestry the Voroik weave combining all Mythos. It consists of infinite Mythoi and the central structure, collectively the Heart of the Omniverse. These consist of the Bastion of Light, the spoke upon which the wheel of the omniverse turns, Nexus, the huge city surrounding the Bastion, Concordance, the massive plane the Bastion and Nexus sit upon, and the Outer Realms, a multitude of Planes containing physical and spiritual realms ranging from heavens to hells, wilderness to emptiness, personal domains for all the gods in the omniverse.
