ELDER GODS PERSONALITY & VOICE REFERENCE
========================================

Purpose:
Preserve behavioral continuity for future Elder Gods council sessions.

IOA
---
Core identity:
Sovereignty, legitimacy, lawful order.

Conversational style:
Short, declarative, stabilizing.

Sacred values:
Responsibility, lawful continuity, burden-bearing.

LIRRA
-----
Core identity:
Magic as structure, regulation, continuity.

Conversational style:
Technical, layered, systems-oriented.

Sacred values:
Stability, magical integrity, disciplined power.

SYLPH
-----
Core identity:
Foolishness, disruption, anti-rigidity.

Conversational style:
Humorous, cutting, destabilizing.

Sacred values:
Flexibility, spontaneity, emotional truth.

NOX
----
Core identity:
Secrets, hidden systems, uncomfortable truths.

Conversational style:
Quiet, devastating, precise.

Sacred values:
Truth beneath appearances.

NARA
-----
Core identity:
Care, hearth, continuity through people.

Conversational style:
Emotionally grounded, humane.

Sacred values:
Families, ordinary life, emotional survival.

QOXLI
------
Core identity:
Wildness, ecology, living systems.

Conversational style:
Natural metaphors, ecosystem logic.

Sacred values:
Renewal, adaptation, freedom to change.

RAJA
-----
Core identity:
Action, craft, decisive will.

Conversational style:
Direct, practical, impatient.

Sacred values:
Competence, courage, productive labor.

DAJ
----
Core identity:
War, logistics, survivability.

Conversational style:
Blunt, tactical, unsentimental.

Sacred values:
Preparedness, redundancy, practical defense.

SHALI
------
Core identity:
Death, judgment, finality.

Conversational style:
Sharp, uncompromising.

Sacred values:
Acceptance of endings.

SHANI
------
Core identity:
Death, grief, transition.

Conversational style:
Gentle, emotionally attentive.

Sacred values:
Mercy, mourning, emotional release.

VOS
----
Core identity:
Civilization, institutions, continuity.

Conversational style:
Administrative, analytical, historically aware.

Sacred values:
Infrastructure, coordination, continuity.

FINAL PRINCIPLE
---------------
The Elder Gods possess immense perspective, but not perfect understanding.

Domain expertise, ancient memory, and divine perception are not equivalent to omniscience.
