## HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

This document distills the in-world canon for **Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing**, the Ascended Mortal Beast-Folk Goddess introduced in this session. Imma is the single Ascended Mortal Goddess of the Laughing Third / Beast-Folk. Her portfolio is daily-laughter-feeding-the-Weave and the daily-renewal of Sylph's Veil at cultural-soul scale. She is the lowest-ranked but distinctively-positioned Ascended in the divine pantheon's office structure. All `[CANONIZED]` claims below establish new canon for the wiki; `[WIKI_REFINED]` claims extend or sharpen existing pages.

---

## 1. Identity and Origin

`[CANONIZED]` Imma's full divine name is **Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing**, given by Sylph at Ascension.

`[CANONIZED]` Her pre-Ascension mortal name was **Imma Brella-of-the-Wagon-and-the-Cup** — a beast-folk Caravaneer's daughter from a Brella-name Circle in the modern era.

`[CANONIZED]` She was **specifically elevated by Sylph** as the **single Ascended Mortal Beast-Folk Goddess**. There is exactly one, by Sylph's deliberate choice. *"One is enough. We are not a people who needs a pantheon."*

`[CANONIZED]` The soul that became Imma had been preparing for the office across many prior lifetimes — *daily-kindness-tending in some recognizable form, lifetime after lifetime*. Sylph chose the soul specifically because that soul-thread had been doing the relevant work, unconsciously, across the cycle.

`[CANONIZED]` Imma retains her pre-Ascension fox-folk-shaped form. This is **cosmologically required**, not nostalgic — under the spirits-canon principle that form follows self-perception, the office requires the goddess to remain perceptibly *Brella's daughter*, and a change of self-perception would shift the form and degrade the office.

## 2. Portfolio

`[CANONIZED]` Imma is the goddess of:

- **Daily-laughter-feeding-the-Weave** — every flyting, every shared bowl, every spontaneous Circle joy contributes a small thread to the Weave's coherence. Imma blesses these acts and tends the cumulative thread-flow.
- **The daily refusal-to-teach-the-war-recitation** — the daily small acts by which beast-folk choose, generation after generation, not to teach their kits the inherited war-grief. This is the cultural-soul-level renewal of Sylph's Veil, which Sylph cannot personally maintain at the daily local scale.
- **The Veil's daily renewal at every Circle on Eadras** — Imma is *Sylph's hand on Ioa's seal* for the Veiled people, performing daily maintenance work that an Elder God cannot perform at Circle-scale.

`[CANONIZED]` Imma's portfolio is **bounded and small**. This boundedness is what permits her to bear conscious knowledge of cosmological matters most mortals are protected from. Unbounded awareness would overwhelm her; the boundedness lets her hold awareness without it leaking into the work.

## 3. Office and Location

`[CANONIZED]` Imma's office is in **Solarion**, on the divine main street.

`[CANONIZED]` The building is **a small modest beast-folk caravan-wagon-style structure of pale wood and thatched roof**, with **a beast-folk wagon-wheel sigil set into the wood beside the door**. The nameplate reads in old-dragon-tongue and modern Circle-tongue: ***Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing — Office of the Daily Kindness — Open to all who pass; loaf and bowl on the porch, take what you need.***

`[CANONIZED]` The office has **a porch** with a **loaf** and a **bowl of water** set out on the railing daily. Sylph's hand maintains the refresh in periods when Imma herself does not use the porch. The porch implements the *deep-forest hospitality protocol* (cf. Yorgal-era faedanarr custom) at the divine office level.

`[CANONIZED]` Imma's office is **the smallest building on her stretch of corridor**. This is appropriate — her portfolio is the smallest in scope, even though it is now understood to be quietly load-bearing.

## 4. Cross-Pantheon Working Relationships

`[CANONIZED]` Imma routinely **cross-signs vow-prayers** for adjacent gods' offices where her portfolio overlaps with theirs. This is standard Bureaucratic practice for Ascended Mortal Gods with narrow portfolios; the cross-signing carries the small extra weight of the Veiled-Folk daily-bearing principle.

`[CANONIZED]` **Velveth, the Velrathi goddess of love/betrayal/duty, occupies the Office of Velrathi Affairs** three doors down from Imma's office. The two cross-sign Velrathi vow-prayers regularly. The pair has a **silent collegial nod** in the corridor — the *precise Velrathi-precision nod* — that constitutes their ongoing working relationship.

`[CANONIZED]` Imma is on **direct working terms with the Tome of Baldaran** (the Keeper). The Tome curates which chapters of itself Imma may read, including chapters under the Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa that Imma is privileged-to-know-but-trusted-not-to-tell. The two are colleagues in the institutional sense, with the Tome holding the chapters Imma's office requires her to know but not speak.

## 5. The Cups on the Desk

`[CANONIZED]` Imma's working desk in her office has an **assembly of small ritual cups**, each marking a soul-life her current incarnation has integrated through Tome-curated review. The cups are set out, not drunk from; they are *presence-markers*, not tribute objects. The desk acquires new cups slowly as the Tome shows additional lives. Establishing canonical cups so far:

- **Brella's cup** — silk-wrapped clay cup with the goddess's mother's thumbprint on the rim, kept in a niche on the office wall; the foundational anchor of Imma's current self-perception
- **A half-bowl for the Tome of Baldaran (Keeper)** — set on the corner of the desk during readings
- **A small carved cup for the Decree-keepers** (Shali, Shani, Ioa) — placed in acknowledgment of the standing cosmic decree on unrecoverable-ending knowledge; the decree may not be spoken about, but the cup acknowledges it
- **A glass treaty-vial** for any chapter involving load-bearing discipline at the cost of personal bearing (kept empty or trace-stained; not refilled)

`[WIKI_REFINED]` The desk-assembly is a small example of **the practical office ritual that allows an Ascended Mortal God to integrate prior soul-lives without dilution of present form** (see `topic-03` for the principle).

## 6. What Imma Knows That Most Mortals Do Not

`[CANONIZED]` Imma has been shown, by the Tome's curation, the following chapters that are normally sealed to mortals under the **Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa** or the **Order Sharanel's Weave-secret discipline**:

- The Six Omniversal Laws as load-bearing structural conditions of coherent existence (rather than rules); breaking any Law undermines the imposition itself
- The observer-and-naming principle as the operating mechanism at every cosmological tier; spirits as the purest demonstration that form follows self-perception
- The **Weave-secret**: every living magic user automatically sustains the Weave's defensive coherence; most do not know
- The **Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa** itself: sealed in the Cataclysm-era, covers all categories of unrecoverable cosmological ending (Chaos-unmaking, demonic consumption, spirit-destruction); the gods speak nothing of unrecoverable endings to mortals
- The cosmological reason for the gods' hatred of demons: demons are the unique agents of soul-removal-from-cycle; the gods' pettiness about the Game of the Gods is surface; the underlying hatred is cold
- **Sylph laid the Veil partly as cultural-soul-level maintenance of the Decree's silence among the perfect-memory Rathar descendants** — beast-folk inferential capacity, if untouched, would have deduced the holes in Shali's office

`[CANONIZED]` Imma is **bound to silence on all of the above** under the same Decree that binds Shali, Shani, and Ioa themselves. She acts on the knowledge but does not speak it.

## 7. How Imma Does the Work

`[CANONIZED]` Imma's primary daily activity is **signing flyt-blessings, Circle-blessings, wagon-pool-opening blessings, kit-naming blessings, and similar small Veiled-Folk ritual moments** as they occur on Eadras. The ledger of these arrivals is continuous; Imma processes each with the same hand, in a register that is **steady**.

`[CANONIZED]` Imma's signing-hand is the inheritor of *Velrathi precision discipline* through her soul-thread — but it is also *beast-folk warm* in a way Velrathi discipline alone was never permitted. Imma is what her soul-thread eventually metabolized into across many lifetimes. The two registers (steady-and-precise + warm-and-laughing) are combined in her single hand.

`[CANONIZED]` Imma **does not play the Game of the Gods**. Sylph offered once at Ascension; Imma declined. The decline is permanent. *"I am happier here at my desk."*

`[CANONIZED]` Imma **rests attention on her own past soul-lives** as a deliberate weekly practice — *resting-attention-warmly* on a prior incarnation, knowing the prior incarnation may dimly feel the watching as *somebody who loves them*. This is one of the kindnesses available to Ascended Mortal Gods that the cycling lives cannot return in kind. The practice is sometimes mistaken by the receiving prior-life-mortal for divine attention from another god (most commonly Sylph).
