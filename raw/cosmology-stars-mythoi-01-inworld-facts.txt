# 1. THE NATURE OF THE STARS

[CANONIZED] [IN-WORLD] The stars visible in Eadras’s night sky are **not** distant suns or physical balls of gas.

They are other complete Mythoi — entire self-contained realities with their own gods, laws, peoples, and versions of creation — seen from the outside through the distorting lens of Limina.

[CANONIZED] [IN-WORLD] Limina acts as a thick refractive barrier. Light (or its metaphysical equivalent) can cross between Mythoi but is heavily warped, making other realities appear as twinkling points of light.

[CANONIZED] [IN-WORLD] There are **no** other planets in the local system. Eadras and its moons constitute the entire local Prime Material setup. Everything beyond the moons is looking outward from this Mythos into the greater Omniverse.

---

# 2. THE PRECESSION CLOCK AND THE SPEAR

[CANONIZED] [IN-WORLD] The slow precession of Eadras’s rotational axis creates a ~26,000-year cycle. When the sky returns to the exact configuration it had when the Spear of Crystallized Entropy was created, the full alignment acts as the cosmic lock that allows the Spear to open its pathway to raw divine power.

[CANONIZED] [IN-WORLD] This alignment is known in scholarly circles as “The Return” or “God’s Day.”

---

# 3. LAYERED STAR LORE ACROSS ERAS

[CANONIZED] [IN-WORLD] Different peoples have interpreted the same sky in radically different ways:

- **Arathi Era**: Crystal Lattices — vast geometric networks of light.
- **Dragons**: The Great Hoard of Souls — draconic shapes and patterns.
- **Unicorns**: Wounds in the Veil — organic, scar-like glowing patterns.
- **Modern Humanoids**: Multiple competing systems (Lirran Weave Constellations, folk animal/hero patterns, Katharim soul-lights).
