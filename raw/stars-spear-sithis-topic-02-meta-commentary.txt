# 1. Why the Cosmology Works

The stars-as-Mythoi concept succeeds because it preserves awe while avoiding fake astrophysics.

The sky reinforces:
- metaphysical isolation
- uncertainty
- layered epistemology
- cosmic scale

Different civilizations interpret the same phenomenon differently.

# 2. Why the Spear Works

The Spear succeeds because it is not merely:
- evil
- cursed
- intelligent
- a superweapon

It represents entropy as instinct.

The decision to make the cycle a lock instead of a recharge mechanism dramatically improved the concept.

The artifact now feels mythic rather than mechanical.

# 3. Divine Exhaustion

The gods function as:
- exhausted administrators
- traumatized custodians
- historical survivors

rather than perfect abstractions.

This keeps divine politics emotionally grounded.
