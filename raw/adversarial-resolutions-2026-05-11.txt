Adversarial probe resolutions — Eadras-position canon, 2026-05-11

(Session-capture: Josh's responses to the seven disagreement points
surfaced by the /adversarial probe on gods-of-eadras §Position in the
multiverse. Three are committed as canon updates this session;
others are confirmed not-issues.)

== Canon decisions ==

DISAGREEMENT 1 -- Independence of intrinsic richness vs Well size

RESOLUTION:
- The two properties are CONCEPTUALLY independent variables.
- In Mythoi created through Primordial sacrifice (the seven closest),
  the two CO-VARY by the same cause: pouring out Well-allotment AND
  Primordial essence into the substance produces intrinsic richness
  at the cost of the Well that remains.
- The typical CONSEQUENCE (small remaining Well → modest pantheon)
  follows in most sacrifice-Mythoi.
- Eadras is unusual: the Bastion-feed location DECOUPLES the
  consequence from the cause. Small Well never empties; godpower
  consequence does not follow.
- So: conceptually independent, empirically correlated at cause in
  sacrifice-Mythoi, decoupled at consequence in Eadras.

DISAGREEMENT 2 -- Unbounded vs bounded Godpower

RESOLUTION: Three-axis bounding model.

Godpower availability is constrained by:
  1. WELL SIZE -- the reservoir from which gods draw.
     - In normal Mythoi: bounded by what's leftover after creation.
     - In Eadras: effectively unlimited due to Bastion feed.
  2. PER-GOD DRAW CAPACITY -- the rate/quantity an individual god
     can channel from the Well in a given working.
     - Bounded everywhere, including Eadras.
     - Auralis and Thamior canonically used their full draw plus
       their own life-force trying to save the Arathi. They didn't
       drain the Well; they hit their own draw limit + spent their
       personal essence. (Per doctrine-of-unheld-hand.md.)
  3. DIVINE BUREAUCRATIC CHANNELING -- the institutional speed,
     competence, and availability of the Divine Bureaucracy to
     translate divine intent into mortal-world effect.
     - The Bureau operates on RULES (doctrine, Favor, procedure),
       not on power scarcity.
     - Miracle denials are doctrinal/procedural, not capacity-
       based.
     - This is a separate bottleneck from raw Godpower.

The Eadras pantheon is UNBOUNDED on axis 1 and BOUNDED on axes 2
and 3.

DISAGREEMENT 3 -- Does Dao count as primordial-tier active divinity?

RESOLUTION: NO. (Categorical.)

Specifically:
- Dao is NOT a Mythos divinity.
- Dao holds NO MYTHOS PORTFOLIO -- unlike Elder Gods and Ascended
  Mortal Gods who each regulate an aspect of mortal or cosmological
  life, Dao has no domain within Eadras's pantheon order.
- Dao does NOT regulate the Mythos's internal affairs.
- Dao operates as an Omniversal Creator Avatar -- a tier-prior
  cosmic layer that does not enter into the Mythos's internal
  divinity ordering.
- "No primordial-tier active divinity in Eadras" is therefore
  correct without qualification when referring to the Mythos's
  pantheon. (The page can usefully clarify that Dao is the cosmic-
  prior exception, but he is not "primordial-tier" in the Mythos's
  sense of that classification.)

== Non-issues (Skeptic findings rejected) ==

SKEPTIC FINDING: "Unbounded Godpower contradicts the Bureau."
REJECTED: Bureau operates on rules, not on power scarcity. Auralis/
Thamior is a per-god draw-capacity precedent, not a Well-drain
precedent. The two are different mechanisms; no contradiction.

SKEPTIC FINDING: "No primordial-tier divinity contradicts Dao."
REJECTED: Dao is not a Mythos divinity. The Position section's
claim refers to the Mythos's pantheon, in which Dao does not
participate (no portfolio, no regulation, no domain). His existence
in a tier-prior cosmic layer is not a contradiction.

SKEPTIC FINDING: "Expansionist gods is unsupported."
NOT-AN-ISSUE: Primary focus of the wiki is Eadras. Omniverse
descriptions are useful background context, not load-bearing
canon that requires rigorous support. Acceptable as background.

SKEPTIC FINDING: Cross-Mythos divine business not mechanically
established.
RESOLVED: Gods from different Mythoi conduct business with each
other in the Outer Realms (circling Concordance) and in Nexus
itself. The cross-Mythos venues are the established mechanism.
Existing canon (omniverse.md) supports this -- "Every god from
every Mythos across the Omniverse maintains a personal domain in
the Outer Realms." Not load-bearing for the Position section but
worth noting as available canon.
