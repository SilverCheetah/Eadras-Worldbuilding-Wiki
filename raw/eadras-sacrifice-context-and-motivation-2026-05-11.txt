== Correction: sacrifice is rare, not unique ==

In the infinite Omniverse, primordial sacrifice has happened in a
small handful of Mythoi:

- The SEVEN MYTHOI CLOSEST to the Bastion of Light (Eadras is one of
  them). All seven had primordial sacrifices.
- POSSIBLY some Mythoi of the SECOND COSMOGRAPHICAL RING outward from
  the Bastion (canonically uncertain; open canon).

Across the vast majority of the Omniverse: NO sacrifice. Primordials
are still present, still ruling, still in possession of their Wells.

The pattern is "still very, very rare" -- in an infinite Omniverse,
7 (or maybe ~14 if the second ring counts) is functionally zero
percent. But not literally unique to Eadras.

Existing primordial-duo.md canon (which I missed earlier today)
already established: "This also placed Eadras among the seven Mythoi
closest to the core of the Omniverse." So the 7-closest framing is
not new -- it's old canon I should have known about and respected.

== Motivation for the sacrifice ==

Evye and Fryr's reasons for sacrificing themselves:

1. THEY HAD NO DESIRE TO RULE.
   The creator's role and the ruler's role are distinct. The
   Primordial Duo wanted only the first.

2. THEY WANTED TO CREATE THE MOST BOUNTIFUL, DIVERSE, MAGICALLY RICH
   CREATION POSSIBLE.
   They were willing to spend themselves for it. Greater creation
   required greater sacrifice; they were willing.

3. THEY TRUSTED THE ELDER GODS (their children) to:
   - Shape creation ALONGSIDE them during the creative act
   - Rule after the Primordial Duo's departure

Implications:

- The Elder Gods are not merely heirs by accident. They are
  co-creators by design, and rulers by deliberate handoff. Evye and
  Fryr trained their children for both roles.
- The sacrifice is a deliberate transfer from creators-who-do-not-
  want-to-rule to children-who-can.
- The intrinsic magical richness of Eadras is the goal achieved
  through this trade-off: deep substance saturation, at the cost of
  primordial existence.

== Connection to other canon ==

- This explains why the Elder Gods are at the top of Eadras's active
  pantheon. By design.
- This is why Eadras's intrinsic magical richness is unusual: the
  Primordial Duo deliberately optimised for richness over their own
  survival.
- The same likely holds for the other six Mythoi closest to the
  Bastion. Their primordial creators also chose creation over
  rulership.
- Why proximity to the Bastion correlates with primordial sacrifice
  is an open question. Existing canon (primordial-duo.md) says the
  sacrifice "placed" Eadras close to the core. So sacrifice ->
  proximity, not the other way around. Implication: pouring all
  your essence into a creation pulls that creation closer to the
  OC's home. (Plausibly: the more divine essence in a Mythos's
  substance, the closer it gets drawn to the Bastion.)
