The Bastion of Light is the absolute center of the Omniverse, the pole around which everything revolves. It sits at the center of Nexus, Concordance, and can be seen from anywhere in concordance at the same brightness.\
\
The Bastion is supposed to be the home of the Omniversal Creator, and only Archons freely move in and out.

The light of the Bastion can be seen at the same level of brightness anywhere in Concordance, a supernatural effect.
