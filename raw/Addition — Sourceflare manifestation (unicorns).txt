Addition — Sourceflare manifestation (unicorns):

The Sourceflare event that produced unicorns resulted in the emergence of a complete herd rather than isolated individuals. This indicates a fundamentally different manifestation pattern from the earlier dragon event.

The herd structure suggests that unicorns were created as a cohesive ecological and social unit, rather than as independent archetypes. Their nature is therefore inherently collective and system-oriented.

This event occurred approximately 4,000,000 years after the creation of dragons, during a period of planetary stability characterized by a supercontinent configuration and mature ecosystems.
