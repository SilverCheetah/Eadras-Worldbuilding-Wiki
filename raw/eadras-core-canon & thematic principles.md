It is interpretive and now incorporates all principles from Principles of Eadras.
Core Cosmology
Eadras exists within a larger Omniverse created by the Omniversal Creator. The Omniverse is surrounded by Chaos — not evil, but the absence of coherent reality. A Mythos is a bounded region of imposed structure floating within Chaos; Eadras is one such Mythos.
Reality in Eadras is observation-dependent: things that are not observed do not fully exist; things that are named are observed by their namer. The Mythos is slightly observer-hungry because the Primordial Duo (Evye & Fryr) sacrificed themselves completely into creation rather than ruling from above. There is no ultimate external observer.
The Primordial Duo created the Elder Gods and then sacrificed themselves into creation itself. The Elder Gods are therefore inheritors and stewards of a reality built from sacrifice — they were born into responsibility.
The Elder Gods
Ancient beings embodying major conceptual domains. They are not perfectly moral, omniscient, or emotionally stable abstractions. They are:

ancient
exhausted administrators
political
wounded
prideful

They rarely intervene directly (Doctrine of the Unheld Hand + institutional restraint). They deceive one another through long cons rather than raw power (Divine Containment Protocols). Divine presence requires mediation via exactly one Icon per god.

The Weave
The Weave is the modern magical infrastructure of Eadras.
It was created during the Demon War by Lirra to prevent demons from drinking the world’s magic dry.
Magic before the Weave was raw, direct, and dangerous. The Weave regulates magic through structured channels.
Netharim are biologically tied to the Weave. Windtalkers stabilize it at planetary scale.
The Weave is:

alive
wounded
repairable
fragile in places
partially dependent on mortal existence

Many major historical events damaged the Weave.
Timeline Skeleton (high-level public framing)

Arathi Era – First true hypercivilization (≈10 million years). Mastered crystalmancy, magitek, conduit infrastructure. The lost civilizational ceiling.
Arathi Cataclysm – Kudur-eṭir forged/claimed the Spear of Crystallized Entropy → Worldbreaker entered via the First Breach. Shattered the world, created plate tectonics, killed most life, permanently traumatized history. Foundational geological and metaphysical trauma.
Chaos Crusades – Reality breaches; introduced horrors and aberrations.
Demon War – Xar’vion summoned a Demon Prince → ontological warfare; half of all life lost; Weave created; Beast-Folk born; Great Betrayal and Banishment. Foundational societal and existential trauma.
Anti-Magic Crusade – A Black Hand manipulation campaign disguised as anti-magic hysteria that targeted all magic users.
The Crusade:
• killed Windtalkers
• damaged the Weave
• caused the Thousand-Year Dimming
• shattered magical trust
• destroyed Sarathis
The world never fully recovered.
Modern Eadras – Rebuilding. Civilization, magic, and trade are rising again, but every society carries inherited trauma. Progress exists; fear of progress also exists.

Tone Guidance
Eadras should feel: ancient • emotionally layered • intellectually serious • occasionally absurd • tragic without nihilism • hopeful without naivety • grand without losing ordinary life.
It is epic dark fantasy with earned hope, not grimdark. Light and darkness amplify each other. Humor, warmth, and beauty exist inside a world that remembers catastrophe. Deep time is core identity. Mystery is stronger than explanation. Humor prevents tonal collapse. Probability is more interesting than destiny. Prophecy creates administrative panic.
