ARATH AND THE SIN OF PERMANENCE
===============================

Core thesis:
Arath was not merely a lost civilization.
It was the civilizational wound by which all later civilization measures itself.

ARATHI CHARACTER
----------------
The Arathi were:
- communal
- layered
- systems-oriented
- continuity-focused
- deeply integrationist

CRYSTALMANCY
-------------
Crystalmancy was not "magic with crystals."
It was a complete civilizational grammar.

Major crystal lineages:
- Saelith Choirs
- Veyruun Bones
- Ishkara Veins
- Thamrite Mirrors
- Oru-Sha Blades

CORE PHRASE
-----------
"What is harmonized is entrusted."

CONTEXT-BOUND RELICS
--------------------
Some relics are not broken.
They are incomplete because the civilization around them is gone.

Examples:
- missing atmospheres
- extinct civic systems
- vanished magical ecologies
- dead biological assumptions

KUDUR-EṬIR
-----------
"He was its prayer spoken too loudly."

The defining Arathi error:
The refusal to permit lawful ending.
