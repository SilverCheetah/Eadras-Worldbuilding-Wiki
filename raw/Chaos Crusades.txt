Chaos Crusades

Two times Chaos has breached the Mythos, using the hole drilled by Ssarvath.

One was faced by Dragons and the Gods, the other was faced by combined force of Dragons, Unicorns, and the Gods.

When Chaos enters a Mythos, it has to take on temproary physical form, or it will dissolve just like anything from a mythos would dissolve if it stepped into chaos.

So the raw chaos that flooded into the Mythos the first time took the shapes of the most basic things imaginable - first order ideals, colors, smells, etc.

And they wreaked havok on 1/3 of the prime material, Bright Mirror, and Dark Mirror, until the Gods realized the Dragons could not win and shoved the Chaos out, sealing the hole.

The second invasion was much different. Chaos took the form of Dragons and Unicorns, but they didn't understand, so their forms were dragon and unicorn body-horror. The gods were quicker to respond.
