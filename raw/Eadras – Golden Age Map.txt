Eadras – Golden Age Map (Pre-Worldbreaker Era)
This illuminated Arathi cartographic masterpiece depicts the world of Eadras at the absolute peak of the Arathi civilization, roughly a generation before Ssar’veth raised the Spear of Crystallized Entropy. Created in the classic Arathi style with glowing crystal inlays, gold leaf, and living ley-line illumination, the map centers on the continent-spanning empire ruled from Altea, the radiant “Heart of the Conduits.”
From Altea radiate thirteen major Crystal Conduits — vast arcane arteries of crystallized Eldritch that channeled reflected Sourceshine across the entire world and back into the planetary core. Each conduit ends at a numbered Terminus (massive crystal citadels and fortress-cities): Northwatch, Frosthold, Skyreach, Sunspire, Dawnward, Emergate, Starfall, Southwatch, Stoneward, Westwatch, Stormpear, and others.
Major regions visible include:

The frozen Haelings and Cloudhelm Mountains
Veridian Wilds and Evergreen Vale
Eldrim Forest
Golden Savannas and Aurelian Plains
Sea of Glass, Sea of Mirrors, and Sea of Origins
Kharosian Waste, Crystal Wastes, and Obsidian Highlands
Thalorian Range and Sea of Giants

The map beautifully illustrates the Arathi ideal: perfect harmony between crystal magitek civilization and the natural world. Ley roads and ancient highways link thriving cities, citadels, temples, and fortresses. This is the world at its height of magical and technological mastery — mere years before the cataclysm that would shatter the continent, birth the Verdant Scar where Altea once stood, and plunge Eadras into ten thousand years of recovery.
