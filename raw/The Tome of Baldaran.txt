The Tome of Baldaran

The Tome of Baldaran exists to chronicle every important event in this Mythos (Eadras), that is decisions that lead to inflection points where the timeline splits. Additionally the Tome records all collected knowledge ever discovered in this Mythos.

The Tome of Baldaran often appears as a culturally relevant document, appearing as a psi crystal to dragons and unicorns, as a book to humans, as a rune-slate to dwarves, as a bark scroll to elves, etc.

It has been present in society since the second age, having the magical ability to conceal and transform itself. It is a sentient object, created for its singular purpose.

It often appears to aid heroes at unexpected times, a purpose it was not designed for, but one its innate intelligence seems to enjoy.


The Tome was created by the Ascended Mortal God Baldaran in the Third Age with the assistance of the Elder God Nox. The Tome exists in a pocket dimension outside of Eadras (withing Baldaran's godly realm), thus it is able to accurately chronicle events that occurred before it was created.
