Addition — Ixthal motivation:

Ixthal's actions were driven by prolonged isolation following the destruction of the Ararhi civilization. As the last survivor, he existed alone for approximately 2,000,000 years while repeatedly petitioning the elder gods to intervene in mortal affairs.

After these petitions were denied, he chose to act independently. His goal was not to gain power or control, but to restore the presence of intelligent mortal life.

He does not regret these actions and considers them justified despite their violation of established rules.
