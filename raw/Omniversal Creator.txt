The Omniversal Creator is the supreme deity of the Omniverse. It is omnipotent, omnipresent, and omniscient. It is said to reside in the Bastion of Light at the center of Nexus, Concordance, but no one knows for sure.

The OC created everything by creating each individual Mythos and turning them over to the creator deities. The OC created the Archons as direct servants and they alone are allowed within the Bastion of Light.

Dao is an example of an avatar of the OC. Dao doesn't have all of the OC's power, he is limited on purpose. Make no mistake, Dao has enormous power, more than an Elder God but less than a Mythos Creator Deity.
