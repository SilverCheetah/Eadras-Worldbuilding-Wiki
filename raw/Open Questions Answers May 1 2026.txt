**Last elder-troll awakening** — whether the dragons' visible unease at the remembered awakening is from witnessed events or only from troll-told records preserved in Ratharil archives.

Ratharil has deep, deep, deep vaults for archives, magically preserved stone and/or metal tablets written in draconic and kept for the benefit of all dragon clans. Each clan posts an archivist permanently (on a rotating basis, the same dragon is not necessarily there for millions of years); yes there are records of Trolls waking up in the archive; yes there are dragons old enough to have experienced elder trolls waking up and describing it for the archives; some archive records (very few) record the entire memories of the eldest dragons on memory crystals, technology gained from arathi ruins

Inki was the gnome who opened the arathi vault. that vault had the food fabricator and a laboratory for magitek and crystalmancy, as well as quite a lot of other things - living quarters, storerooms, etc
