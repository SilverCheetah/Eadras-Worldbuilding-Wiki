# 1. Cosmology of the Night Sky [CANONIZED]

The stars visible from Eadras are not distant suns.

They are other Mythoi: complete, self-contained realities existing elsewhere in the Omniverse.

Limina acts as:
- a metaphysical transition layer
- a distortion medium between realities

Because of Limina’s distortive effects, other Mythoi appear only as twinkling points of light.

Most inhabitants of Eadras do not know this.

# 2. The Great Precessional Cycle [CANONIZED]

Eadras experiences an astronomical precession cycle approximately every 26,000 years.

The cycle gradually returns the night sky to a previous configuration.

This cycle functions as:
- a cosmic lock
- a repeating celestial condition
- the activation key for the Spear of Crystallized Entropy

The cycle is a lock, not a recharge mechanism.

# 3. The Spear of Crystallized Entropy [CANONIZED]

The Spear:
- cannot be destroyed
- resists divine authority
- possesses instinctive quasi-intelligence
- seeks corruptible wielders

The Spear is not fully sentient.

It behaves more like:
- hunger
- instinct
- metaphysical predation

During the correct alignment, the Spear opens a pathway into the raw Source of creation.

The wielder undergoes simultaneous transformation of:
- body
- soul
- mind

The resulting being becomes an unrestricted Elder God.

# 4. Current Location of the Spear [CANONIZED]

The Spear currently rests near mantle depth beneath the Worldbreaker Scar.

It is protected by a metaphysical enclosure.

The location remains functionally inaccessible.
