Devils are the common rank-and-file spirits of Nyxar, primarily based in Dreadharrow. They serve as the everyday staff of the Infernal Bureaucracy.
Their work consists of endless paperwork — processing damned souls, managing torture quotas, filling out forms about forms, performance reviews, and maintaining the vast bureaucratic machine that is the Infernal Bureaucracy. The entire operation runs with terrifying efficiency. It is not chaotic or dysfunctional; it is competent, relentless, and soul-crushing in its effectiveness. This competence is what makes it horrifying.
In addition to their bureaucratic duties, devils also serve as tempters and adversaries in the mortal world. This is simply another assigned role within the system.
While they maintain the traditional fiery, infernal aesthetic when appearing in the material plane, their true nature is bureaucratic. The horror of devils comes not from fire and brimstone, but from the fact that their infernal system works — and works extremely well.
The Infernal Bureaucracy has a dreadful, soul-crushing office atmosphere similar to a mix of Death and Taxes, the Hell from Disenchantment, the TVA from Loki, and Robot Hell from Futurama. Everything is paperwork. The machine never stops.
They are the dark mirror to the Seraph of Celestia. Where Seraph staff the Divine Bureaucracy and carry out the will of the gods, Devils staff the Infernal Bureaucracy and carry out its cold, efficient malice.
