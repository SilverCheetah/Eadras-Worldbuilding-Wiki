# Shali

**Summary**: Shali is one of the twin Elder Goddesses of death, born from the splitting of the tenth Elder God at creation — the compassionate, patient, merciful half who gently escorts worthy souls to peace and rest, locked forever in argument with her twin [[shani|Shani]].

**Sources**: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt, Creation Myth.txt, Gods of Eadras.txt, More corrections.txt, Grok Chat Results 6-2-26.txt (2026-06-02, crystal-moon detail)

**Last updated**: 2026-06-02

---

## Domain

Death and the judgment of souls — specifically, the gentle escort of worthy souls to peace and rest (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

In the [[creation-myth|first naming]], Shali stepped up with chin high: *"I am Shali — goddess of death."* — only for [[shani|Shani]] to immediately contest the claim.

## The Split at Creation

Shali and [[shani|Shani]] are **two halves of the same original soul**, split at creation because each half interpreted the divine decree regarding death differently (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt). The tenth Elder God, intended to be one being, came into existence already torn — one half gentle, the other stern.

This split is canonized in the [[creation-myth|Creation Myth]] as *"Death, born torn."*

## Personality

Compassionate, patient, and merciful (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt). Shali's argument with her twin is fundamentally over which approach to death is superior — Shali maintains hers is.

## The First Argument

The very first words spoken between the twins were:

> Shali: *"I am Shali, goddess of death, and she's a mistake."*
> Shani: *"No, I'm not!"*
> Shali: *"Yes, you are!"*

Cut short by [[ioa|Ioa]]'s *"Be quiet, both of you."* (source: Creation Myth.txt). They have not stopped bickering since.

## Mortal Perception

Mortals know there are two goddesses of death. Shali is respectfully called **"Mistress Death"** or **"Goddess Death"** (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

## Splitting the First Soul

After the great sacrifice of [[primordial-duo|Evye and Fryr]], Shali and Shani — *still bickering* — split the first soul: one to light, one to dark (source: Creation Myth.txt).

## Tension With Nox

Shali views [[nox|Nox]]'s liberal use of [[necromancy-and-undeath|undeath and necromancy]] as distasteful — a mockery of death. She does **not** view it as inherently evil; it is a violation of her domain, not a moral wrong (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

## Ioa's Management

[[ioa|Ioa]] frequently keeps the twins apart and occupied to prevent their constant arguments from disrupting the pantheon. He sometimes assigns deliberately menial or absurd tasks — for example, personally reaping every fly that dies in the southern wetlands on a given day — just to keep them busy and separated (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

## Celestial aspect — twin crystal moon

One of the two **twin crystal moons** of Eadras is named for Shali. Shali and her twin [[shani|Shani]] are the third- and fourth-largest of [[four-moons-of-eadras|Eadras's four moons]]; they orbit each other while jointly orbiting Eadras, and are seen together in the sky as a coupled pair. The crystalline composition of the moons aligns with the metaphysical crystallinity often associated with the death-twin domain — clarity, finality, the unbreakable. The moons are each **roughly the size of Charon** and **made entirely of the crystal used in [[crystalmancy|crystal magic]] and technology**; the pair shines one **blue** and one **purple** (which colour belongs to which goddess is unspecified). (source: Grok Chat Results 6-2-26.txt)

Like all four moons of Eadras, Shali is **reflected across the Wyldglimmer and Glimmerdark** — there is a Shali (and a Shani) in each plane. (source: More corrections.txt)
