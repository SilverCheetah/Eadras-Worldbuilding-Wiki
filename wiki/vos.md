# Vos

**Summary**: Vos is the Elder God of cities, wisdom, order, law, justice, wealth, commerce, and technology â€” the methodical and aloof architect of civilization, close ally of [[raja|Raja]] and [[nara|Nara]] and a frequent foil to [[qoxli|Qoxli]] and [[sylph|Sylph]].

**Sources**: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt, Creation Myth.txt, Gods of Eadras.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-04-29

---

## Domain

Civilization, structure, progress, lawful prosperity (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt). Formal pantheon listing covers cities, wisdom, order, law, justice, wealth, commerce, and technology (source: Gods of Eadras.txt).

He was the fourth of the [[creation-myth|ten Elder Gods]] to step forward in the first naming. In the [[lirra|Lirran]] cosmological summary of the Elder Gods, *"Vos codifies."*

## Personality

Methodical, intellectual, precise, and somewhat aloof. Has a dry wit and **little tolerance for foolishness** (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

## Relationships

- **Close allies with** [[raja|Raja]] and [[nara|Nara]]
- **Frequent clashes with** [[qoxli|Qoxli]] and [[sylph|Sylph]]
- **Respects** [[lirra|Lirra]] but **finds her magic chaotic**
- **Works closely with** [[lirra|Lirra]] on matters of structure (Lirra reciprocates: she works closely with Vos)

## Note on Spelling

Some source material renders his name as "Voss"; the canonical spelling is **Vos**.
