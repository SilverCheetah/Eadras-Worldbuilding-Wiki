# Apotheosis

**Summary**: **Apotheosis** is the cosmological transition by which a living mortal becomes a god in the Eadras [[eadras-cosmology|Mythos]]. The mechanism is **unique to each case** — there is no single procedure to record, and no [[gods-of-eadras#Ascended Mortal Gods|Ascended Mortal God]] has ever fully described the experience even to another. What is observable is the outcome: at a specific moment, the mortal's body becomes an [[icons|Icon]] anchored to the Prime Material, the mortal's mind becomes divine, an outer-plane divine realm appears, and one or more divine [[icons#Simulacra|simulacra]] appear in the spirit realms. Apotheosis can only happen to living mortals. A dead soul must be reborn into the cycle and have its candidacy reset across new lifetimes.

## What is observable at the moment

At the moment of Apotheosis, four things happen simultaneously:

- **The mortal body becomes Icon** — the [[icons|Prime-Material anchor]] required for any god to directly interact with the Mythos. The Icon manifests *on the Prime Material* at that moment, regardless of where the candidate's body was at the time. (Velveth's body was in [[veyr-keth|Veyr'Keth]] on the [[glimmerdark-gaia|Glimmerdark]] reflection of [[gaia|Gaia]] at her Apotheosis; her Icon still manifested on the Prime Material of Eadras.)
- **The mortal mind becomes divine** — qualitatively transformed, not merely augmented. Memory, identity, and continuity of selfhood are preserved; cognition is restructured to operate at divine scale.
- **A divine realm is instantly created in the outer planes** — a new outer-plane region specific to the Ascended. Velveth's is the [[hall-of-the-long-quiet|Hall of the Long Quiet]]. Imma's office in [[solarion|Solarion]] is not her divine realm but her institutional Simulacrum presence; her outer-plane realm is the larger primary presence.
- **One or more divine simulacra appear in the spirit realms** — wherever the Ascended's divine power has reach. Solarion ([[celestia|Celestia]]) is the most common; [[nyxar|Nyxar]], the [[wyldglimmer|Wyldglimmer]], the [[glimmerdark|Glimmerdark]], and the Elemental Planes are also possible.

The Icon, the divine mind, the outer-plane realm, and the simulacra are all the same goddess — distributed across the cosmology, but one consciousness.

## The mechanism is unique to each case

**The mechanism of each Apotheosis is different from every other Apotheosis.** No single procedure exists.

The [[tome-of-baldaran|Tome of Baldaran]] does not record the mechanism — not because of the [[decree-of-shali-shani-ioa|Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa]] or any other formal silence, but because *there is no single mechanism to record*. Each Ascended Mortal God's transition was its own private cosmological event. Similarities across cases certainly exist, but the gods have never compiled them. [[gods-of-eadras#Ascended Mortal Gods|Ascended Mortal Gods]] are characteristically reluctant to discuss the moment of their own Apotheosis, even with one another. The experience is regarded as **intensely personal** — possibly the most personal experience the cosmology contains.

A small number of fragmentary details are preserved in *scattered mortal myth*, distributed across the religious traditions of peoples who worship various Ascended Mortal Gods. No single tradition contains a description of Apotheosis; together, the fragments would form a partial mosaic if anyone were to compile them. Such a compilation has not been attempted in known canon.

## Apotheosis can only happen to living mortals

This rule is absolute. A soul that has died must be reborn into the cycle and have its candidacy reset across new lifetimes. There are no posthumous Ascended Mortal Gods.

This rule shapes the *timing* of every documented Apotheosis. [[velveth|Kathira]] lived on in [[veyr-keth|Veyr'Keth]] for an indeterminate span after the [[great-betrayal|Great Betrayal]] and the [[great-banishment|Great Banishment]]; her Apotheosis occurred during that continued mortal life, not at the moment of the poisoning. [[imma|Imma Brella-of-the-Wagon-and-the-Cup]] was a living modern-era [[beast-folk|beast-folk]] caravaneer's daughter; her Apotheosis occurred during her life. [[baldaran|Baldaran]] ascended in the Third Age while living. Each case is the same in this single respect.

## Designation, not acquisition

Apotheosis is **not earned** in the transactional sense and **not acquired** by ritual. It is best understood as **designation** — the cosmology recognising that a specific living soul has crossed a threshold whose nature is unique to that soul's life. The designation is keyed to *the specific soul with its specific architecture of bearing*, not to a generic candidate-type.

Velveth's case is the cleanest illustration. A different [[velrathi|Velrathi]] princess in [[velveth#Mortal life|Kathira's]] position — same political role, same engagement, same act — would have produced *a different goddess, or no goddess at all*. The portfolio that Velveth holds is shaped by Kathira's specific [[velveth#The Spellsinger work|Spellsinger]] discipline, her specific eighty-year engagement, her specific [[the-long-quiet|six-hour Long Quiet]], her specific architecture of betrayal-love-duty held as one bearing. The Apotheosis-event selected Kathira *because she was the only configuration that produced the required portfolio*.

This is **Principle 17 — Designation Over Acquisition** of the in-world principles (see [[inspirations#In-World Principles]]).

Designation has a *capacity side*, however: the slow [[collection-fields|collection-field]] feedback loop (power grows the field; the field feeds power and wisdom) is what brings a living soul to the structural threshold at which designation is even possible. The two doctrines are complementary, not competing — the field-ceiling explains why candidates are rare and old in power and wisdom; designation explains why standing at the threshold has never, by itself, made a god. See [[collection-fields#The ceiling — collection fields and Apotheosis]].

## The portfolio inherits the bearing

When a mortal performs an act that requires a specific internal architecture, and the mortal subsequently undergoes Apotheosis, the resulting **divine portfolio is shaped by the architecture of the bearing, not by the act's external form**.

Velveth is not the goddess of *poisoning* or *political assassination*. She is the goddess of **betrayal, love, and duty** held inseparably — the precise internal architecture Kathira had to construct to perform the act without being destroyed by it. The portfolio inherits the internal structure of the act, not its external category.

This is why divine portfolios are often *odd-shaped triplets or composites* rather than clean single-domain categories. When designing the place of a new Ascended in canon, the question to ask is: *what internal architecture did the mortal have to hold to do what they did? — that, expanded, becomes the portfolio.*

## Avatars are impossible in Eadras

A god in Eadras cannot manifest a true **Avatar** — a direct partial divine presence carrying full reality-weight, of the kind that is routine across many other Mythoi in the [[omniverse|Omniverse]]. Manifesting one in Eadras would risk **cracking the narrative foundation of the world**. The Mythos is too cosmologically wounded — observer-hungry from the [[observer-and-naming|Primordial Sacrifice]], scarred by the [[arathi-cataclysm|Worldbreaker-wound]], weakened by the [[chaos-crusades|Chaos Crusades]], the [[demon-war|Demon War]], and the [[anti-magic-crusade|Anti-Magic Crusade]] — to bear that weight without structural failure.

Every god in Eadras therefore operates through the [[icons|Icon]]-and-[[icons#Simulacra|Simulacrum]] mediation. The mediation is **not chosen by any god; it is required by the Mythos's condition**.

This is the cosmological floor beneath the [[doctrine-of-unheld-hand|Doctrine of the Unheld Hand]]: the gods do not merely *choose* not to manifest fully in Eadras — they *cannot*, structurally.

## The same framework applies to Elder Gods

Although the term *Apotheosis* refers specifically to the transition of mortals into gods, the resulting **Icon-and-Simulacrum framework** is identical for Elder Gods. Elder Gods were not born from mortal Apotheoses; their position in the Eadras pantheon was established at cosmological foundation, by [[primordial-duo|Evye and Fryr]]'s shaping. But every Elder God in Eadras likewise has one Icon on the Prime Material and one or more Simulacra in the spirit realms. The *mediation-mechanism* is the same. What distinguishes the two categories is *origin* and *the manner in which their divine realms came to exist*.

## Documented Ascended Mortal Gods

Each of the following ascended while living, by their own unique mechanism, and now operates through the standard Icon-and-Simulacrum framework. None has fully described their Apotheosis to mortals or to each other.

- [[baldaran|Baldaran]] — Third Age, the [[tome-of-baldaran|Tome's]] custodian
- [[aramet|Aramet]] — [[elfen|Elfen]], Lesser Goddess of Hidden Things
- [[ixthal|Ixthal]] — [[arathi|Arathi]], one of the [[three-last-lights|Three Last Lights]] (with his peers **Auralis** and **Thamior**, also Ascended)
- [[imma|Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing]] — [[beast-folk|Beast-folk]], modern era
- [[velveth|Velveth]] — [[velrathi|Velrathi]], during her Veyr'Keth life after the Banishment
- [[inki|Inki]] — Gnomish, modern Ascended God of invention
- [[din-shadar|Din'shadar]] — [[dwarves|Shada'din]], Ascended God of mining
- **Korvath the Brave** — First Tarran Ascended God
- **Vexara the Boneweaver** — First Velrathi Ascended Goddess
- **Duana the Priestess** and **Elaran Dawnblade** — Human Goddesses
- **Nirya the Confused**, **Lirael the Whisperwind** — Elfen Goddesses
- **Starwyn, Faylune, Valenor** — the Three [[unicorns|Unicorn]] Gods
- The **[[nine-embers-of-renewal|Nine Embers of Renewal]]** — every original Dragon progenitor (three publicly named: Cindirith, Davian, Koruss)

[[xarvion|Xar'vion]] is a special case — Ascended through a path the gods have judged illegitimate; his status is contested. **Tarloth the Cruel** is also special — Ascended by forcing the path through the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy|Spear of Crystallized Entropy]] after the legitimate path was denied her.

[[kudur-etir|Kudur-eṭir]] (Ssar'veth) — the original Spear-forced Ascended — is the unique exception to the Icon-and-Simulacrum framework's destruction rule. See [[icons#Total Icon Binding]].

## Open canon

- Are there **similarities** across documented Apotheoses that would emerge if a mythographer compiled the scattered fragments? The mythographer would have to be unusually careful.
- Whether the silence about the mechanism is **enforced** (by Decree or instinct) or **natural** (the experience produces no transmissible record). Both readings are present in scholarly tradition.
- How **tenure** ends for an Ascended Mortal God. None has yet ended in canonical record; the question is open.
- Whether the **post-Banishment Velrathi pantheon's Icons** (Velveth, Vexara, and others) are all maintained on the Prime Material of Eadras despite the people's exile, and by what mechanism — open canon.
