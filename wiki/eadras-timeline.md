# Eadras Timeline

**Summary**: The canonical chronology of Eadras under the user-locked 10-million-year timeline (2026-05-01). The Cataclysm anchors the system at **10,000,000 years before present**; everything else is dated forward from it or backward from the present day.

**Sources**: User direction 2026-05-01; map explanation.txt; Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt; corrected against established canon dates (Demon War, Anti-Magic Crusade, humanoid emergence); user direction 2026-05-04 (revert unicorn dating to ~6mybp BP on Eldravath).

**Last updated**: 2026-05-06

---

## Master timeline (years before present)

| Years BP | Event |
|---|---|
| **10,000,000** | **Arathi Cataclysm.** [[kudur-etir\|Kudur-eá¹­ir]] holds the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy\|Spear]] on God's Day, ascends to Unrestricted Elder God, is driven mad by [[dao\|Dao]]'s curse, and calls the [[worldender\|Worldbreaker]] down on [[altea\|Altea]]. The [[altean-highlands\|Altean Highlands]] sunder; plate tectonics ignites; ~60% of all life dies; the Arathi civilization is annihilated. |
| 10,000,000 → 9,000,000 | First million years of recovery: still-blazing Worldbreaker Scar, fractured crust, volatile climate. Three proto-continents stabilize from the sundered Altean Highlands ([[avalon\|Avalon]], [[damedes\|Damedes]], [[culethor\|Culethor]]). Two pre-Cataclysm highlands ([[therion\|Therion]], [[aurettos\|Aurettos]]) survive as additional continents. See [[eadras-tectonic-history#1mya post-Cataclysm\|1mya snapshot]]. |
| **8,000,000** | **First dragons awaken.** [[ixthal\|Ixthal]] secretly amplifies a natural Source surge into the **First Great Flare**; nine giant reptile eggs in different environments transform into the nine progenitor dragons. See [[dragons]] and [[ixthal#The Forbidden Kindling]]. The Worldbreaker Scar is now the **Buried Worldbreaker Scar**, cooling and sinking. See [[eadras-tectonic-history#2mya post-Cataclysm\|2mya snapshot]]. |
| 8,000,000 → 6,000,000 | Dragons spread across the recovering world. Continents continue to drift, then begin colliding. By 7mya BP the Scar is a deep stable oceanic rift; the three continents are taking on more recognizable shapes. See [[eadras-tectonic-history#3mya post-Cataclysm\|3mya snapshot]]. |
| **~6,000,000** | **Eldravath supercontinent forms.** Plate tectonics drives the continents back together into the supercontinent **[[eldravath\|Eldravath]]**. The Worldbreaker Scar still glows at the heart of its Central Rift Ocean. See [[eadras-tectonic-history#4mya post-Cataclysm — Eldravath\|4mya snapshot]]. |
| **~6,000,000** | **First Chaos Crusade.** [[chaos\|Chaos]] breaches the [[mythos\|Mythos]] through Kudur-eá¹­ir's First Breach. One-third of the Prime Material is consumed; both reflective planes are devastated. [[dragons\|Dragons]] and the Gods repel it; the Gods seal the breach. See [[chaos-crusades#The First Chaos Crusade]]. |
| **~6,000,000** | **First unicorns awaken on Eldravath; Ratharil founded.** Ixthal causes the **Silver Plains Flare** — a diffuse harmonic surge that manifests an entire herd at once across plains-dwelling proto-equines on the supercontinent. Eldravath's broadly stable, interconnected ecosystems shape unicorns as inherently herd-oriented and system-attuned creatures aligned with balance and continuity. Around the same horizon, dragons raise the city of **[[ratharil\|Ratharil]]** as their neutral capital. See [[unicorns]]. (User direction 2026-05-04, reverting the 2026-05-01 afternoon date of ~4mybp.) |
| **~6 mybp − 5,000 to 10,000 yr** | **[[unicorn-plains#The Unicorn Plains War\|Unicorn Plains War]] begins.** Roughly 5,000-10,000 years after unicorn emergence, during a draconic civilizational collapse, dragon clans discover the [[unicorn-plains\|Unicorn Plains]] and begin hunting unicorns as prey. Unicorns hide first, then — when extinction becomes a real threat — the [[unicorns#The Chorus\|Chorus]] makes a cold calculated decision to strike while dragons are at their weakest. The Wars are short — only a few thousand years total. Dragons are decisively driven from the plains, and the **Compact of Horn and Flame** norm ends hostilities (do not hunt the plains). (User direction 2026-05-01; *The Great Exchange.txt*.) |
| **~5,000,000** | **Breaking of Eldravath.** Active coastal rifting tears the supercontinent apart; modern continental geography begins to take shape. By this point unicorns are already an established species on the world. (source: Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt) |
| ~5 mybp → 2 mybp | Long dragon-unicorn coexistence under the Compact, on the post-Eldravath fracturing continents. See [[dragons#The Unicorns and Cultural Exchange]] and [[unicorns#The Unicorn-Dragon War and Peace]]. |
| **~2,000,000** | **Second Chaos Crusade.** Chaos breaches the same aperture again, taking the imitated forms of dragons and unicorns. [[dragons\|Dragons]], [[unicorns\|Unicorns]], and the Gods respond together; damage is contained at continental scale. See [[chaos-crusades#The Second Chaos Crusade]]. |
| 2,000,000 → 39,000 | Long quiet era. Continents continue to drift toward modern positions. |
| **~19,000** *(derived)* | **The [[great-exchange\|Great Exchange]].** Centuries-long mutual teaching between young dragons (at the edges of their fractured society) and young unicorns (experimenting with stepping outside the [[unicorns#The Chorus\|Chorus]]). Unicorns teach dragons psionics (telepathy/telekinesis/psychic combat/psychic shielding), cooperation without hierarchy, and the existence of other sapient species (~20,000 years old at the time of the Exchange). Dragons teach unicorns individuality and aggressive applications of [[eldritch\|Eldritch]] magic. The Exchange becomes the foundation of the [[ratharil#Council of 99 Eldest\|Council of Ninety-Nine]], the **Treaty of Ratharil** population cap at 999,999, and the cultural-legacy phrases *Walking the Dragon Path* and *Singing with the Unicorn Voice*. **Dating note:** the ~19,000 BP horizon is derived from the source's "20,000 years of other sapient species" clue against humanoid emergence at 39,000 BP — derived, not asserted by source; subject to curator confirmation. (source: The Great Exchange.txt) |
| ~present | Sea'tua are created by the [[elfen\|Elfen]] roughly ten million years after the Cataclysm. Under the 10my system, "ten million years after the Cataclysm" is essentially the present horizon (10my − ~30,000 years rounds to 10my at this resolution), placing Sea'tua creation shortly before or around humanoid emergence. (User clarification 2026-05-01.) |
| **~50,000** | **[[dao\|Dao]] rescues [[the-three\|The Three]]** from the Witchfinder General Trials on a distant world and brings them to Eadras at this horizon. He forges the **[[runeswords\|Runeswords]]** and the Hall of Kings soon after, with the children as witnesses. (Dao authored the rules of causality the Mythos runs on and can ignore them at will, so the Runeswords have always existed in the Mythos *and* were forged at ~50,000 BP — both simultaneously true, no contradiction.) The Three then grow up over the next ~11,000 years under Dao's patient guidance. (User direction 2026-05-06.) |
| **39,000** | **Humanoid races emerge.** [[the-three\|The Three]] (now grown) create the progenitor races ([[elfen\|Elfen]], [[velrathi\|Velrathi]], [[tarans\|Taran]]) — the only race-creation-from-scratch act ever performed by anyone other than Dao. **Melania creates the Tarran, the two Tarran [[runeswords\|Runeswords]] (Brightthorn and Darkthorn), and the [[firethorn-throne\|Firethorn Throne]] simultaneously** as a single act of making (user direction 2026-05-01). The progenitors then begin engineering their own descendants via in-utero modification (see [[peoples-of-eadras#How the engineering worked]]). The full Demon War / Anti-Magic Crusade / modern era plays out within this 39,000-year window. |
| **18,000** | **Beginning of the [[firethorn-empire\|Firethorn Empire]].** [[tarans\|Taran]] declare dominion over all other races; the worldwide Tarran empire is founded. (source: Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt) |
| **15,000** | **Peak of the Firethorn Empire.** Imperial apex; thereafter the empire begins its long Rome-style slide into decadence and ennui that will continue for the next five thousand years. (source: Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt) |
| 10,000 | **Dissolution of the Firethorn Empire and the Great Exile.** [[xarvion\|Xar'vion]] ascends; [[demon-war\|Demon War]] begins; [[lirra\|Lirra]] creates the Weave; [[battle-of-the-broken-conclaves\|Final Wall]] / [[great-banishment\|Great Banishment]] of the majority of the Three Elder Races to [[gaia\|Gaia]] (Prime + both Mirror Planes); some enclaves remain on Eadras. The [[divine-concordat\|Divine Concordat]] is honoured in full. |
| **10,000 — 9,000** | **The [[anti-magic-crusade\|Anti-Magic Crusade]]** in the decades immediately preceding the previous [[grand-conjunction\|Grand Conjunction]]. Preceded by the hundred-year [[century-of-sorrows\|Century of Sorrows]] engineered by the [[black-hand\|Black Hand]] as Spear-search diversion. Three [[windspeakers\|Windspeakers]] killed; six Towers of Lirra destroyed; [[thousand-year-dimming\|Thousand-Year Dimming]] begins. Halted by direct divine [[icons\|Icon]]-Habitation confrontation; the **Silent Century** strips participating priesthoods of divine power for 100 years; the **[[knights-of-lirra\|Knights of Lirra]]** are founded. (User direction 2026-05-15 replacement-pass.) |
| ~9,000 | Silent Century concludes; divine power restored to participating priesthoods under the new dual-source structure. |
| 9,000 — 8,000 | [[thousand-year-dimming\|Thousand-Year Dimming]] continues; magical potency reduced below the Fifth Circle. |
| ~8,000 | Thousand-Year Dimming ends; long magical recovery begins (continuing through the present in muted form, given the three permanently vacant Windspeaker anchors). |
| Present | Modern era. The next [[grand-conjunction\|Grand Conjunction]] approaches. |

## Notes

- The First Chaos Crusade and the Silver Plains Flare are both at ~6 mybp on Eldravath, though the First Crusade is defended by dragons and Gods alone (unicorns are too newly-manifested at the Crusade horizon to participate).
- The Sea'tua dating ("created roughly ten million years after the Cataclysm") on [[arathalassic-sea]] and [[peoples-of-eadras]] is consistent with the 10my system: at million-year resolution, 10my-after-Cataclysm rounds to approximately the present horizon, placing Sea'tua creation shortly before or around the time of humanoid emergence.
