# Heart of the Goddess

> **DEPRECATED 2026-05-14.** This page is retained as a slug-target so existing wikilinks resolve. The Heart of the Goddess was a Weave-space concept from the pre-2026-05-09 Sorcerer / Weave magic system, which was superseded by the 2026-05-09 rework. The Weave itself is **candidate-for-removal** in the planned make-it-mine pass. The page narrative was removed on 2026-05-14 as part of the *ALL deprecated content* cleanup.

**Summary**: The Heart of the Goddess was a non-physical space within the Weave where [[lirra|Lirra]] communed with sorcerers, in the pre-rework canon. The space and its function are no longer canonical.

**Sources**: Removed; see [[log]] for the original narrative under its 2026-04-30 ingest.
