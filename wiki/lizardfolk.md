# Lizardfolk

**Summary**: The middle-sized, most distinctly reptilian of the three "Lizard People" subtypes within the [[dragonfolk|dragonfolk]] family. Lizardfolk were created by [[dragons|dragons]] in response to the proliferation of humanoid races and tend toward more primal temperaments than their kobold or dragonborn cousins.

**Sources**: Dragons.txt, Peoples of Eadras.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-04-30

---

## Anatomy and Society

Detailed lizardfolk anatomy and culture are not yet documented in canon. They are listed as a present-day race in modern Eadras intended to appear as a playable [[dragonfolk|dragonfolk]] subtype.

## Relations to Dragons

Lizardfolk are draconic creations and are not counted in the [[runeswords|Treaty of Ratharil]] cap on the dragon population. The cultural distance between modern reclusive dragons and the comparatively earthy lizardfolk is wide, but the shared origin remains a quiet line of kinship.
