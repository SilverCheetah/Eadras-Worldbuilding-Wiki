# Lizardfolk

**Summary**: The **body** of the [[draconic-servitor-races|draconic servitor races]] — the labor-and-defense caste engineered by [[dragons|dragons]] ~30,000 BP for physical strength and lair-defense. Where [[kobolds|kobolds]] are the mind and mouth, lizardfolk are the muscle of the order. They descend from no progenitor; they are dragon-made.

**Sources**: Dragons.txt; Peoples of Eadras.txt; draconic-servitor-races.txt (2026-06-02 ingest).

**Last updated**: 2026-06-02

---

## Origin

Lizardfolk are one of the four [[draconic-servitor-races|draconic servitor races]] — reptilian humanoids reshaped from dragon-derived flesh and made ontologically real by bargain with the ascended dragon-gods at approximately **30,000 BP** (see [[draconic-servitor-races#Creation — how dragons made them|the creation account]]). They were **not** engineered by any of [[the-three|the Three]]'s progenitor races; they are dragon-made.

## Role — the body of the order

In the four-caste design (mind / body / will / champion), lizardfolk are the **body**: labor, lair-defense, and physical strength. They are the most physically formidable of the working castes, raised around dragon-lands as the muscle that builds, hauls, and defends.

> [!note] Stub
> The lizardfolk's culture and anatomy are deliberately thin in current canon — flagged for development at the depth the [[snake-folk|snake folk]] received. Their cold-blooded habits have long associated them with wetland and tropical territory, where they sometimes share range with **Black** dragons.

## Binding — the Mandate

Like the other two working castes ([[kobolds|kobolds]] and [[snake-folk|snake folk]]), lizardfolk are bound by the unbreakable **[[draconic-servitor-races#The Mandate|divine Mandate]]**: bound to purpose, capped in number. The cap is **pooled** across all three castes (each living dragon anchors up to 100 draconic-race beings *total*) and gated at **egg-quickening**; over-cap eggs wait, viable and patient, until capacity frees. This Mandate is a **separate instrument** from the [[ratharil|Treaty of Ratharil]] (the breakable ~999,999 cap on *dragon* numbers).

## Baseline condition

Lizardfolk live, overwhelmingly, **harmoniously in their designed role** in integrated communities around dragon lairs and at [[ratharil|Ratharil]]. Loyal members of any working caste may, rarely, be elevated to the individually-forged [[dragonborn|dragonborn]] champions; a lizardfolk-sourced dragonborn tends to show its origin as greater might beneath the dragon-overlay.
