# Dreadharrow

**Summary**: Dreadharrow is the capital city of [[nyxar]] — the lowest plane of the [[eadras-cosmology|Eadras Mythos]] — built from the architecture of every era and constructed in part from soul metal.

## Overview

Dreadharrow is the capital of [[nyxar]], the lowest plane inside the Eadras Mythos — the analogue to Hell. Like its counterpart [[solarion]] in [[celestia]], Dreadharrow contains architecture from every era of mortal history. However, many of its buildings are constructed from **soul metal** — a material unique to [[nyxar]], presumably formed from or connected to the souls of the dead.
