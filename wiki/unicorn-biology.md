# Unicorn Biology

**Summary**: The bodily architecture beneath [[unicorns|unicorn]] culture. Unicorns run on **two separate fuel lines**: forage runs the body; raw **Sourceshine** runs the horn and the mind. The horn is simultaneously the species' **only manipulator** (horn-projected telekinesis), its **telepathy organ**, and its **Source-intake organ** — to lose it is to be handless, voiceless-to-kind, and starving, at once. Cognition is physically a **shared telepathic field**: the herd thinks; a body is a sense-organ and limb of that thinking. A herd-mind requires a **quorum of bodies** (~8 physical; 10 observed) to form at all. Foals are born ordinary animals and **awaken only when first bathed in Sourceshine**. The species' true demographic chokepoint is not breeding rate but **mares**. Companion page to [[unicorns]] (culture, history, politics) and [[collection-fields|Collection Fields]] (the world-physics beneath the quorum).

## The two fuel lines

Unicorn metabolism is **dual**, with two separate and independent fuel lines:

- **Grass and leaves run the body** — muscle, heat, healing, foaling. In this respect a unicorn is what it appears to be: a large plains herbivore.
- **Raw Sourceshine runs the horn** — thought, telekinesis, telepathy, and the shared field that hosts the herd-mind. No amount of forage substitutes for it.

The split is origin-consistent: unicorns are ordinary proto-equines **altered by a [[source-surges|sourceflare]]** (the Silver Plains Flare — see [[unicorns#Origin]]). The Source is an *added* engine bolted onto an animal that already worked. The animal layer never stopped working; the Source layer is what makes the animal a unicorn.

Because the fuel lines are independent, they **fail independently** — the foundation of the starvation states described below.

### Unicorns use raw Sourceshine — and are Eldritch-blind

Unicorns are the **only beings on Eadras who feed on raw Sourceshine directly**. The corollary is absolute: **unicorns cannot sense or use [[eldritch|Eldritch]] at all**. Where the [[arathi|Arathi]] built [[crystalmancy|crystal arrays]] to transform Sourceshine into stable Eldritch, and every later mortal tradition works in the reflected paradigm, unicorns simply *drink the light upstream of all of it*. Eldritch — the entire reflected economy of mortal magic — is invisible to them, as Sourceshine is intangible to nearly everyone else.

### Sourceshine propagation

Sourceshine passes through everything — cloud, storm, canopy, flesh, stone walls. It is weather-proof. The **only thing that blocks or reflects it is bedrock** — the deep mantle layer of the world's body. The consequences:

- Source is **ambient everywhere above ground**. On the open surface, a unicorn is always feeding.
- Only **sky-sealed places** are Source-poor: deep underground workings, below-bedrock spaces, magically sealed vaults.
- **Plains-living is metabolic necessity, not preference.** Open sky is the feeding ground. Forest herds, mountain herds, cave-adjacent herds are all paying a Source-cost the plains herds do not pay.

## The horn

The horn is intensely magical and psionic, grows continuously throughout life, and its **length gates both the range and the power** of a unicorn's magic and psionics. It is three organs in one:

1. **The hand.** Unicorns have no manipulative appendages — a horse's body, end to end. Horn-projected **telekinesis is their only manipulator**. Every gate opened, every wound dressed, every object carried in the species' whole history has been moved by horn.
2. **The voice.** The horn is the telepathy organ — the body's connection into the shared field, and its channel to any mind outside it.
3. **The mouth that feeds.** The horn is the Source-intake organ. The body's second fuel line runs through it.

**"Crippled" therefore means something specific and total** in unicorn terms: a hornless unicorn is handless, voiceless to its own kind, and unable to feed on Source — all at once. (Violently removed horns also decay magically or become dangerous; see [[unicorns#Horns]].)

The horn's cost is structural: the one manipulator is **mounted exposed on the brow, unarmored, irreplaceable** — and the more capable it grows, the more it advertises itself as the thing to take. Dexterity that can be amputated in a stroke.

### Horn-length as stored autonomy

A body **stores Source against its horn length, like a battery**. Deprived of open sky, a unicorn runs on the stored charge — and reverts to animal nature when the charge runs out, on a timer set by horn length. The young run dark quickly; **elders run dark far longer**. Elder-length horn is therefore a *prerequisite* for sky-poor missions — deep vaults, deep wood, underground passages. (What the reversion looks and feels like from inside, and how it reverses on re-exposure, is open canon.)

## The shared field

Unicorn cognition is **physically a shared telepathic field, not a set of discrete minds**. The HERD thinks; an individual body is a sense-organ and limb of that thinking, not a thinker. There is no native "I." What mortals meet as a unicorn's *self* is a temporary structure the herd has issued (see [[unicorns#The extruded I|the extruded I]]); "rejoining the we" is the dissolution of that temporary structure.

### The quorum floor

A herd-mind cannot form below a **quorum of bodies**:

- **Physical floor: ~8 bodies.** Below it, no herd-mind forms — the overlapping [[collection-fields|collection fields]] of the member bodies cannot merge into one self-sustaining field large enough to host a mind.
- **Observed floor: 10 bodies.** Unicorns refuse to approach the physical floor; the two-body margin is sacred. No healthy herd lets itself near 8.

Even in the pre-dragon deep past, herds split into divisions but **never below quorum**. The theology built on this floor — the [[unicorns#The Great Silence|Great Silence]] — is treated on the main page; the floor itself is physics.

## Awakening

A foal is **born an ordinary, un-awakened animal**. It cannot awaken into a unicorn until it is first **bathed in Sourceshine** — every birth re-enacting the sourceflare origin in miniature. On the surface this is near-automatic: Source is ambient, and the foal awakens within moments of birth. A foal born in a Source-sealed place — below bedrock, inside a sealed vault — **stays a horse**.

Whether that un-awakening is reversible if the foal later reaches Source, or whether the window closes, is open canon.

## Breeding and the mare chokepoint

Unicorns breed **slower than horses** — one foal at a time — but mares are **near-perpetually pregnant**, so across a herd's life the raw birth rate is not the limiting factor. Awakening on the surface is near-free. The real demographic chokepoint is **mares**.

Attrition — the Dragon Path, the [[circling-of-the-returned|Circling's]] refusals, exile, Source-starvation, war — falls on bodies indiscriminately. But only mares replace the herd. A run of mare-losses threatens reproduction independent of how many bodies remain. A herd therefore lives under **two failure thresholds that move independently**:

- **The thinking floor** — the quorum (~8/10 bodies). Below it, the mind goes out.
- **The breeding floor** — enough mares. Below it, the herd is reproductively doomed *no matter how many bodies it still has*.

A herd can be comfortably above quorum and already dying. The cultural answer to this — **fuse-and-split**, the single mechanism that is simultaneously the species' demographic safety valve and its memory-distribution network — is treated at [[unicorns#Fuse-and-split|Unicorns — Fuse-and-split]].

## Starvation states

Because the fuel lines fail independently, unicorn starvation comes in two unlike kinds:

- **Body-starvation** is ordinary animal starvation — forage failure, winterkill. Tragic and familiar.
- **Source-starvation** is the alien one. A Source-starved herd is *not dead*. The bodies graze on — warm, alive, healthy — while the herd-mind dims out above them. What remains is a field of grazing horses where a mind used to be. This is the **decapitated-herd state**: the visible, physical face of the [[unicorns#The Great Silence|Great Silence]], the thing unicorn theology holds as worse than death.

## Dragon-crafted storage crystals

Since the era opened by the [[great-exchange|Great Exchange]] relationship, unicorns have carried **dragon-crafted Sourceshine-storage crystals** — canteens for the second fuel line, extending a body's reach past its horn-length timer.

The makers matter. Unicorns could never craft these: they have no hands beyond the horn, and the crystal-shaping craft itself is Eldritch-capable work — a paradigm unicorns are blind to. **[[dragons|Dragons]] handle both Source and Eldritch, have artisans, and recreated the crystal-storage craft**; the craft is Eldritch-capable, the *product* is Source-charged. The deep-ranging Long Strayer, the vault-adjacent emissary, and the [[demonfall-marches|Demonfall]] scout are survivable roles **only because of dragon crystalwork**. Unicorn individuality is owed to dragons twice over — psychologically (the Dragon Path) and logistically (the canteen).

## Memory biology

Unicorns **remember consequence perfectly and fact not at all** — by construction, not by failure. The species was built from herd herbivores whose survival ran on deep pattern-recognition: where the predator is, which ground is dangerous, what threat *feels* like. Herd-memory accordingly keeps wound-shapes, danger-patterns, and emotional truth across millions of years, and lets go of names, dates, schematics, and linear history.

It is not a failed archive. It was never built to be an archive. It is a **danger-map spanning deep time** — the biological bedrock under [[unicorn-wound-keeping|Wound-Keeping]] (a prey brain stores *that place, that feeling, DON'T* — not reusable technique) and under the species' [[unicorns#The unicorn mythic voice — sorrowful, watchful, alarmed|canary voice]] (the best threat-detector and worst historian in the setting).

## Open canon

- The **exact quorum number** — physical floor ~8 is approximate; the observed floor of 10 is confirmed.
- Whether a Source-sealed foal's un-awakening is **reversible** on later Source exposure, or whether the window closes.
- What **Source-starvation reversion** looks and feels like from inside, and whether/how it reverses on re-exposure.
- Whether herd-mind **stored memory** (the five layers) and the **sung-resonance memory** of the [[unicorns#The Silver Runs|Silver Runs]] are one system in two modes or two distinct systems.
