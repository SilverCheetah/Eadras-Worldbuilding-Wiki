## Origin

Culethor is one of the **three continents that formed from the sundered [[altean-highlands|Altean Highlands]]** when the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]] struck and ignited plate tectonics. Together with [[avalon|Avalon]] (north) and [[damedes|Damedes]] (southwest), Culethor makes up the Altean-derived continental triad. The fragmentation began at the moment of impact (10mya BP); the named, distinct continent of Culethor stabilized within the first million years (see [[eadras-tectonic-history#1mya post-Cataclysm]]).

Culethor's pre-sundering identity was as the southeastern portion of the Altean Highlands. Specific pre-Cataclysm features that mapped onto Culethor's modern footprint are undefined.
