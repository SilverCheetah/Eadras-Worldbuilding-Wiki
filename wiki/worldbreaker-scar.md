# Worldbreaker Scar

**Summary**: The Worldbreaker Scar is the persistent geological wound at the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]]'s impact site — the same crustal point where [[kudur-etir|Kudur-eṭir]] punched his cosmic aperture and where [[altea|Altea]] once stood. It has burned, buried, cooled, and rifted across [[eadras-tectonic-history|millions of years of plate tectonics]] without losing its identity, and it is the eventual surface point at which the [[arathyn|Arathyn]] continent rises. Since the [[demon-war|Demon War]] ~10,000 years ago, **the Scar is the active site of the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy|Spear of Crystallized Entropy's]] impalement-prison** — the gods' fourth-attempt containment, which rammed the alien artifact back into the same Wound through which the Worldbreaker came in. The Scar is therefore not merely a remnant of past trauma; it is the **active site of the world's most dangerous ongoing cosmological situation**.

## What it is

The Worldbreaker Scar is **not the Worldbreaker** (the alien Falling Star itself — see [[worldender]]) and **not Arathyn** (the continent that eventually grew from sustained LIP extrusion at the impact center — see [[arathyn]]). It is the **persistent crustal feature** at the impact point: a wound, a buried scar, a deep ocean rift, a glowing rift-center, depending on how many million years post-Cataclysm one is observing.

It coincides with three other things at that same point:
- The location where [[altea|Altea]] once stood (now annihilated).
- The cosmic **aperture Kudur-eṭir drilled** during the forging of the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy|Spear]] — the breach the [[neverborn|Neverborn]] later used to open both [[chaos-crusades|Chaos Crusades]].
- The eventual **surface basement** of [[arathyn|Arathyn]] once Large Igneous Province extrusion finishes.

These are not unrelated coincidences. The Worldbreaker fell along Kudur-eá¹­ir's aperture because Kudur-eá¹­ir aimed it through his own cosmic conduit; the Scar marks where that conduit terminates in the planetary crust.

## Magical and metaphysical significance

The Scar is **not just a geological feature**. Several lines of canon converge here:

- **Ley distortion** — even when buried, the Scar distorts ocean currents and ley lines.
- **Conduit context** — the seven world-piercing Eldritch Conduits did not move when plate tectonics reorganized the surface (the conduits are cosmologically anchored — see [[verdant-scar#The shifted surface and the antipode]]). The Scar's position relative to the conduits is a fixed cosmic-geometry datum, even as the surface drifts above it.
- **Aperture proximity** — Kudur-eṭir's drilled aperture into raw [[chaos|Chaos]] terminates at this point in the crust. The seal at the end of each [[chaos-crusades|Chaos Crusade]] was placed here. The current seal stands, but its location has been physically *under* the Scar's evolving surface signature for millions of years.
- **[[worldender|Worldender]] fragment concentration** — most surviving Worldender fragments are buried in the Scar's deep crust or along the LIP plume that produced Arathyn. The Scar is the densest single deposit of alien-origin material on the planet.
- **The Spear's impalement** — since 10,000 BP, the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy|Spear of Crystallized Entropy]] has been **impaled directly into the original First Breach Wound** at the conceptual heart of the Scar. This is the gods' [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy#hiding-4-shove-it-back-down-the-hole-after-xarvion|fourth-attempt containment]]. The act of impalement triggered the renewed volcanism that forced [[arathyn|Arathyn]] into its modern shape; the continent above is the visible scar tissue grown over the impalement. The Spear's ongoing presence in the Wound is the **single most dangerous active cosmological situation on Eadras**, and the [[#Reaching the Scar|approach to the Scar]] is dominated by it.

## Practical implications for canon

- **The Arathyn continent's basement is the Scar.** Anyone deep-mining Arathyn is excavating Scar substrate. Arathyn-origin orichalcum, magitek artifacts, and Worldender-fragment finds all share this deep root.
- **The Scar is still active.** The Spear is impaled into it; the Wound has not closed; the post-Demon-War volcanic phase that forced Arathyn into its modern shape is recent on geological time and the continent is still growing. Modern Eadras has detectable Scar resonance and it shapes the entire interior of Arathyn.
- **Approaching the Scar is dangerous.** Each previous chaos breach used the same conduit-aperture; if a third breach comes, this is where it appears. The deep interior of [[arathyn|Arathyn]] is, as a working assumption, **progressively reality-frayed** as one approaches the Spear's impalement site at the conceptual center.
- **It is a candidate pilgrimage site** — for [[ixthal|Ixthal]]-followers (the place where the Last Arathi survived), for those mourning Altea, and for those who study the [[worldender|Worldender]]. Whether any settled or active pilgrimage tradition exists is undefined.

### Reaching the Scar

The obstacles to reaching the impalement site at the Scar's conceptual center are **not clever defenses the gods built**. They are the **consequences of the gods' desperation**. As any approach to the inner Scar progresses:

- **Reality itself begins to come apart.** Causality frays. Time moves unevenly. Memory degrades. Identity slips. The closer one gets to the artifact, the more wrong every dimension of normal experience becomes.
- **The land is the guardian.** No constructed defense is needed; the continent of [[arathyn|Arathyn]] itself, the scar tissue of the world's wound, *actively rejects the existence of intruders the closer they come to the center.*
- **Any guardian-being encountered along the way is a casualty by accident, not a guardian by design.** Beings caught in the fraying region around the Spear persist there in distorted form — not as guardians, but as further evidence of the wrongness.

This framing supersedes any prior treatment of the Spear's prison as a clever-defenses-and-bosses dungeon. The land is dying inward. Any party approaching the Spear is crawling into a mutilation. That is the experience. (See [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy#reaching-the-spear]].)

## Open canon

- **Modern surface state.** Whether the Scar is detectable today as a glowing point in some surface feature, an ocean rift, or a buried-but-resonant subterranean signature.
- **The seal's stability.** The current Chaos seal stands, but no canon discusses its visible state from the surface.
- **Whether the Scar's emergence into Arathyn is complete or still ongoing.** Canon places Arathyn's LIP buildup at 2-8 million years; under the 10my timeline, Arathyn must be in the upper end of that range or even still forming.
- **Names in the modern era.** "Worldbreaker Scar" is an out-of-universe map label. In-universe naming (Arathi era? modern dragons? unicorns? humanoid scholars?) is undefined.
