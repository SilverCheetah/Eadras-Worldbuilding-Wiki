# Shadowdark

**Summary**: The Shadowdark is the dark reflection of the Prime Material Plane within the [[eadras-cosmology|Eadras Mythos]], home to darkness-tainted spirits and the underdark of Eadras.

**Sources**: Shadowdark.txt, Spear of Crystallized Entropy.txt, Internal Cosmology of the Eadras Mythos.txt, More corrections.txt, Extra large grok chat 5-9-2026.txt, spear-containment-diagnosis-and-the-alien-artifact-principle.md (2026-05-13 ingest, third-hiding god-prison and reordered plane-corruption sequence).

**Last updated**: 2026-05-13

## Overview

The Shadowdark is a **complete reflection** of the entire Eadras Prime â€” including its planet, all four moons ([[gaia|Gaia]], [[daj|Daj]], [[shali|Shali]], [[shani|Shani]]), and every other Prime feature. It is shifted into a dark, negative-energy resonance influenced by [[nyxar|Nyxar]], and serves as the home of spirits tainted by darkness â€” including the restless dead. It contains what would be called the Underdark in other Mythoi.

### The Deep Shadowdark â€” near-infinite extent beyond the reflection

Like the [[feywild|Feywild]], the Shadowdark is **not bounded by the reflected geography**. The reflection of the Prime Material occupies one region of the Shadowdark, but the plane extends **near-infinitely** in every direction beyond that reflection (source: More corrections.txt). Beyond the Prime-reflected territory lies vast, indeterminate Shadowdark expanse â€” endless tunnels and labyrinths, regions of pure negative resonance, dark realms within dark realms, jurisdictions of beings whose existence has no Prime analogue.

The current working name for this near-infinite outer region is **the Deep Shadowdark** (provisional, captured 2026-05-09 in parallel with the matching [[feywild#The Deep Feywild â€” near-infinite extent beyond the reflection|Deep Feywild]] term). The plane names *Feywild* and *Shadowdark* are user-acknowledged D&D-coded placeholders likely to be renamed before publication; the Deep-Shadowdark label is held alongside them and may change.

This means the Shadowdark is **far larger than the Prime Material**, with the Prime-reflected portion serving as a kind of anchored shore from which the rest of the plane extends. The farther one travels from the Prime, the stranger and more dangerous things become.

## Dark Paths

The Shadowdark has a system of portals called **Dark Paths** that allow quick traversal of the nearly infinite plane. These paths are corrupt and dangerous to use.

## History: Twisted by the Spear

The [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy|Spear of Crystallized Entropy]] was once imprisoned in the Shadowdark (then called **Darkmirror**, the original dark reflection of the Prime Material). The Elder Gods used the deepest depths of the plane for the **third hiding** of the Spear â€” the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy#hiding-3-reverse-time-bubble-over-a-god-prison-in-the-deepest-shadowdark-after-darkhoof|reverse-time-bubble god-prison]] in which a volunteer [[dragons|dragon god]] was sealed with the Spear inside a single composite Icon, the prison wrapped in a reverse time bubble that accelerated subjective time vastly relative to the world outside.

Over the long centuries the Spear sat in the deepest Darkmirror, its presence corrupted the plane around it â€” **Darkmirror gradually transformed into the Shadowdark**, tunneling deep into the ground and creating the twisted mazes and chambers of dark energy that mark the plane today. This transformation was unintended in scope but anticipated in kind: the gods knew the Spear's prolonged presence corrupts whatever plane houses it (the same mechanism by which Brightmirror became the [[feywild|Feywild]] during the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy#hiding-2-time-displacement-in-the-feywild-after-tarloth|second hiding]]), and they accepted the corruption-cost as the price of containment. The corruption took millions of years; the plane we know today is the result.

The Spear is **no longer in the Shadowdark.** [[xarvion|Xar'vion]] broke into the deepest depths and killed the volunteer dragon god to take the Spear; in the wake of the [[demon-war|Demon War]] the gods committed the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy#hiding-4-shove-it-back-down-the-hole-after-xarvion|fourth-and-final hiding]] elsewhere. But the Shadowdark's plane-character â€” the cold, the lightlessness, the predatory wrongness of its deeper layers â€” is the lingering residue of the millennia the Spear spent here.
