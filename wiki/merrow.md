## Origin

Merrow descend from the **Sea'tua**, the [[elfen|Elfen]]-engineered race created to oversee natural balance in the oceans. The Sea'tua were created roughly ten million years after the [[arathi-cataclysm|Arathi Cataclysm]] (source: Peoples of Eadras.txt). Merrow are one of the five Sea'tua-descended modern oceanic peoples; the other two Sea'tua descendant lines, [[kuo-toa|Kuo-toa]] and [[sahuagin|Sahuagin]], drifted dramatically further from the Sea'tua template.

## Anatomy

Merrow have **elongated, elegant bodies covered in living polyps and anemones** that function as clothing, tools, and mood/status displays. The polyps are not parasitic â€” they are cultivated symbiotes, raised on the body and groomed daily, and their color and arrangement signal social standing, mood, and current life-project to other Merrow at a glance. (source: Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt)

## Society

Merrow society revolves around **Growth Circles** â€” massive, multi-generational collaborative projects that produce living architectures across decades. A typical Growth Circle is several dozen to several hundred Merrow joined for the lifetime of a coral structure; participants come and go, but the structure persists.

They **sing entire coral megastructures and kelp cathedrals into existence**. Songs are layered, overlapping, and harmonic; the work of one voice shapes the polyp clusters in one substrate, while a second voice tunes a current, while a third holds a long bass note that aligns the whole structure to the season. The result is recognizable as architecture but never quite repeatable â€” every reef-city is unique.

Merrow speak in **layered, overlapping harmonies that sound to outsiders like an entire choir** even when only one Merrow is talking.

## Relations with the surface

Of the five modern oceanic races, Merrow are the **most open to surface trade**. They exchange **living coral art** and **current-tuned navigational shells** with surface ports â€” the latter highly prized by surface mariners as they encode local current information that improves significantly on dead instruments.

Trade with Merrow is patient. A negotiation with a Merrow Growth Circle may take a season; a deal closed in less than a tenday is treated by the Merrow as an unfinished sketch.

### The Orden alliance

Merrow hold a deep symbiotic alliance with the [[orden|Orden]], the seafaring clan-culture of modern Eadras. The Merrow:

- Assist Orden ships with **navigation, storm warnings, and sea-creature defense**
- **Rescue Orden sailors who go overboard**, taking them to underwater villages as honored guests, showing them their civilization (including underwater brawling, which Orden visitors particularly appreciate), and eventually returning them to the surface
- Honor the Orden's openness to all peoples by adopting many Orden customs themselves; Merrow regularly come ashore for Orden celebrations, moots, and diplomacy

Sailors who earn the Merrow's highest respect are named **Deep Friends** and given a **reusable charm that allows them to breathe underwater**. Among the Orden, Deep Friends are affectionately (and somewhat teasingly) called **Fish Friends**, and most go on to become valued advisors to coastal Jarls. The relationship is one of the few cross-race symbioses in the modern era that an outside observer can describe simply: *the Merrow rescue, the Orden remember.* (source: notes on various topics 2.txt)
