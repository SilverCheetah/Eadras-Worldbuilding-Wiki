# Halaran

**Summary**: Halaran is the **large eastern continent of the modern world** — where the **majority of Eadras's peoples live**. It is the fused product of two pole-crossing tectonic fragments (a northern fragment over the north pole, a southern fragment under the south pole) that welded together on the far side of the world, and so it spans an enormous latitude range from boreal north to warm-temperate south. A volcanic suture range runs east–west across its middle where the two fragments collided. Halaran holds [[ratharil|Ratharil]] in its glacier-scarred northern mountains, the [[demonfall-marches|Demonfall Marches]] and [[kharathen|Kharathen]] in its forested mid-west, the [[azure-basin|Azure Basin]] in its enclosed far-eastern bay, and the demographic bulk of the modern races across its temperate western heartland.

**Sources**: EADRAS WORLDBUILDING — MAP SESSION.txt (map-building working session, 2026-05-31); trading-cultures-halaran.txt + bold.txt (2026-06-03, five-culture trade network; Orden moved to the cold south; land caravan routes; climate bands); cross-references [[eadras-world-map]], [[eadras-tectonic-history]].

## Origin and structure

Halaran is a **fused continent**, assembled from two fragments of the second megacontinent that rifted apart around the [[arathyn|Worldbreaker Scar]] ~4 million years ago (see [[eadras-world-map#Tectonic-assembly-of-the-modern-arrangement|the world map's tectonic history]]):

- A **northern fragment** drifted north and **transited the north pole.**
- A **southern fragment** drifted south and **transited the south pole.**
- The two **merged on the far side of the world**, fusing into the single eastern continent.

Two consequences follow directly from this history and are written into Halaran's geology:

- **Relict glaciation.** Both fragments glaciated heavily during their pole crossings. The northern third of Halaran carries **relict glacial terrain** — fjords, U-valleys, glacial lakes, scoured bare rock — even where it now sits at milder latitudes. The land remembers the crossing.
- **The Collision Range.** Where the two fragments welded, the crust crumpled up into a **great east–west mountain range across the continent's middle** — a Himalaya-analogue suture, but **mantle-active and volcanic** (collision volcanism). It runs east–west and wraps the far-eastern bay.

Because it is the welded product of a boreal-north fragment and a pole-crossing southern fragment, Halaran **spans a wide latitude range** and contains nearly every climate band — from northern taiga and tundra, through a temperate green middle and a tropical equatorial belt, to a **cold far south** that brushes the south-polar ice. (The southern reaches run cold, not warm-temperate — see [[#The five trading cultures|the climate bands]] and [[orden|Orden's]] placement; user direction 2026-06-03.)

## Regions

### Northern Mountains

Cold, high, glacially scarred basalt highlands — the relict-glaciated northern fragment. This is the seat of **[[ratharil|Ratharil]]** (Rhtrakhadara, the City of Dragons, ~58°N) and the ancestral [[mitans|Shada'din]] deep-holds. Fjords, ice-vaults, scoured rock; the dragons' high basalt plateau sits here.

### The Collision Range

The volcanic east–west suture range across the continent's middle — **rich in mana-ore** from its mantle-active collision volcanism. This is the **[[mitans|Shada'din]] industrial heartland and the gnome forge-belt**: ore in the range, workshops at its feet. The range wraps the far-eastern bay.

### The Arid Steppe

In the **northern rain-shadow lee of the Collision Range**, prevailing westerlies drop their rain on the range and the northern lee stays dry — producing **grassland and steppe**. This is the **orc plains**: orc herds, clan-holds sited off the open grass, and the Great Gathering.

### The Forested Mid-West

The forest belt above the natural western bay. Here lie the **[[demonfall-marches|Demonfall Marches]]** (the Demon-War wound) and **[[kharathen|Kharathen]]** (the City of Unforgotten Names, at the western lip of the Marches). [[katharim|Katharim]] wandering caretaker circuits radiate from here; [[beast-folk|beast-folk]] inhabit the surrounding woods.

### The Temperate Heartland

The warm west coast and centre — the **demographic bulk of Halaran**: humans, halflings, mixed cities, and the conduit-trade world. This is where most of Eadras's modern peoples live.

### The Far-Eastern Bay — the Azure Basin

The enclosed far-eastern bay, ringed by the Collision Range and kept warm by enclosure: the **[[azure-basin|Azure Basin]]**, the Mediterranean-analogue mosaic of city-states (Venethar and the rest), with the [[amunorians|Amunorian]] diaspora banking houses concentrated here.

## Coastal climate

Halaran's coasts are shaped by the great ocean gyres (see [[eadras-world-map#Ocean-currents-and-gyres|the climate model]]):

| Coast | Temperature | Cause |
|---|---|---|
| **West coast** | **Warm** for its latitude — mild, wet | Warm limb of the northern (clockwise) gyre; the Demonfall/bay side and [[bold|Bold's]] coast |
| **Northeast coast** | **Cool** | Returning (equatorward) limb of the northern gyre |
| **Southern / southeast tail** | **Cool** — cooler than its latitude suggests | Cold limb of the southern (counterclockwise) gyre |
| **Azure Basin bay** | **Warm** regardless of offshore currents | Kept warm by enclosure |

The warm, wet west coast supports [[bold|Bold]] and the bay-side forests; the deep south runs cooler than a naïve latitude reading would predict.

## The five trading cultures

The eastern continent is shared by **five active trading cultures**, moving goods between them along two route-types: **sea routes** (coast to coast, polity to polity) and the **land caravan routes** — a **northern** and a **southern** pass through the central transverse [[#The Collision Range|Collision Range]]. The two passes are chokepoints: natural sites of toll-cities, fortress-towns, smuggling, and leverage. Whoever holds the north and south passes holds structural power over the overland half of the trade. (source: trading-cultures-halaran.txt, 2026-06-03)

1. **[[bold|Bold]] / the Bold Republic** — the **warm west coast**; Italian-influenced maritime republic, the biggest city in the world, conduit-city and melting-pot entrepôt. Restored after the [[bold#The Ismander Scar (~500 years ago)|Ismander]] episode.
2. **[[azure-basin|Azure Basin]]** — the **warm enclosed eastern bay**; a deliberately *non-Italian* Mediterranean mosaic (Greek / Levantine / Phoenician / North-African / Iberian / Balkan textures) of competing city-states. Suffered worst under Ismander, who took the majority of it.
3. **[[amunorians|Amunorian successor states]]** — the old desert heartland. The map-label still shows Amunor's historical borders, but present-day it is a patchwork of **squabbling successor states** on a thinly-populated heartland, while the [[amunorians|Amunorian diaspora]] banks from the Azure Basin. Amunor fell in the [[anti-magic-crusade|Anti-Magic Crusade]] era; Ismander later took half of the old territory. The fallen conduit-city of [[amunorians|Sarathis]] lies here.
4. **[[orden|Orden]]** — Norse-influenced Hanseatic trade-league culture of the **cold south** (~30–80°S), whose deep-south coasts brush the south-polar ice. Ismander took *parts* of Orden, but not its cold far-south reaches. Orden is the cold-**southern** counterpart to the cold-**northern** [[orcs|orc]] steppe.
5. **[[free-cities|Free Cities]]** — loosely-affiliated city-states with no single sovereign, in the **tropical/equatorial band** (the equator runs just south of [[arathyn|Arathyn]]) on the wet, fertile windward side of the mountains. A tropical Holy-Roman-Empire-*without-an-emperor*; high-value luxury goods; positioned in the mountain-and-polity gap as brokers of the land routes. Freedom won twice — from Amunor, and from Ismander.

### Climate bands (placement context)

The continent runs **cold-north → warm-equator → cold-south**: the cold arid [[orcs|orc]] steppe in the northern rain-shadow; a warm temperate green belt and the tropical [[free-cities|Free Cities]] near the equator; and the cold southern reaches of [[orden|Orden]] toward the south-polar ice. A separate **hot, subtropical desert** sits in the southern hemisphere below the southern mountains (distinct from the cold northern steppe). East and west of the mountain spine differ (windward wet / leeward dry), and frozen caps sit at both poleward ends. (How the southern desert and southern mountains reconcile with the 2026-05-31 region map is a pending refinement — see [[open-questions]].) (source: trading-cultures-halaran.txt, 2026-06-03)

## Peoples

Halaran is home to the **majority of Eadras's modern peoples**. By region: dragons and [[mitans|Shada'din]] in the north; Shada'din and gnomes along the Collision Range; orcs on the arid steppe; [[katharim|Katharim]], [[beast-folk|beast-folk]], and the Demonfall communities in the forested mid-west; humans, halflings, and mixed-city populations across the temperate heartland; and the Azure-Basin city-states with their Amunorian diaspora on the eastern bay. (The thriving [[dragon-empire|Dragon Empire]] and the [[verdant-scar|Verdant Scar]] sit on the **northwestern continent**, not Halaran; see those pages.)
