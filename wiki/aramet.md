# Aramet

**Summary**: Aramet is a Lesser Goddess of Hidden Things — an Ascended Mortal of Elfen origin who guides seekers toward lost paths, buried secrets, and forgotten runes.

**Sources**: Aramet.txt, Gods of Eadras.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-04-29

---

## Overview

Aramet is the subtle patron of concealed things: lost paths, buried secrets, enemy weak spots, forgotten runes. She guides rather than fights — [[gods-of-eadras|gods]] are barred from direct combat except during apocalyptic situations like the Demon War. Call on her, and she will whisper where to look, what to avoid, how to slip through.

She appears in the [[gods-of-eadras|divine pantheon]] as the **Third Elfen Goddess** and "Aramet the Wise," Goddess of Finding Things.

## Origin

Aramet was born [[elfen|Elfen]]. Eldritch pulsed through her from birth — it was like breathing. By her teens, she cast magic like a master; [[eldritch]] bent to her will and spells came naturally.

## Ascension

During the Demon War, Aramet was scouting a secondary gate when she faced a guardian she could not defeat. In the clash, she **ascended** — the demon was obliterated and the gate sealed. This moment of mortal-becoming-divine is characteristic of Ascended Mortal Gods in the Eadras setting.

## Symbol

A silver leaf with a compass etched in the veins — pointing to what is lost.

## At the Table

When players call on Aramet: *"Aramet, please help me find it."*

She might answer softly: *"It's there — behind the crag. Careful."*
