# Da'shar

> **DEPRECATED 2026-05-14.** This page is retained as a slug-target so existing wikilinks resolve. The Da'shar concept was part of the pre-2026-05-09 Sorcerer / Weave magic system, which was superseded by the [[netharim|Netharim]] / [[lexanari|Lexanari]] / [[druids|druid sub-priesthood]] / fourth-order rework on 2026-05-09. The page narrative was removed on 2026-05-14 as part of the *ALL deprecated content* cleanup.

**Summary**: The Da'shar ("Those Who Crossed") were transmuted sorcerers in the pre-rework Sorcerer/Weave system. The mechanism by which a sorcerer became a Da'shar (the *Burning Passage* into the *Heart of the Goddess*) is no longer canonical.

**Sources**: Removed; see [[log]] for the original narrative under its 2026-04-29 ingest.
