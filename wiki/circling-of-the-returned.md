# Circling of the Returned

**Summary**: The **Circling of the Returned** is the [[unicorns|unicorn]] quarantine and reintegration rite performed when a unicorn returns from dangerous wandering. The herd surrounds but does not fully merge with the returning unicorn; they run in widening circles and sing through horn, hoof, breath, and ground. Surface memory is released first. If the memory bites, it is not admitted deeper. The rite tests for corruption, demonic stain, entropy-warp, predatory memory, harmful resonance, Black Vault contamination, and psychic infection â€” and *can refuse the return* if the contamination is too deep.

## What the rite is

A unicorn returning from a period of separation â€” particularly a [[unicorns#Modern unicorn life patterns|Seasonal Strayer]] coming home from a scouting mission to a wounded region, or a Long Strayer attempting reintegration after years apart â€” does not simply rejoin the herd. The herd performs the **Circling of the Returned** to test what the returning unicorn is carrying.

The structure:

- The herd **surrounds** the returning unicorn but does **not fully merge**. Herd-mind contact is established at the outermost layer, not at the herd-deep level.
- The herd runs in **widening circles** around the returning unicorn. The motion is functional â€” the geometry of widening concentric rings creates the resonance pattern the rite requires.
- The herd **sings through horn, hoof, breath, and ground**. The song is not melodic in the mortal sense; it is a sustained harmonic-and-rhythmic field that the returning unicorn responds to.
- The returning unicorn **releases surface memory first**. The recent, easily-accessible layer of their experience is offered to the herd. The herd evaluates.
- If the surface memory **passes**, the herd opens to receive deeper layers. The rite continues, with progressively deeper memory offered and evaluated.
- If the surface memory **bites** â€” if it carries any of the categories below â€” the herd closes. The rite ends. The returning unicorn does not merge that day.

The rite may take **hours, days, or longer**, depending on the depth of separation and the complexity of what the returning unicorn carries.

## What the rite tests for

The Circling is designed to detect contamination categories that would damage the herd if absorbed:

- **Corruption** â€” moral, magical, or spiritual deformation of the returning unicorn's character
- **Demonic stain** â€” residue from contact with [[demons|demonic]] entities, particularly through [[demonfall-marches|Demonfall Marches]] travel
- **Entropy-warp** â€” exposure to entropy-related magic (the [[spear-of-crystallized-entropy|Spear's]] influence, certain [[arathi-geo-substrate|Arathi geo-substrate]] of the Oru-Sha line, [[kudur-etir|Kudur-eá¹­ir's]]-pattern damage)
- **Predatory memory** â€” exposure to entities or experiences that have left the returning unicorn carrying *the predator's mode of attention* rather than the unicorn's own
- **Harmful resonance** â€” magical signatures that would disrupt herd-mind cohesion if integrated
- **Black Vault contamination** â€” substrate-distress patterns from [[deep-vaults|Black Vault]] proximity
- **Psychic infection** â€” patterns transmitted through telepathic contact with hostile minds

These categories are not exhaustive. The herd's wisdom about what to test for evolves; new categories have been added as new threats have emerged across history.

## What happens when contamination is found

If the rite detects contamination too deep to safely integrate, the herd may:

### Exile

The returning unicorn is **not permitted re-entry**. The herd opens to the outer-layer contact long enough for communication but does not allow deeper merge. The returning unicorn may attempt return later, after the contamination has either healed naturally, been deliberately worked through, or faded.

Exile is not punishment in the human sense. It is structural protection of the herd. The exiled unicorn knows the herd holds care for them; the herd's refusal is a measure of how dangerous the contamination is, not of how loved the unicorn is.

### Sleep

The herd may **induce extended dormancy** in the returning unicorn. This is a specific herd-mind technique: the unicorn is put into a deep restorative state, often for years, during which the contamination is slowly metabolised. The unicorn does not consciously experience the passage; they wake when the herd judges them safe to reintegrate.

Sleep is reserved for cases where the contamination is deep but reversible.

### Death

In extreme cases â€” Black Vault contamination, demonic-symbiosis cases, predatory-memory cases that have progressed to identity-replacement â€” the herd may judge that the returning unicorn cannot be saved and that their continued existence threatens both themselves and any future herd they might contact.

In these cases, the herd offers a **final return**: the contaminated unicorn enters the centre of the Circling, the herd's song deepens into the death-song mode, and the unicorn is allowed to die in the herd's presence. The death-song is also a barrier â€” the herd holds whatever the unicorn was carrying *outside* the herd-mind during the transition, so the contamination does not propagate through the death.

This is rare. Most contamination is recoverable. But the rite must be able to handle the unrecoverable case, and it does.

### Long wandering

If the contamination is severe but the unicorn refuses the offered death, the herd may **release** the unicorn to long wandering â€” solitary travel through specific kinds of land that the herd's Wound-Kept memory recognises as restorative for that particular contamination type.

Long wandering is not exile in the angry sense. It is the herd saying: *you carry something we cannot hold; go to a place that can hold it, and return when the carrying has eased*. Some unicorns return; some do not. Those who return undergo a second Circling.

## What the rite is NOT

- **Not a trial** â€” there is no accusation, no defence, no verdict in the mortal sense
- **Not punishment** â€” exile is structural, not moral
- **Not optional** â€” every returning unicorn after substantial separation submits to the Circling; the herd cannot safely receive otherwise
- **Not transparent** â€” the returning unicorn does not always know which contamination category triggered the closure; they may experience the rite from the inside as the herd simply stopping
- **Not always conclusive** â€” some contaminations require multiple Circlings across time; a unicorn may pass surface and middle layers and be turned back at deeper layers

## Relation to other unicorn practices

The Circling is part of a broader unicorn discipline around memory and contamination:

- **[[unicorn-wound-keeping|Wound-Keeping]]** preserves the memory of what *categories* of contamination exist, so the Circling knows what to test for
- **[[herd-listeners|Herd-Listeners]]** sometimes witness Circlings when their service is required â€” particularly when a returning Seasonal Strayer carries information the herd intends to share with mortal institutions
- The [[unicorns#Modern unicorn life patterns|stallion and alpha mare's roles]] (guardian of departure, guardian of return) are the institutional structure that makes the Circling possible â€” the alpha mare ultimately decides what may return

## The cost of the rite to the herd

The Circling is **not free** for the herd. Each rite costs:

- **Attention** â€” significant herd-mind capacity is devoted to the testing for the duration
- **Risk** â€” even careful Circling can leak contamination if the test fails
- **Grief** â€” when the rite ends in death, exile, or long wandering, the herd carries that loss
- **Time** â€” the herd cannot do other things during a Circling

Herds with frequent Strayer departures spend more on this rite than they spend on most other activities. The investment is the price of having Strayers at all.
