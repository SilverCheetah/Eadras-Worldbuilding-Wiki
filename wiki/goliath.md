# Goliath

**Summary**: The descendants of the **Giants**, who themselves were the natural devolution of the **[[titans|Titans]]** — Melania's [[tarans|Tarran]]-engineered warriors against chaos. Goliath stand roughly **ten feet tall**, two stages of devolution removed from the original 25–30 meter Titan war-stock.

**Sources**: Peoples of Eadras.txt, titans-canon-2026-05-10.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-05-10

---

## Origin

Goliath are two steps of devolution from their original progenitors:

1. **[[titans|Titans]]** — Tarran-engineered as warriors against chaotic incursions (source: Peoples of Eadras.txt). Titans were the heaviest piece of the Tarran martial cluster — adults reaching **25–30 meters** in height (children already 10 meters), bred to break chaotic incursions through sheer ruinous mass. See [[titans]] for the full treatment.
2. **Giants** — natural devolution of Titans: smaller, less capable, but still enormous by humanoid standards (source: Peoples of Eadras.txt).
3. **Goliath** — natural devolution of Giants: ~10 feet tall (source: Peoples of Eadras.txt).

The pattern is consistent with all "natural devolution" races: each generation is smaller, less long-lived, and less magically capable than the one before, while retaining the basic body plan and core competencies of the progenitor stock.

The devolution from Titans to Goliath is dramatic: from **25–30 meters** at full Titan adulthood, down through intermediate Giant scale, and finally to the **~3 meter (~10 foot)** modern Goliath. Across two stages, the lineage has shed roughly an order of magnitude of size and the deeper Tarran martial spellcraft that made [[titans|Titans]] effective war-engineering. Modern Goliath are recognizable kin in body-plan but bear only fragments of the progenitor stock's scale and capability.

## Anatomy

Roughly ten feet tall (source: Peoples of Eadras.txt). Specific anatomical detail beyond height remains to be defined, but the lineage implies a heavy, durable build oriented toward combat.

## Society and Culture

Specific goliath culture, settlements, and traditions are not yet documented in canon. They are listed as a present-day playable race intended for modern Eadras.

## Relations

- **Giants** are the immediate parent stock and presumably still exist in some form, though canon does not explicitly confirm their modern presence.
- **Other Tarran-descended lines** (humans via Aetherin, dwarves and halflings via Mitans) are distant cousins through the shared Tarran progenitor.
- The exiled **[[tarans|Tarran]]** of the [[gray-matriarchy|Gray Matriarchy]] would in theory recognize goliath as faded children of one of their oldest war-engineering projects, though canon offers no detail on whether they care about the Titan line specifically.
