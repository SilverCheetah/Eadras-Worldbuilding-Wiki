## Cladding

A clad Tideclad is not invulnerable; the cladding is alive, dynamic, and must be sustained. But a Tideclad in their natural element, properly clad, is dangerous on a scale that surface martial traditions struggle to translate.

## Combat philosophy

The Tideclad view **battle as "releasing the sea's tension."** They fight by turning an opponent's own momentum and the ocean's force against them â€” currents are routed back into the attacker, pressure waves are reflected at angles that crack what the attacker presents, and a Tideclad blade is often the same blade the attacker brought, redirected by water. This is a discipline more than a weapon. Tideclad martial training emphasizes patience, position, and the reading of currents. A Tideclad who has chosen the ground and timed the tide has, in their own teaching, already won.
