## Overview

Solarion is the divine city located in [[celestia]], the highest plane of the [[eadras-cosmology|Eadras Mythos]]. It is where the avatars of the [[gods-of-eadras|gods]] gather, socialize, and â€” obsessively â€” play the **Game of the Gods**.

## The Game of the Gods

Created by [[dao]] in secret, the Game simply appeared one day in Solarion. All important mortals are represented as pieces on a giant divine board. The gods play it like a tabletop roleplaying game â€” taking turns moving tokens representing mortals, shaping events, competing and collaborating.

The Game is so deeply addictive that most gods spend the majority of their time in Solarion playing it rather than directly intervening in mortal affairs. This explains much of the divine non-intervention in mortal matters.

## Seat of the Divine Bureaucracy

The entire **[[divine-bureaucracy|Divine Bureaucracy]]** â€” the vast, ancient, dysfunctional administrative organization that handles the day-to-day operations of divinity (prayer routing, Favor tracking, miracle approval, soul judgment, oath registration, and the rest) â€” is located within Solarion. In Bureaucratic and theological writing, Solarion in this aspect is referred to as **the Eternal City**: the same place, named for its institutional function rather than its avatar-gathering function.

The two roles coexist within the same divine city without much friction. The gods' avatars socialize and play the Game of the Gods in the city's social and ceremonial spaces; the Bureaucracy's many bureaus, archives, courts, and offices occupy the city's administrative substructure. The [[divine-bureaucracy#Bureau of the Eternal City|Bureau of the Eternal City]] manages the physical infrastructure that supports both functions. The Game proceeds without interfering with prayer-routing, and prayer-routing proceeds without interfering with the Game.

Solarion is therefore both **the social heart of the gods** (Game of the Gods, avatar gatherings, divine festivals) and **the operational heart of divinity** (the bureaucratic capital where divine business is actually conducted). The two aspects are not in tension; they are the same city seen from two different angles.
