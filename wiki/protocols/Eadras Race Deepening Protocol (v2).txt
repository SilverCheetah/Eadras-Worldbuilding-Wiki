Eadras Race Deepening Protocol (v2)

Purpose
This is a standardized, reusable framework for systematically expanding any race in the Eadras setting. It is designed to:
- preserve canon consistency
- reduce AI improvisational drift
- enforce thematic alignment
- encourage anthropological depth
- standardize information gathering
- distinguish established canon from speculation

This protocol is incremental by design. Work section-by-section. Do not attempt to finalize every section in one pass.

--------------------------------------------------
REQUIRED CONTEXT BEFORE USE
--------------------------------------------------

Before applying this protocol, ingest:

1. The target race page.
2. Any pages directly referenced on the race page.
3. Relevant civilization pages.
4. Relevant religion/deity pages.
5. Relevant timeline pages.
6. The current Eadras Core Canon & Thematic Principles document.
7. Any related species/culture pages likely to overlap.
8. Relevant naming-convention pages if available.

If context is missing:
- identify the missing context explicitly
- request the missing pages or clarifications
- avoid inventing authoritative canon silently
- separate speculation from established canon
- preserve uncertainty where uncertainty exists

The wiki is authoritative.
Protocols are interpretive support tools.

--------------------------------------------------
INFORMATION INTEGRITY RULES
--------------------------------------------------

If information required for a section is missing:

DO NOT:
- silently invent authoritative canon
- smooth over gaps
- infer certainty where none exists
- fabricate relationships, timelines, institutions, or terminology without explicit indication
- present speculation as settled canon

INSTEAD:

1. Explicitly identify the missing information.
2. Request the missing pages, notes, or clarifications if necessary.
3. Distinguish:
   - established canon
   - implication
   - speculation
   - optional expansion
   - unknowns
4. Preserve ambiguity where ambiguity is intentional.
5. If generation proceeds despite incomplete context, clearly label all extrapolations.

Unknown information is preferable to false certainty.

Partial canon is acceptable.
Contradictory historical understanding is acceptable.
Uncertainty is part of Eadras.

--------------------------------------------------
CANON CONFIDENCE RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Every section should distinguish between:

- Established canon
- Strong implication
- Probable extrapolation
- Open unknowns
- Optional future hooks

Do not present speculative synthesis as settled canon.

If a section lacks sufficient information:
- summarize what is known
- identify the gap
- optionally suggest directions separately

--------------------------------------------------
CULTURAL COMPLEXITY RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Do not flatten cultures into:
- single traits
- genre shorthand
- monocultures
- moral binaries

Every race should contain:
- regional variation
- class variation
- ideological disagreement
- historical layering
- institutional tensions
- internal contradictions
- evolving identities

Avoid generic fantasy defaults unless justified structurally.

Infrastructure and social logic matter more than trope recognition.

--------------------------------------------------
RELATIONAL CONTEXT RULES
--------------------------------------------------

A race must never be developed in isolation.

Always identify:
- neighboring peoples
- historic enemies
- allies
- trade partners
- creator/progenitor relationships
- migration relationships
- linguistic relatives
- shared traumas
- religious overlap
- political tensions

Cultures are shaped through interaction.

--------------------------------------------------
TERMINOLOGY RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Use canonical terminology and spelling from the vault.

If multiple spellings exist:
- identify canonical form
- identify deprecated or alternate forms
- do not alternate inconsistently

Preserve:
- singular/plural rules
- adjectival forms
- demonyms
- formal titles
- cultural self-names
- outsider exonyms

RACE EXPANSION FRAMEWORK
--------------------------------------------------

- Direct creation?
- Engineered?
- Evolutionary divergence?
- Magical alteration?
- Hybridization?

What is the layered historical ancestry?

What major historical traumas shaped them?

How do they mythologize their own origins?

How accurate is their self-understanding?

Height ranges.

Distinctive features.

Lifespan and aging.

Environmental adaptations.

Disease resistance/vulnerability.

Social instincts.

Conflict styles.

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL VALIDATION
--------------------------------------------------

Before finalizing:

Check:
- canon consistency
- terminology consistency
- timeline consistency
- thematic alignment
- relationship integration
- anti-generic-fantasy compliance

Identify:
- unresolved unknowns
- unsupported assumptions
- inferred relationships
- speculative chronology
- missing dependency pages

Explicitly separate:
- canonical facts
- interpretive synthesis
- speculative suggestions

Every major element should connect to at least one thematic spine principle:

continuity · memory · catastrophe · identity · structure · survival · meaning
