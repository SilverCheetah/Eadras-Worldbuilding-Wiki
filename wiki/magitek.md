# Magitek

**Summary**: Magitek was the [[arathi|Arathi]] engineering discipline that applied [[crystalmancy]]-regulated [[eldritch|Eldritch]] through machines, constructs, networks, and everyday devices — the second of the two pillars of Arathi supremacy, and the most thoroughly lost technological tradition in Eadras's history.

**Sources**: Crystalmancy.txt, Major changes to Arathi Capitol.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-05-01

---

## The Twin Pillars

The Arathi were the greatest civilization Eadras has ever known precisely because they perfected the marriage of two arts:

- **[[crystalmancy]]** — the living, sung, harmonic manipulation of crystal to capture, regulate, and transform transparent Sourceshine into stable Eldritch.
- **Magitek** — the engineering application of that regulated Eldritch through machines, constructs, networks, and everyday devices.

Crystalmancy provided the **power source and the regulatory mind**; magitek provided the **delivery systems, automation, and daily convenience**. Because the Arathi were the only sapient race alive for their entire history, they spent millennia with no external competition or cultural borrowing. Every breakthrough in one field fed the other in a self-reinforcing golden loop. (source: Crystalmancy.txt)

To an Arathi citizen, there was no meaningful separation between *magic* and *technology*. A train was a living crystal organism guided by song. A communications band was both a machine and a piece of attuned personal crystalmancy. Their entire culture treated art, engineering, and magic as a single discipline.

## Major systems

### The Crystal Conduit and Ley Network

Seven world-piercing **Eldritch Conduits** (the source's "thirteen, later more" is non-canonical — canon is **seven conduits piercing the planet like poles, producing fourteen world-surface end points**; see [[magic-in-eadras]] and [[arathi-golden-age-map]] errata) functioned as both magical superhighways and literal power grids. Cities, factories, and transit hubs were deliberately built along these lines and along the surface ley-roads that connected them.

### Transportation

- **Crystal Rail Network** — Levitating magitek trains that rode rails of solidified light and crystal, reaching speeds that made continental travel routine. Stations were grown crystal cathedrals with perfect acoustic resonance.
- **Skyfleet and personal airships** — Elegant crystal-hulled vessels ranging from massive floating palaces to small personal skiffs. Propulsion came from tuned **Emberite** (combustion-side) and **Celestite** (lift/conduction-side) cores; see [[crystalmancy]].
- **Personal teleportation and Spire-Step** — Short-range personal teleportation devices (limited by safety protocols) and a public teleport network linking major Termini and city hubs. Long-distance travel still preferred rail or airship for comfort and capacity.
- **Underground magitek trains and portals** linking [[altea|Altea]] to every other Arathi city. (source: Major changes to Arathi Capitol.txt)

### Everyday devices

- **Echo Orbs** — Floating crystal spheres that projected visual recordings, broadcast live transmissions from distant cities, and carried harmonic music and news across the ley networks. The Arathi television/radio.
- **Whisper Bands** — Personal crystalline communicators worn as bracelets or necklaces. Allowed near-instant voice and thought transmission between attuned users across the empire, limited only by Conduit coverage.
- **Servitor Constructs** — Non-sentient crystal-and-metal automatons for labor, cleaning, security, and companionship. Many were grown with **Verdantite** cores to self-repair using ambient Eldritch.
- **Comfort Engines** — Home devices that regulated temperature, purified air, projected illusory scenery, played recorded memories, and gently massaged occupants using tuned **Aqualith** and **Prismite** arrays.

### Military and grand magitek

- **Crystal golems and war constructs** powered by Emberite hearts.
- **Floating siege platforms and city-scale defensive shields.**
- **The legendary Crystalline Forge artifacts** — portable factories that could unfold into full manufacturing complexes (see [[arathi#Major Artifacts|Crystalline Forge]]).

## Construction-grade integration

Magitek was not bolted onto Arathi cities; it was the substance of them. Walking through an Arathi city was walking through an active, mind-regulated machine: every working surface, transit path, and architectural element doubled as living magical infrastructure. See [[arathi#Cities|Arathi cityscape]] for the visual canon — towering crystal spires, glowing arcane circuitry traced along buildings, rainbow light-bridges, multi-altitude floating platforms, bioluminescent vegetation, and volumetric Eldritch lighting.

## What survived

When [[kudur-etir|Kudur-eṭir]] called down the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]] onto [[altea|Altea]], the integrated transit, ley, and governance functions of Arathi magitek collapsed planet-wide in a single instant — and continued collapsing for years afterward as orphan systems failed without their central regulating mind. Almost nothing of the working network remains.

Surviving traces:

- **Crystal minds** salvaged from non-Altean ruins are the basis of modern [[golems|gnome golem-craft]] — the most direct living continuation of any Arathi magitek tradition.
- **Conduit Ends** still leak raw Eldritch where the seven Conduits were sheared, creating wild magic zones.
- **Floating Crystal Shards** — a handful of city districts and crystal towers still drift in the sky; some have crashed.
- **Major artifacts** — the Orichalcum Ring of Command, the Rod of Transmutation, the Crystalline Forge (see [[arathi#Major Artifacts]]).
- **Echo Orbs still faintly glowing in deep vaults**, occasional Whisper Band fragments, half-swallowed airship docks — relics of a civilization that turned the entire world into a single, harmonious machine powered by song and crystal. (source: Crystalmancy.txt)

The successor traditions — Lirran Weavespinning, gnome golem-craft, scattered high-elf crystal practice — have recovered fragments of crystalmancy. The full **engineering** discipline that bound those fragments together into a working empire has not been recovered by anyone.

## What's been resolved (user direction 2026-05-01)

- **All named Termini were destroyed in the Cataclysm.** There are no surviving local Termini-magitek networks. Whatever ley-road and conduit infrastructure stood at Northwatch, Frosthold, Skyreach, Sunspire, Dawnward, Emergate, Starfall, Southwatch, Stoneward, Westwatch, and Stormpear was annihilated alongside everything else; the names attached to those locations on the [[arathi-golden-age-map|Golden Age Map]] are themselves later-civilization conjectures (see that page's errata).
- **Three Crystalline Forges have been activated since the Cataclysm.** All three were activated by **gnomes** — true heirs of Arathi magitek through the [[golems|salvaged crystal-mind]] tradition — and have been **used by [[dwarves|Shada'din]]** in the gnome-Shada'din factory partnership (see [[gnomes#The Shada'din factory partnership]]). The mass production of magitek tools, weapons, and household goods that defines the modern industrial economy traces directly to these three Forges in operation.
- **Lirran Tower-craft is a combination of magical traditions** — not a preserved Arathi lineage and not a wholesale parallel rediscovery, but a deliberate synthesis of multiple living magical practices brought together to govern the Weave conduits. See [[order-sharanel]].
