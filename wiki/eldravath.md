# Eldravath

**Summary**: Eldravath — *"The Forged Land"* — is the **supercontinent** that formed approximately 4 million years after the [[arathi-cataclysm|Cataclysm]] (~6 million years before present), when plate tectonics drove the scattered post-Cataclysm continents back together into a single landmass. Eldravath is *"the last time the world existed as a single landmass before fracturing into the modern continents."*

**Sources**: 4mya.jpg; map explanation.txt; Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt; Addition — supercontinent phase context.txt; user direction 2026-05-04 (unicorn dating reverted to ~6mybp BP on Eldravath).

**Last updated**: 2026-05-06

## Formation

Following the [[arathi-cataclysm|Cataclysm]] of 10 million years ago, the [[altean-highlands|Altean Highlands]] sundered into the three continents [[avalon|Avalon]], [[damedes|Damedes]], and [[culethor|Culethor]], which then drifted apart through the first 3 million years of post-Cataclysm tectonic activity (see [[eadras-tectonic-history]]). Plate motion did not continue separating them indefinitely. By **4 million years post-Cataclysm** (~6 mybp), tectonic forces had **driven them back together**, along with whatever portions of [[therion|Therion]] and [[aurettos|Aurettos]] participated, into the single supercontinent **Eldravath**. (source: map explanation.txt)

## Geography

Eldravath is a world in **violent geological rebirth**. Continental collision on this scale produces correspondingly extreme features:

- **Towering central mountain ranges** dominate the interior — the suture zones where the older continents fused.
- **Vast interior basins, shifting sands, and newly formed inland seas** create dramatic environmental contrasts; rain-shadowed desert sits not far from new flooded basins.
- **Active coastal rifting and volcanic activity** marks the beginning of the forces that will eventually tear the supercontinent apart again in another million years.

The **[[worldbreaker-scar|Worldbreaker Scar]]** still glows faintly at the heart of the **Central Rift Ocean** within Eldravath — the supercontinent did not seal the Scar, only surrounded it. The continent's core is the impact aperture itself, surrounded by mountain country built by collisional uplift. (source: map explanation.txt)

Visible features on the 4mya snapshot include the **Sundered Atoll** off the eastern margin, the **Windward Isles** in the Worldbreaker rift area, and various coastal capes — all consistent with a continent in active mountain-building.

## Significance

Eldravath is the geological setting of two of the three most consequential events of the dragon-unicorn era:

- **First Chaos Crusade** (~6 mybp), in which one-third of the Prime Material was consumed before the Gods sealed the breach. The First Crusade is defended by dragons and Gods alone — unicorns emerged from the Silver Plains Flare around the same horizon, but in different regions of the supercontinent.
- **Silver Plains Flare** — [[ixthal|Ixthal's]] second amplified Sourceflare, which manifested the first unicorn herd as a single cohesive unit, occurred on Eldravath. The supercontinent's broadly stable, interconnected ecosystems were the very reason unicorns are inherently herd-oriented and system-attuned: large-scale environmental stability shaped the species as creatures aligned with balance and systemic continuity. (source: Addition — supercontinent phase context.txt; user direction 2026-05-04)

The Dragon-Unicorn Wars (which begin a few thousand years after the Silver Plains Flare) **also begin on Eldravath**. Whether they conclude before or after the Breaking of Eldravath at ~5mybp is undefined under the current map (see [[#Open canon]] below).

## Fate

Eldravath does **not survive** as a single landmass. Active coastal rifting visible at 4mya post-Cataclysm (~6mya BP) marks the beginning of the forces that tear the supercontinent apart. The **Breaking of Eldravath** is canonically dated to **~5 million years before present** — one million years after the supercontinent's formation. (source: Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt) Modern Eadras's continents are the *post-Eldravath* fracture pattern, not the original Cataclysm-era three-continent layout.

Eldravath represents *"the last time the world existed as a single landmass before fracturing into the modern continents."* No subsequent supercontinent has formed.

## Open canon

- **The fracture pattern of Eldravath** — which modern continents descend from which sectors of Eldravath.
- **The duration of Eldravath's existence** — formed at ~6 mybp; began fracturing at ~5 mybp; full breakup undefined.
- **Specific Eldravath regions** — only general features are described; named regions, mountain ranges, and inland seas are not yet defined.
- **Inhabitants** — dragons (well-established by Eldravath's formation) and the newly-emerged unicorn herds are the canonical sapient inhabitants. The map snapshot does not depict cities, lairs, or political geography.
- **Whether Eldravath's collisional event triggered the unicorn flare** — the timing coincidence is striking; whether [[ixthal|Ixthal]] chose Eldravath's geological upheaval as the cover for his second amplified surge is undefined.
- **Whether the Dragon-Unicorn Wars conclude before or after the Breaking of Eldravath** at ~5mybp. Map is still in flux; the wars are short (only a few thousand years) but the precise placement against the Breaking is open. (User direction 2026-05-04.)
