# Merfolk

**Summary**: *Merfolk* is the colloquial surface-dweller term that lumps together the **Five Modern Oceanic Races** descended from the **Sea'tua** — [[merrow|Merrow]], [[sirens|Sirens]], [[shimmercloaks|Shimmercloaks]], [[ink-lords|Ink Lords]], and [[tideclad|Tideclad]]. The term is convenient on land and inadequate everywhere else: the five peoples are visually, culturally, and politically distinct from one another, and prefer to be addressed by their own names. There is no single race called "merfolk" in the modern world.

**Sources**: Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt; Peoples of Eadras.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-05-06

---

## What "merfolk" means

To a surface dweller, "merfolk" generally means *anything humanoid that lives in the ocean and looks vaguely elven.* This is a useful shorthand on land — a coastal trader who needs to say "an oceanic race delegation arrived" can say "merfolk delegation" and be understood — but it is **not a unit of political, cultural, or biological reality** in modern Eadras.

What the term actually covers, in modern canon, are the **Five Modern Oceanic Races** descended from the [[elfen|Elfen]]-engineered **Sea'tua** (source: Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt):

| Race | Epithet | Specialty |
|---|---|---|
| **[[merrow\|Merrow]]** | The Tideweavers | Symbiotic coral architecture, patient gardening, surface trade |
| **[[sirens\|Sirens]]** | The Echo Weavers | Sonic geological reshaping, song tunnels |
| **[[shimmercloaks\|Shimmercloaks]]** | The Echo Traders | Living memory-currency, ultimate neutral middlemen |
| **[[ink-lords\|Ink Lords]]** | The Story Eaters | Communal scholars, embedded chroniclers, story-debt honor |
| **[[tideclad\|Tideclad]]** | The Current Wardens | Disciplined martial protectors who clad themselves in living water forces |

The two **Sea'tua-descended alien branches** — [[kuo-toa|Kuo-toa]] (the Fracture Cult of the Gaps) and [[sahuagin|Sahuagin]] (the Blood Fleets) — share the common Sea'tua progenitor but diverged so dramatically in body and culture that they are not normally included under "merfolk" even by the loose surface usage.

## Origin

All seven Sea'tua-descended peoples — the five modern oceanic races plus the Kuo-toa and Sahuagin — descend from the **Sea'tua**, the [[elfen|Elfen]]-engineered race created to oversee natural balance in the oceans. The Sea'tua were a relatively late Elfen project, created roughly ten million years after the [[arathi-cataclysm|Arathi Cataclysm]] (source: Peoples of Eadras.txt).

This makes all seven peoples ultimately Elfen kin — distantly related to surface [[elves|elves]] and [[fae|fae]] through the common Elfen progenitor — though the cultural distance to elves is enormous and any cultural memory is gone.
