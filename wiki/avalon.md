## Origin

Avalon is one of the **three continents that formed from the sundered [[altean-highlands|Altean Highlands]]** when the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]] struck and ignited plate tectonics. Together with [[damedes|Damedes]] (southwest) and [[culethor|Culethor]] (southeast), Avalon makes up the Altean-derived continental triad. The fragmentation began at the moment of impact (10mya BP); the named, distinct continent of Avalon stabilized within the first million years (see [[eadras-tectonic-history#1mya post-Cataclysm]]).

Avalon's pre-sundering identity was as the northern portion of the Altean Highlands â€” geographically close to whatever became the northern conduit termini of the pre-Cataclysm Arathi network. Specific pre-Cataclysm features that mapped onto Avalon's modern footprint are undefined.
