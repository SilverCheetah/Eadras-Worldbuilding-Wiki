## Overview

They are not moral angels and do not perform the duties such beings might in other cosmologies â€” rather, they enforce the conditions of coherent existence wherever those conditions are at threat. Their humorlessness, their mission-focus, their refusal to negotiate, and their immediate violent response to violations of the [[nexus|Nexus]] One Law (Do No Violence) all derive from this character: they are *implementation*, not deliberation. Whenever one is seen, **it is there by the OC's direct will** â€” Archons do not act on initiative, do not patrol on their own, and do not appear except where the Creator's attention has reached. **Their presence therefore indicates direct omniversal attention** and extraordinary cosmological significance. Mortal traditions that catalogue Archon sightings treat them as a kind of cosmic seismograph: where an Archon walks, something at the foundations is being either broken or repaired. Only Archons are permitted to freely enter the [[bastion-of-light]].
