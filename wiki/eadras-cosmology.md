# Eadras Cosmology

**Summary**: The internal structure of the Eadras Mythos — a Prime Material Plane surrounded by eight additional planes, held within a single bubble of the [[omniverse]].

**Sources**: Internal Cosmology of the Eadras Mythos.txt, Playground generation 1st part.txt, Grok Chat Results 6-2-26.txt (2026-06-02, geocentric cosmos and the Sun God's barge)

**Last updated**: 2026-06-02

---

## Internal Planes

Eadras is composed of its Prime Material Plane (physical reality) and eight additional internal planes:

| Plane | Description |
|---|---|
| [[celestia]] | Highest plane, dimensionally above the Prime Material. Analogue to Heaven. |
| [[nyxar]] | Lowest plane, dimensionally below the Prime Material. Analogue to Hell. |
| [[wyldglimmer]] | Bright reflection of the Prime Material, sharing the same dimensional space but shifted. Influenced by positive energy. |
| [[glimmerdark]] | Dark reflection of the Prime Material, occupying the same shifted space. Influenced by negative energy. |
| [[limina]] | The connecting plane — the "air" of the Mythos that surrounds and links all other planes. |
| Elemental Fire | One of four elemental planes forming a rotating ring around the Prime Material. |
| Elemental Water | Part of the elemental ring, rotating with the seasons. |
| Elemental Earth | Part of the elemental ring. |
| Elemental Air | Part of the elemental ring. |

## Layout

The **Prime Material Plane** sits at the center. The [[wyldglimmer]] and [[glimmerdark]] occupy the same space as the Prime Material but are dimensionally shifted — brighter above, darker below in essence.

[[celestia]] is dimensionally above; [[nyxar]] is dimensionally below.

The four Elemental Planes form a **continuous rotating ring** around the Prime Material, their rotation synchronized to the seasons.

[[limina]] fills the entire Mythos like air — an infinite mist or fog within which all other planes exist and through which inter-planar travel is possible.

## The geocentric cosmos and the Sun

Eadras is **fully geocentric.** The Prime Material world sits at the literal center of the cosmos, and **everything — including the other planets — orbits it.** The **Sun is the furthest celestial body of all**, orbiting the world at the outermost remove.

The Sun is **two things at once**: a genuine physical fusion source (the [[magic-in-eadras|Source]] whose Sourceshine drives the world's magical cycle) and the literal **chariot — or barge — of the Sun God.** The barge has an actual steering position, and the Sun God sometimes lets subordinate gods or honored guests take the helm. Concealed behind the sun's overwhelming light, the barge also serves as a **hidden divine party-space and sanctuary** — attended chiefly by [[gods-of-eadras#Ascended Mortal Gods|Ascended Mortal Gods]], with Elderborn and, very occasionally, [[gods-of-eadras|Elder Gods]] also invited. The Sun God is a **jolly and welcoming host.** (source: Grok Chat Results 6-2-26.txt)

This geocentric frame is compatible with the [[night-sky-cosmology|night-sky cosmology]]: the *stars* are other [[mythos|Mythoi]] glimpsed through [[limina|Limina's]] distortion, while the *sun, the four moons, and the planets* are bodies of Eadras's own Mythos orbiting the Prime world at the center.

## Travel Between Planes

Travel requires portals, gates, or movement through [[limina]]. Travel through Limina requires a clearly articulated destination — travelers must know where they are going.

## Why Eadras is Magic-Rich

The [[primordial-duo|Creator Deities Evye and Fryr]] sacrificed themselves completely into the Mythos rather than retaining power. This total sacrifice means orders of magnitude more magical energy exist in Eadras than in almost any other Mythos. It is one of the seven Mythoi closest to the core of the [[omniverse]].
