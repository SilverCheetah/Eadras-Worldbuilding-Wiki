# Altea

**Summary**: Altea was the capital of the [[arathi|Arathi]] civilization and the *"Heart of the Conduits"* — a three-ring city of crystal, flame-veined marble, orichalcum, and magisterium, built atop a lake at the heart of the [[altean-highlands|Altean Highlands]] and connected to every other Arathi city by teleportation, magitek underground trains, airships, and portals; it was the direct impact site of the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]] and was annihilated so completely that no trace of it remains.

**Sources**: Major changes to Arathi Capitol.txt, Arathis.txt, Playground generation 1st part.txt, Eadras – Golden Age Map.txt, Crystalmancy.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-05-06

## Heart of the Conduits

Altea's epithet on Arathi cartography was *"Heart of the Conduits"* — see [[arathi-golden-age-map]]. Conduits and ley-roads radiated from Altea outward to every fortress-city and named region of the world. The city was both the seat of political and magical power and the geometric center of the surface ley network. Whether the seven world-piercing conduits *all* passed through Altea (rather than radiating from it through surface arteries only) is open canon — see the [[arathi-golden-age-map|Golden Age Map]] errata for the conduit-count question.

## Geography and structure

Altea sat at the geographic center of the **Altean Highlands**, a continent-sized upland whose surface elevation relative to the surrounding world resembled (in proportion) what a continent looks like compared to ocean basins on a drained Earth (source: Major changes to Arathi Capitol.txt). The immediate surface plain around the city was called the **Altean Plains** (per Golden Age cartography).

The city was originally built atop a lake, in the manner of Tenochtitlán, and grew into a three-ring **Atlantis-pattern**: concentric rings of land separated by canals, with canals serving as the primary surface transport. Bridges, lifts, and stepped quays linked the rings.

**Construction materials** (source: Major changes to Arathi Capitol.txt):
- **Crystal** — for spires, conduits, and ley-channels
- **Flame-veined marble** — distinctive Arathi structural stone
- **Orichalcum** — load-bearing magitek alloy; also the substance of the Orichalcum Ring of Command's golems
- **[[mana-metals#Magisterium|Magisterium]]** — Apex three-base mana-metal alloy (Mithril + Orichalcum + Valthyr); said to hold permanent, self-sustaining magic. See [[mana-metals]].

**The central spire**: Altea's central spire was grown from **a single titanic [[crystalmancy#1. Celestite — Sky Crystals / Star Quartz|Celestite]] heart-stone** — a city-scale heart-stone, the largest of its lineage class ever produced (source: Crystalmancy.txt). Celestite is the lineage of supreme conduction and ley-waypointing; the city's heart-stone served as the world's archetypal *star-binding* anchor, aligning Altea's central position to the planet-wide ley network. When the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]] struck, the heart-stone vaporized with the city — its annihilation is part of the reason every conduit downstream of Altea failed in the same instant.

## Function

Altea was simultaneously:
- The **political capital** — seat of the council of archmages and crystalmancers that ruled Arathi civilization (source: Arathis.txt)
- The **commercial capital** — largest city by population; primary market hub
- The **cultural and entertainment capital**
- The **transit nexus** — connected to every other Arathi city by:
  - **Teleportation** circles
  - **Magitek underground trains**
  - **Airships**
  - **Portals**
  
  (source: Major changes to Arathi Capitol.txt)

This concentration is part of why Altea's loss erased the civilization. Severing the capital's transit, ley, and governance functions in a single instant collapsed Arathi infrastructure planet-wide.

## Annihilation

Altea was the direct impact target of the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]] — the ten-mile alien Falling Star Kudur-eṭir called down during the [[arathi-cataclysm|Arathi Cataclysm]]. The Falling Star did not strike near Altea. It struck **on Altea**.

Consequences specific to the capital:
- The city was vaporized at the impact instant — no buried ruins, no deep vaults, no ley-vaults, no glass domes, no sealed sanctuaries.
- The Altean Highlands sundered into three continents under the impact and the subsequent unzipping; the cavity between them became the [[arathalassic-sea|Arathalassic Sea]].
- The impact crater itself became a hole in the planetary mantle covered briefly by seawater, then filled — over 2 to 8 million years — by lava spilling out as a Large Igneous Province. The resulting landmass is the present-day **[[arathyn]]** at the center of the Arathalassic Sea (source: Major changes to Arathi Capitol.txt).

**Canonical absolute**: *There is no Arathi descent, no Arathi echoes, and no remnants of Altea whatsoever* (source: Major changes to Arathi Capitol.txt). Remnants of *other* Arathi cities are rare-but-existent — see [[arathi]] and [[pre-cataclysm-relics]] — but Altea itself left nothing. The post-Cataclysm fauna of the [[arathalassic-sea|Arathalassic Sea]] (Abyssal Crownwhales, Vent Seraphs, Scaldwyrms, Crown-Eels, the Bathyric Mandate) belong to the new abyssal habitat the Cataclysm produced, not to Altea's grave.
