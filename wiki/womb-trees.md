# Womb Trees

**Summary**: The [[elfen|Elfen]] do not reproduce sexually — they have no reproductive equipment at all. New Elfen are born **whole and adult** from **womb trees**: an awakened, willing nature-spirit infused into a living tree, joined with attached Elfen living tissue and sung into an external gestation chamber. Gestation lasts one year, during which the unborn Elfen is visited **daily by every member of the village**, who lay hands on the womb to transfer memory and knowledge. The newborn emerges fully grown, already knowing who they are, their role, and the community's accumulated library — but **wisdom cannot be transferred**, only lived. The womb tree is the most sacred instance of the single Elfen act: **song over willing living matter, non-consumptive**.

## What a womb tree is

A womb tree has three parts, all joined by song:

1. **A willing nature-spirit**, awakened and infused into a living tree. The consent is baked into the mechanism: only a *willing* spirit is invited in, exactly as in the broader Elfen ethic of singing only over willing living matter. (Compare the [[velrathi-architecture|Velrathi Stone-Bound]], who similarly elevate an already-willing genius loci — a quiet structural rhyme between the two progenitor traditions.)
2. **Attached Elfen living tissue**, the biological seed of the new person.
3. **An external gestation chamber**, sung into shape around the joined tree-and-tissue.

Because the [[elfen|Elfen]] make only by song over living matter, and because singing does not harvest or kill, reproduction is **non-consumptive**: the womb tree lives. Reproduction is not an exception to Elfen making; it is its **most sacred case**.

## Gestation and the laying-on of hands

Gestation lasts **one year**. Across that year the unborn Elfen attunes to nature's cycles, and is visited **daily by every villager** — each laying hands on the womb to **transfer memory and knowledge** into the growing mind. By birth the new Elfen carries the community's accumulated **library**: facts, the village's shared memory, their own role, and an understanding of who they are. There is **no childhood**. An Elfen is born an adult.

This is why Elfen society is **choral at the root**, not by training: an Elfen is *made by the whole village* and is therefore constitutively everyone's. Individualism among the Elfen is not suppressed — it was simply **never installed**.

## Knowledge transfers; wisdom does not (load-bearing)

The single most important limit of the transfer:

- **Transferable at gestation** — facts, memory, role, the community "library."
- **NOT transferable — earned only by living** — **wisdom**: present-tense judgment, living attunement, synthesis. The download gives the **library**, not the **librarian**.

Wisdom therefore ripens on the **same century-scale clock** as mana-density in a tree or the depth of a [[elfen#Wardens and the Council of the Eldest|warden]]: a warden, a womb tree, and a stand of [[goldenwood|goldenwood]] are all the same kind of thing — slow accumulators. This single fact organizes much of Elfen life:

- **Funerary grief** is grief for *how they understood* — the one untransferable mind. (The Elfen [[elfen#Reverence for all taken life|funerary ethic]] — a death must be utilized to the fullest, waste is obscene — applies to their own dead too.)
- **The daily laying-on of hands** is the civilization-scale hedge against that loss: the library is preserved even though the librarian is not.
- It is why the [[elfen#Lifespan, the apex, and ennui|scheduled loss of the deepest elders]] is the race's defining tragedy — knowledge survives them; wisdom does not.

## Relation to the engineered child races

The Elfen engineered their child races ([[el-mer|El'mer]], Sidhe, [[sea-tua|Sea'tua]]) by the same universal mechanism every progenitor used — [[peoples-of-eadras#How the engineering worked|magical modification of their own offspring across generations]] — which for the Elfen means modification worked through the **womb-tree process** rather than through pregnancy. ("Engineered by" and "descended from" remain the same act; the womb tree is simply the Elfen form of that descent.) Modern [[elves|Elves]] retain a diluted echo of this in their **purpose-born, womb-tree-grown** character.
