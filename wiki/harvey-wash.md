## Identity and the Wash alias

Harvey was born with a traditional [[goblins|goblin]] name in the standard goblin pattern (see [[goblins#Naming|Goblin Naming]]). Operating in human urban society, he adopted the human-sounding alias **Harvey Wash** to **blend in with the humans of [[bold|Bold]]** â€” a deliberate piece of social camouflage that has hardened into his everyday identity.

He **dresses the part**: fine silks and high-quality human-style clothing, the deliberate visual register of a wealthy merchant-prince. The dress is functional, not vain â€” Bold's mercantile and civic establishment treats well-dressed humans differently from any goblin in any clothes, and Harvey has no time to lose to the first impression.

The alias **does not deceive other goblins**. Within the goblin network â€” which spans cities and civilizations (see [[goblins#Society|Goblin Society]]) â€” Harvey is known by his original name, his goblin lineage, and his title.

## Significance

Harvey Wash is a rare canonical example in modern Eadras of:

- A **goblin** operating successfully and visibly inside a major Prime-Material city's establishment, by way of a deliberate human alias.
- A worked example of the **scavenger / network goblin** archetype taken to its civic-positive extreme â€” turning the goblin instinct for *value-in-discards* and *cross-city networks* into citywide infrastructure.
- A **non-aristocratic philanthropic power center** in a kingdom-capital city, with concrete leverage (employment, sanitation, charity, the Thieves' Compact) that makes him difficult for the establishment to dislodge.
