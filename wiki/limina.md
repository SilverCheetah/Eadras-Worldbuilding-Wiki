# Limina

**Summary**: Limina is the connecting plane of the [[eadras-cosmology|Eadras Mythos]] — an infinite mist or fog that surrounds all other planes, fills the Mythos bubble, and serves as the medium through which inter-planar travel occurs. It is also the **first plane any Chaos-side material enters** when a major fracture opens in the Prime Material; entities can then spill from Limina into the Prime, but never directly.

**Sources**: Limina.txt, Internal Cosmology of the Eadras Mythos.txt, Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-05-02

---

## Overview

Limina has no counterpart in the Prime Material Plane — it is entirely unique as the connective tissue of the [[eadras-cosmology|Eadras Mythos]]. It surrounds all other planes in mist or fog, stretching to infinity and ultimately bordering the infinite shell of the Mythos itself.

## Function

All inter-planar travel within the Eadras Mythos requires either portals, gates, or physical travel through Limina. Travel in Limina requires the traveler to **articulate a clear destination** — without a specific intention, one cannot navigate the fog.

## The Gaps: fractures into Limina

A core cosmological fact, only recently surfaced through the [[kuo-toa|Kuo-toa]] cult's theology and confirmed in canon: **all major fractures in the Prime Material — whether created by the [[chaos-crusades|Chaos Crusades]], by [[kudur-etir|Kudur-eṭir]]'s First Breach, or by other catastrophic ruptures — ultimately open into Limina first.** From Limina, entities can then spill into the Prime Material. (source: Updates afternoon 5-1-26.txt)

This is the route the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]] took on God's Day: pulled out of Chaos through Kudur-eá¹­ir's First Breach, through Limina, then through the shattered exterior shell of the Prime Material Plane and onto Altea. It is also the route Chaos took during both Crusades, and the route demons take when they cross from their own Mythos into Eadras.

The Kuo-toa worship these fractures — which they call **the Gaps** — as sacred doorways. They believe widening and stabilizing enough Gaps will eventually achieve **Final Reconciliation**: the merging of Limina and the Prime Material into a single perfected reality with no contradictions. See [[kuo-toa]].

### The microfracture network — the modern Gap field

The few large historical Gaps (the [[chaos-crusades|Chaos Crusades]] sites; the rare points where [[kudur-etir|Kudur-eṭir's]] First Breach in the [[mythos|Mythos Shell]] above propagates through) are not the dominant interface between Limina and the Prime. The dominant interface is a **planet-wide network of microfractures** in the Prime Material Plane's own exterior shell — the residue of the Worldbreaker's passage on God's Day, after the gross wound substantially healed across the ~10 million years since. (canon clarification, curator 2026-05-02)

The microfractures are too small for entities of meaningful size to traverse, but they are real holes — and they are everywhere. Most ambient Limina-bleed felt anywhere on Eadras (from minor wild-magic flickers in old ruins to the faint *otherness* of certain wells, caves, or cracked ice) is leakage through this network rather than through any named Gap. See [[arathi-cataclysm#The Healed Shell and the Microfracture Network|arathi-cataclysm: The Healed Shell]] for the origin and [[kuo-toa]] for the modern theology that grounds itself in this fact.
