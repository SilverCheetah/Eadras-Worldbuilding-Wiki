## Welcome to Eadras

> *In this world, light and darkness cannot exist without each other.*
> *The presence of one makes the other bigger, deeper, and brighter.*
>
> Great civilizations have fallen. Terrible wars have scarred the land. Ancient evils still stir in the shadows.
>
> And yet, hope is not foolish here.
>
> It is precisely because the darkness is so real that every act of courage, every act of mercy, every choice to build something beautiful matters all the more.
>
> This is Eadras.

Eadras is **epic dark fantasy with earned hope**, not grimdark. The darkness is real. So is the light. Each makes the other matter.

- [magic-in-eadras](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/magic-in-eadras.md) — Overview of all magic sources and traditions; the Weave (**partial-rework-pending status as of 2026-05-09**)
- [eldritch](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/eldritch.md) — The ancient name for usable magical energy; reflected Sourceshine
- [eldritch-cycle](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/eldritch-cycle.md) — The planetary cycle of intake, transformation, distribution, and reintegration that underlies all magic on Eadras
- [seven-conduits](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/seven-conduits.md) — The eternal magical structures with fourteen surface termini that anchor the Eldritch Cycle; not physical, not destructible, older than the Weave
- [source-surges](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/source-surges.md) — Natural fluctuations in the Source's magical output; C-class through X-class flares; the Weave does NOT buffer them
- [collection-fields](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/collection-fields.md) — Every living creature's unconscious magic-gathering field; size scales with power (slow power↔wisdom feedback); the capacity-side threshold of Ascension (designation still decides); field-merge of a unicorn quorum hosts the herd-mind; unstudied by any mortal tradition
- [necromancy-and-undeath](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/necromancy-and-undeath.md) — Necromancy as a neutral technical discipline; stigmatized by Xar'vion
- [crystalmancy](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/crystalmancy.md) — The Arathi art of growing, attuning, and conducting living crystal lattices; seven Greater Lineages
- [magitek](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/magitek.md) — The Arathi engineering discipline that applied crystal-regulated Eldritch through machines, transit, and everyday devices

### The Four Magical Orders (current canon; 2026-05-09 rework)
- [wizards](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/wizards.md) — Scholarly arcane casters; mental focus + physical grimoires; Arcane Language tier; multi-source spell lists; personal-shorthand-encoded grimoires as prized treasure; mana-flavored spell ingredients shape and enhance workings
- [priests](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/priests.md) — Divine practitioners who call upon their gods; daily capacity is granted by the **Divine Bureaucracy** based on the priest's current **Favor** (Neutral / Positive / Negative / God's Favorite); priests do not memorize spells; restoration timing is set by the deity's temperament (sunup / sundown / noon / midnight)
- [lexanari](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/lexanari.md) — The Name-Bound. Innate-only mortal speakers of the Primordial Language. Enormous power, catastrophic failure mode (mortal minds can't hold deific complexity, so vague invocation kills); fills the niche the borrowed Druid concept used to occupy. Uses [Pillar Magic](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/pillar-magic.md).
- [netharim](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/netharim.md) — The "Little Gods." Intuitive reality-shapers whose power scales with the accuracy of their personally-studied internal model of reality; worldwide order, numerous, politically and scholastically active; fills the niche the borrowed Netharim concept used to occupy. Uses [Pillar Magic](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/pillar-magic.md).
- [druids](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/druids.md) — Now a specialized sub-priesthood of [Qoxli](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/qoxli.md), [Sylph](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/sylph.md), and [Nox](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/nox.md) (no longer a standalone fourth order); self-sufficient wilderness-skilled mediators between mortals and the [fae](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/fae.md)
- [pillar-magic](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/pillar-magic.md) — The shared underlying grammar of [Lexanari](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/lexanari.md) and [Netharim](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/netharim.md) magic. Five **Command Pillars** (Control / Transform / Create / Destroy / Perceive) + five **Substance Pillars** (Essence / Matter / Flesh / Mind / Spirit) = 25 canonical combinations. Per-Pillar mastery progression in place of spell points
- See [true-language](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/true-language.md) for the **Language Hierarchy** (True / Primordial / Divine / Arcane) underlying all four orders

## Factions & Institutions

- [black-hand](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/black-hand.md) — Ten-thousand-year-old conspiracy serving Xar'vion's Inner Hand goal; Outer Hand pursues power via information, infiltration, and blackmail
- [white-palm](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/white-palm.md) — Penitential order descended from Inner Hand defectors; protects Netharim and hunts the Black Hand
- [order-sharanel](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/order-sharanel.md) — The singular ancient and most prestigious order of Netharim; sworn directly to Lirra; based in the Towers of Lirra; founded during the Demon War as Lirra's Weave-creation act transmuted 10,000 mortals (no progenitors) into the first Netharim
- [kingdom-of-xanar](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/kingdom-of-xanar.md) — Xar'vion's pre-ascension realm; foundation of the Black Hand
- [dragon-empire](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/dragon-empire.md) — Far-eastern modern-era polity ruled by a coalition of semi-divine [dragons](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/dragons.md); comprehensive bureaucratic meritocracy with pictogram-and-hashmark skill ranks; free public education; Tang+Khmer aesthetic; authoritarian-meritocratic pole of the [three-cultures triangle](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/three-cultures-ethical-triangle.md); founded by a post-Demon-War dragon-mercenary coup
- [fae-da-nar](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/fae-da-nar.md) — Hidden frontier society of [Woodkin](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/beast-folk.md) survivors who maintain coexistence with humans rather than confrontation; *Fae-da'Nar does not ask whether the individual is precious; it knows the individual is precious — then it asks whether keeping them alive will kill everyone else*
- [four-laws-of-fae-da-nar](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/four-laws-of-fae-da-nar.md) — The four behavioural thresholds (don't frighten humans / don't interfere / oppose Shunned threats / obey Druids)
- [the-shunned](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/the-shunned.md) — Predatory races and groupings that opted out of the frontier compact; functional category (those whose practices have crossed the line), not a racial classification
- [frontier-hospitality](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/frontier-hospitality.md) — Human-side of the coexistence compact (First Bowl / No Iron on the Table / Fair Measure Under Witness / No Mocking the Mask / Guest-Fire Peace); Peace-Brass and Guest-Bronze as marker metals
- [threshold-cities](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/threshold-cities.md) — Five-stage growth pattern from Guest-Bowl Settlement to Severed City; five long-term endpoints — Faithful, Ceremonial, Severed Expansion, Ruined Caution, Relocated

## Artifacts & Objects

Wielded 3 times, hidden 4: destruction-illusions / time-displacement in the [Wyldglimmer](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/wyldglimmer.md) / reverse-time-bubble god-prison in the deepest [Glimmerdark](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/glimmerdark.md) / impaled back into the original First Breach Wound under [Arathyn](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/arathyn.md)
- [worldender](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/worldender.md) — The apocalyptic falling star pulled through Kudur-eṭir's First Breach when his shattered post-ascension mind reached uncontrollably back into Chaos; destroyed his Icon and shattered the planet
- [worldender-crystal](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/worldender-crystal.md) — Alien crystal Dao harvested from the Worldender; foreign to the Mythos; all fragments hoarded by Dao alone
- [firethorn-throne](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/firethorn-throne.md) — Melania's masterpiece from leftover alien crystal; vanished after the Emperor's poisoning; not in any known location
- [hall-of-kings](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/hall-of-kings.md) — Dao's extradimensional sanctum; houses all eleven Runeswords on a multi-tier dais; Door manifests in Firethorn Hold
- [tome-of-baldaran](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/tome-of-baldaran.md) — Sentient chronicle of all Eadras history and knowledge
- [mana-metals](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/mana-metals.md) — Mithril, Orichalcum, Kylum, Valthyr and the alloys built from them; Magisterium is the apex tier
- [alien-objects](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/alien-objects.md) — General principle: objects not native to the Mythos have peculiar properties (harder, stronger, weirder)
- [goldenwood](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/goldenwood.md) — Alien-origin tree species growing in scattered groves; the only documented alien-origin *living* organism in canon; extraordinarily durable, remains biologically alive after cutting for years, propagates from cuttings in a process closer to *continuation* than *reproduction*; the canonical example that alien-origin matter is not automatically hostile

## Antagonist Entities

- [demon-prince-of-the-open-wound](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/demon-prince-of-the-open-wound.md) — The demon entity Xar'vion summoned that ignited the Demon War
- [Flesh-Eaters](https://github.com/SilverCheetah/Eadras-Worldbuilding-Wiki/blob/main/wiki/flesh-eaters.md) — Small (<100 dragons at any given time), secretive cult of traditionalist dragons; reject the Council of 99 and every reform of the Great Exchange; hold that all non-draconic sapient life is prey and that thinking creatures taste better; hunt civilization (villages, towns, travelers) from concealed mobile cells with layered primary/secondary lairs; mark territory with **corpse-drops of sapient remains** — the standard draconic territorial signal turned to abnormal target; cabal slang: *horn-horses* (unicorns), *snacks* (humans-sized humanoids), *not even snacks* (gnomes/halflings/goblins, beneath consideration); sustained through ongoing egg-theft and cohort indoctrination; **wiped out many times historically and always reborn from buried doctrinal caches** containing carved doctrine, supremacy plinths, and a **psychic resonance crystal with imprinted personality** that teaches new finders (motto: *"Always reborn"*); declared enemies of dragonkind
