# Ioa

**Summary**: Ioa is the head of the Elder Gods and leader of the [[gods-of-eadras|Eadras pantheon]] — the calm, authoritative arbiter who settles disputes, maintains balance, and assigns absurd menial tasks to the death twins to keep them apart.

**Sources**: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt, Creation Myth.txt, Gods of Eadras.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-04-29

---

## Domain

Sovereign Order; rulership; oversight of the entire pantheon. Ioa settles disputes among the gods and maintains balance among them (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

He was the first of the [[creation-myth|ten Elder Gods]] to step forward in the [[primordial-duo|first naming]], declaring: *"I am Ioa — god of rulership."*

## Personality

Wise, calm, authoritative, and patient. He rarely raises his voice but commands instant respect. Has a dry, understated sense of humor (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

In the moments after the [[creation-myth|first naming]], when [[shali|Shali]] and [[shani|Shani]] began their immortal argument, it was Ioa whose single command — *"Be quiet, both of you."* — froze the twins and stilled the void.

## Relationships

Ioa acts as the neutral mediator of the pantheon (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

- **Closest to** [[nara|Nara]] and [[raja|Raja]] — the parental pair
- **Watchful over** [[nox|Nox]] and [[sylph|Sylph]]
- **Respects** [[lirra|Lirra]]'s importance but **worries about her independence**
- **Frequently keeps** [[shali|Shali]] and [[shani|Shani]] apart and occupied to prevent their constant arguments from disrupting the pantheon

## The Twins' Busywork

Ioa's most documented divine intervention is administrative: he assigns the death twins deliberately menial or absurd tasks to keep them busy and separated. A canonical example: *"personally reaping every fly that dies in the southern wetlands on a given day"* (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

This pattern is treated with affection in mortal liturgy — Ioa is depicted not as a tyrant but as a long-suffering elder sibling running a divine household.
