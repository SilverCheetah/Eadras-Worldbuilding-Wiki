# Celestia

**Summary**: Celestia is the highest plane inside the [[eadras-cosmology|Eadras Mythos]], located dimensionally above the Prime Material Plane — the analogue to Heaven.

**Sources**: Celestia.txt, Internal Cosmology of the Eadras Mythos.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-04-29

---

## Overview

Celestia sits dimensionally above the Prime Material Plane of Eadras. It is the upper plane of the [[eadras-cosmology|Eadras Mythos]] — a place of positive spiritual energy, home to divine presences and blessed souls.

Within Celestia lies **[[solarion]]**, the divine city where the avatars of the [[gods-of-eadras|gods]] gather and play the [[gods-of-eadras|Game of the Gods]].
