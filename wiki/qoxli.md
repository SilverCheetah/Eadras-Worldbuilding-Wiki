# Qoxli

**Summary**: Qoxli is the Elder Goddess of the wild, animals, the bow, forests, freedom, and the chase — the fierce, restless, bluntly honest patron of untamed nature whose closest friendship is with [[sylph|Sylph]] and whose nemesis is [[nox|Nox]].

**Sources**: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt, Creation Myth.txt, Gods of Eadras.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-04-29

---

## Domain

Untamed nature, instinct, hunting, personal freedom (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt). Formal pantheon listing covers the wild, animals, the bow, forests, freedom, and the chase (source: Gods of Eadras.txt).

She was the fifth of the [[creation-myth|ten Elder Gods]] to step forward in the first naming, declaring: *"I am Qoxli — goddess of the wild."* In the great work, she ran the forests. In the [[lirra|Lirran]] cosmological summary, *"Qoxli sings through wildness."*

## Personality

Fierce, exuberant, restless, and bluntly honest. **Lives for the thrill** and has little patience for rules (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

## Relationships

- **Best friends with** [[sylph|Sylph]]
- **Natural rival of** [[vos|Vos]]
- **Wary but respectful toward** [[raja|Raja]] — a respectful rivalry of hunt vs. wild freedom
- **Strongly dislikes** [[nox|Nox]]
