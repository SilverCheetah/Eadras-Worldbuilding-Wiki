# Aqua Vitae

**Summary**: Aqua Vitae â€” *Water of Life* â€” is the universal alchemical solvent and catalyst that underlies most potion-craft on Eadras. It is produced by **distilling a race's signature alcoholic beverage** down to a clean, ultra-refined form, and because the source drink leaves a fingerprint in the result, **each race's Aqua Vitae retains characteristics of its origin** and is therefore prized for different categories of potion.

## What it is

Aqua Vitae is the **purest, strongest spirit a distillation chain can produce** when pushed all the way to refined clarity. It can be produced from a normal fermented beverage (single-step distillation to clean spirit) or from an already-distilled beverage (further refinement). The endpoint is the same: a clear, near-flavorless, very high-proof spirit with the magical property of being an exceptional **solvent and catalyst** for working alchemical mixtures.

This is the substance most potion-craft assumes as its base. Most potions on Eadras â€” healing draughts, magical philters, alchemical reagent mixes, enchantment carriers â€” use Aqua Vitae as the medium that holds the working components in suspension and lets them bind. A potion-maker buys or distills Aqua Vitae the way a smith buys or smelts iron: it is the foundational stock.

## Why each race's Aqua Vitae is different

Distillation removes flavor, color, and most volatile organic matter, but it does **not** remove the magical character of the source drink. Whatever was magically distinctive about the original beverage â€” the magical signature of the herbs, the ley-line resonance of the fermentation, the bound mana of any ingredients â€” carries through into the Aqua Vitae and shapes what the resulting solvent is good at.

This is why the alchemy traditions of every race that produces a signature drink also produce a corresponding signature Aqua Vitae. The Aqua Vitae is not a generic product. It is a **distilled cultural fingerprint**, and potion-makers select among the available Aqua Vitae varieties based on what kind of working they are trying to perform.

## Known Aqua Vitae varieties

### Elven Aqua Vitae

Distilled from [[elfen|Elfen]] [[elfen-blossom-wines|blossom wines]]. Light, subtly bubbly, and carrying a delicate floral-herbal character that survives even the most aggressive refinement. Especially prized in alchemy for potions involving **nature, growth, healing, and life magic**, where it resonates strongly with the natural forces those potions try to express.

The bubbliness is canonically retained from the source wine and is not an artifact â€” it is a magical fingerprint of the Elfen sung-living-wood barrel tradition (see [[elfen-blossom-wines]]). Alchemists who work with Elven Aqua Vitae learn to read the bubble pattern as a quality indicator.

### Other races

Other races' Aqua Vitae varieties exist but are less canonically detailed at present:

- **[[goblins|Goblin]] Ancestor Liquor** can be further distilled into a very harsh, very high-proof Aqua Vitae carrying the brimstone-and-tar fingerprint of the source. Used in alchemy where the working benefits from a *biting* solvent â€” particularly forge-magic, smelting-related potions, and certain combat alchemy. (Open canon: which traditions specifically use it.)
- **[[orcs|Orcish Stonefire]]**, the high-proof distilled spirit produced only in clan holds and cities, refines further into Stonefire Aqua Vitae. Its specific alchemical applications are open canon, but its dual use as a wound-and-instrument sterilizer in everyday orcish medicine suggests its working temperament leans toward purification, sealing, and pain-numbing.
- **Tarran venom-aged liquor**, when distilled, produces a controversial Aqua Vitae that carries the *flavor-without-toxicity* signature of the source's denatured venom. (Whether the magical signature carries the source venom's character even after the venom itself has fully denatured is open canon and may matter for healing-vs-harm potion alignment.) See [[tarans]].
- **[[orden|Orden whale-honey mead]]** distills into an Aqua Vitae with the rich, oceanic, slightly salty-sweet fingerprint of the source. Useful in marine and weather magic; not commonly seen far from Orden territory, since the source mead is itself rare outside their trade compact.

The [[velrathi|Velrathi]] **do not have a native alcoholic drink** â€” they treat alcohol as cognitively detrimental â€” so they have **no Velrathi Aqua Vitae of their own**. Velrathi alchemists working in their own labs source whichever foreign Aqua Vitae best suits the working at hand, and famously keep careful catalogues of which varieties they prefer for which categories of potion.

## Production

The technical production of Aqua Vitae is, by Earth standards, **distillation pushed to its practical maximum** â€” typically a multi-pass column distillation with magical assistance to drive the spirit toward higher purity than ordinary equipment can reach. Most production is done at recognized distilleries within each race's territory; small-batch production by individual alchemists is possible but uneconomical for anything beyond personal use.

Aqua Vitae is shelf-stable and travels well, which makes it the single most-traded alchemical staple on Eadras. Most major trade hubs maintain a permanent stock of three to six varieties to serve their alchemists.

## Open canon

- The **full alchemical character** of each non-Elven Aqua Vitae variety. Only the Elven variety has been canonically sketched in detail; the others have working hypotheses but no codified profiles.
- Whether **double-distillation** (an Aqua Vitae of an Aqua Vitae) produces a meaningfully different substance, or whether the second pass simply yields the same product with no additional refinement.
- The role of Aqua Vitae in pre-Cataclysm [[arathi|Arathi]] alchemy â€” whether it was the same universal-base concept or whether the Arathi had a different, possibly superior, alchemical-base substance that the modern tradition inherits from at one remove.
- Whether **dragon biology** produces any drinkable substance that could in principle be distilled into an Aqua Vitae, and whether the Dragon Truth (the unified Source/Eldritch view) would make such an Aqua Vitae behave fundamentally differently from any race's product.
