# Aurettos Continent

**Summary**: Aurettos is one of two **pre-Cataclysm highlands that survived the [[arathi-cataclysm|Cataclysm]] intact** — distinct from the three continents that formed by sundering the [[altean-highlands|Altean Highlands]]. It is visible in the [[eadras-tectonic-history#1mya post-Cataclysm|1mya post-Cataclysm tectonic snapshot]] as a discrete landmass alongside [[avalon|Avalon]], [[damedes|Damedes]], [[culethor|Culethor]], and [[therion|Therion]].

**Sources**: 1mya.jpg; map explanation.txt; user direction 2026-05-01.

**Last updated**: 2026-05-01

---

## Origin

Aurettos is a **pre-Cataclysm highland** — a continent-class upland that already existed before the [[worldender|Worldbreaker]] fell, distinct from the [[altean-highlands|Altean Highlands]] (which were sundered into [[avalon|Avalon]], [[damedes|Damedes]], and [[culethor|Culethor]]). It survived the catastrophe in continent-scale form. (User direction 2026-05-01.)

This places Aurettos in a small category alongside [[therion|Therion]]: both are non-Altean pre-Cataclysm landmasses that were *not* sundered by the impact. Together they are the only continent-scale survivors of the pre-Cataclysm world that were not derived from the Altean Highlands.

## Position across tectonic snapshots

- **1mya post-Cataclysm** — visible as a distinct continent on the eastern margin of the map, separate from the Altean-triad.
- **2mya post-Cataclysm onward** — Aurettos is not labeled by name on subsequent snapshots in the current map series. Whether it persists as a named landmass, drifts away from the rendered map area, or is absorbed into the [[eldravath|Eldravath]] supercontinent at 4mya is currently undefined. The eastern features visible at 4mya (Sundered Atoll, Windward Isles) may be Aurettos-derived.
