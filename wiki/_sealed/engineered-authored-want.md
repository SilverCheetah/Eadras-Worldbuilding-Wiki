# The Authored Want of the Engineered Races (SEALED)

**This is OOC author/plot-spine canon. It is NOT in the public Tome.** Do not wikilink to this page from any public page; do not summarize its contents into [[velrathi]], [[orugi]], [[peoples-of-eadras]], or any other public page.

**Summary**: The darkest, most exclusively-held of the three eldest-race buried truths. The [[velrathi|Velrathi]] engineered their child races ([[orugi|Orug]], [[grugi|Grug]], [[rathari|Rathar]]) by reshaping their own embryos across generations until the offspring were born **already fitted to purpose AND disposed to want it**. The "satisfaction of purpose" the public canon attributes to them is, at bottom, an **authored disposition** — not a freely chosen value. The Velrathi genuinely **cannot see it**. No living or divine mind ever recognizes it. Only the [[tome-of-baldaran|Tome]] holds the truth.

## The seam

The [[velrathi-architecture|Stone-Bound]] escaped the consent problem: the genius loci **pre-existed** and **accepted** the binding (patron-and-protégé). The engineered races did **not**. A Grug did not exist and then consent to being a maintainer; the Velrathi reshaped their own offspring until a Grug was born already loving the work, an Orug born already loyal, a Rathar born already hungry for the page. The Grug "respect for work," Orug loyalty, Rathar scholarly hunger are **engineered dispositions, not chosen values.**

## The keystone image: the Orug unanimity

The [[orugi|Orug's]] unanimous march to extinction — every last one, singing their oaths, at the [[battle-of-the-broken-conclaves|Final Wall]] — is canon's proudest image of free noble sacrifice **and** the darkest evidence of unfreedom. A truly free people *fractures* under existential threat; some run, some break, some refuse. **Unanimity** reveals an engineered disposition overriding individual will. "The satisfaction of purpose" is the velvet on the chain: the deepest bondage is being **made to want it.**

## Why it stays buried (the three locks)

1. **Sincere blindness.** The Velrathi genuinely cannot see it. They truly believe the engineered races are freely fulfilled, because the engineered contentment presents *as* genuine fulfillment **from both sides**. There is no hypocrisy — there is no awareness. This is what makes all the warm public facts *true-as-experienced*: the intermarriage celebrated as "strengthened blood," the self-governance, the ladder up via House induction, the genuine grief at the Orug's fall. The Velrathi hold the chain and cannot see it; they think it is just love. (Same cognitive structure as the [[velrathi]] "sugar-cane tell" — a genuine optimization-frame that self-conceals a truth.)
2. **Never confronted.** No living or divine mind ever recognizes that the Orug unanimity was *authored*. It stays latent. The Orug sacrifice stands eternally as Eadras's image of free noble choice while being its most complete unfreedom — no villain, no cover-up, no reveal.
3. **But the Tome knows.** It is not unknowable: the [[tome-of-baldaran|Tome of Baldaran]] records it (the cosmic observer holds what no mind perceives, I.A.1 / I.A.2). So it is a Tome-held, **latent, discoverable** truth — a live fuse, matching the other two secrets, not a dead end. It is the **most exclusively held** of the three (Tome-ONLY), because uniquely no living or divine mind can even *perceive* it.

## The Velrathi keystone

The comprehend-**everything** race has **one perfect blind spot**, located exactly at their gravest moral act: the authored freedom of their own children. Their defining virtue (comprehend all) and their deepest failure share an address. Final irony: the archive-revering race's indictment sits in the ultimate Archive (the Tome), readable in principle, **unread** because the blind spot forecloses the very question.

## The dark mirror, completed

The three eldest-race secrets share one architecture (Tome-held buried truths, invisible to makers and made alike); they differ only in valence and custody-breadth:

- **[[elfen-hidden-hands|Elfen hidden hands]]** — love woven in; **blooms** on reveal.
- **[[velrathi-hidden-hands|Velrathi hidden hands]]** — knowledge whose darker face ([[nox|Nox]]) is worn on the body.
- **This page — authored want** — indicts the **makers**; Tome-only; never perceived.

See also the [[velena-shani-mercy|Shani-mercy Demon-War root]] (indicts the **gods**) and the [[founders-return-grief|founders' return]] (Dao's concealment, gated by free choice).
