# The Velrathi Hidden Hands (SEALED)

**This is OOC author/plot-spine canon. It is NOT in the public Tome.** Do not wikilink to this page from any public page; do not summarize its contents into [[velrathi]] or any other public page. Public-facing breadcrumbs only (see bottom).

**Summary**: The dark mirror of the [[elfen-hidden-hands|Elfen hidden hands]]. When [[the-three|Rand]] made the [[velrathi|Velrathi]], two gods he had formed "sort-of friendships" with secretly wove pieces of themselves into the race: **[[lirra|Lirra]]** and **[[nox|Nox]]** — the two faces of knowledge. The Velrathi do not know. Where Mark's hidden hands (Qoxli + Sylph) *harmonize* and make the Elfen a balance, **Lirra and Nox do not harmonize**, and the unresolved tension between them is the engine of Velrathi history.

## The two hands

Rand's bonds were **cool / instrumental** ("sort-of friendships"), not Mark's warm loves — Rand befriended gods as **sources**. The two who helped him are the two faces of knowledge:

- **[[lirra|Lirra]]** (magic, the Weave, mother of every Netharim) — knowledge as **lawful structure**. The daily engine of the Velrathi. In daily life **Lirra leads**: the Velrathi are far more rigorous empiricists than dark transgressors.
- **[[nox|Nox]]** (night, secrets, undeath, the loathsome) — the **permission-set**: nothing is off-limits to *study*. Not the daily drive — the **willingness**. "No forbidden arts" is Nox's content permitted; the prestige-publication brake is Nox's *secrecy* denied.

The Velrathi are **Lirra's method with Nox's permissions**. Their sinister reputation is mostly outsider projection + the post-exile Nox-rising + the rare catastrophic exception ([[xarvion|Xarvion]]). The Lirra/Nox tension is real, but Lirra governs in peacetime.

## The greying is the hidden hand made flesh

The single hardest-to-deny part of this secret is that **the grey skin and red eyes are Nox in the bloodline, literally.** The public canon ([[velrathi]], [[peoples-of-eadras]]) now holds that the iconic phenotype is **acquired** — generations of pregnant Velrathi using Nyxar's negative energy greyed the race one pregnancy at a time, a self-accelerating ratchet. What the public canon does **not** say is *why* negative energy was ever in the weave to begin with, or that the propensity toward it is Nox's instilled strand. The Velrathi tipped toward Nox culturally **and** became visibly Nox-touched through their own choices — and the deepest layer is that Nox was in them from creation, pulling.

This is why the Velrathi reveal lands **darker** than the Elfen's: a hidden goddess of the wild is a consecration; a hidden god of the loathsome, whose mark you wear on your own face and pass to your children, is something else. It would hit **Nox-worshippers hardest** (the grey skin as unwitting testament to the hidden hand).

## The cultural Nox-rising tracks the same tip

The Lirra→Nox tip happens at two scales:

- **Individually** — it produced [[xarvion|Xarvion]] (the appetite weaponized by grief).
- **Culturally** — the **Banishment** shattered the prestige-publication economy and dropped the exiles into [[glimmerdark|Glimmerdark]], **Nox's own domain made into a world**. Pre-exile Velrathi were radically transparent (Lirra ascendant); the secretive, paranoid, stranger-arts character is a *post-exile* development — Nox rising at the cultural scale. The public **Nox worship-vs-acknowledge split** (some exiled Velrathi worship him; most pre-exile merely *acknowledged* a proven god) is the countable religious measure of the same arc — and a safe public breadcrumb, because it never names the hidden hand.

## Reveal status (held open, per I.G.32)

Whether the Lirra/Nox hands stay hidden or can be revealed is **unruled**. A reveal would land darker than the Elfen's. Note it would hit Nox-worshippers hardest. Park until the user rules.
