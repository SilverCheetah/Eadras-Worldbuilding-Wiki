# The Elfen Hidden Hands (SEALED)

**This is OOC author/plot-spine canon. It is NOT in the public Tome.** Do not wikilink to this page from any public page; do not summarize its contents into [[elfen]] or any other public page. Public-facing breadcrumbs only (see the bottom of this page).

**Summary**: Everyone — including Mark himself, the other two of the Three, and every Elfen who ever lived — believes that **Mark alone** made the [[elfen|Elfen]]. This is false. Over the 11,000-year slow childhood, Mark grew close to two Elder Goddesses, [[qoxli|Qoxli]] and [[sylph|Sylph]] (who are themselves best friends). When Mark made the Elfen, **each of them secretly wove a piece of herself into the race** — not a cold enhancement, but a genuine instilling of her own nature. No one knows. It is a true fact about Elfen nature that is **unsourceable from within the world**.

**Sources**: elfen_consolidated.txt (Section 0, Force Check §5, Values), 2026-06-08 ingest; user direction 2026-06-08 (seal the buried truths).

## The two hands

- **[[qoxli|Qoxli's]] strand** — wild-attunement, hunt-craft, the **bow** and master archery, reverence-in-killing ("kill to mend the web"), the easy comfort of wild animals. The Elfen field-craft, the herbivores that beg from a warden's hand, the cull performed with ritual regret — all of it is **Qoxli's direct hand**, not merely Mark's design. The Elfen carry a piece of Qoxli in them.
- **[[sylph|Sylph's]] strand** — aesthetic over-enthusiasm (the ~500 teas, joy past need, indulgent play) **and** the ennui-defense (the discipline of *facing each day new*, the cure for the sameness that kills an immortal mind). Sylph is never the same twice; she gave the Elfen a piece of that protean freshness, and it is the **literal cure** for the ennui that would otherwise crack an immortal at the Law-6 wall. The Elfen will to *find each day new* **is Sylph in them**.

## Why it is load-bearing

- **Temperament = two divine loves held in balance.** Qoxli is sacred **gravity** (reverence, the hunt, the held-sacred); Sylph is joyful **flight** (whim, indulgence, the rule broken for joy). Only-Qoxli would have produced severe wardens; only-Sylph produced the **Fae** (which is why Mark's Sidhe line leans full-Sylph and *became* Fae). The **Elfen hold both.** This two-stranded temperament is Mark's own inner life — his semi-romantic love of Qoxli-as-the-wild and his deep friendship with Sylph-as-chaos — woven into a people.
- **It closes the equilibrium loop.** The Elfen keep the world's balance because they *are* a balance — of two divine natures in tension. Equilibrium-keeping is not a job assigned to them; it is the outward expression of what they are made of. The public [[elfen]] page can say *they are equilibrium-keepers because of who Mark was*; the deeper truth is that two goddesses are in the weave.
- **Mark's nature forecloses jealousy.** Mark believes his hand alone made them. The reveal would **delight** him, not wound him — *"the two I love chose to be in the thing I love most, forever."* His wonder-native, love-formed temperament cannot read it as betrayal.

## The reveal blooms (the dark mirror's bright pole)

This secret is dormant but **revealable** — "waiting for the right hands." Its resolution is **warm on every side**: Mark delighted; the Elfen **honored** (a reverent people receives a hidden divine origin as *consecration*, not rupture). It is therefore an instrument of the setting's **earned hope** — in grimdark it would be a horror (gods meddling without consent); here it is a kindness that **blooms** on contact with light.

It is the **bright pole** of the three eldest-race buried truths, all of which share one architecture (Tome-held truths invisible to makers and made alike):

- **Elfen hidden hands** (this page) — love woven in; **blooms** on reveal.
- **[[velrathi-hidden-hands|Velrathi hidden hands]]** ([[lirra|Lirra]] + [[nox|Nox]]) — lands **darker** (Nox is literally in their bodies).
- **[[engineered-authored-want|The authored want of the engineered races]]** — indicts the makers; Tome-only.

Compare also the [[velena-shani-mercy|Shani-mercy Demon-War root]]: an indictment that *detonates*, where this one *heals*.

## Seams (held open, per I.G.32)

- **Sylph-worship asymmetry.** The Elfen openly worship Qoxli (goddess of the wild they tend) but, per current canon, **not** Sylph — yet Sylph's gift is equally in them. Sylph is patron of the Fae. The Elfen may carry Sylph's gift unworshipped while the Sidhe venerate her openly. When the secret surfaces it lands on *existing* devotion (they already revere Qoxli) and deepens it.
- **[[aramet|Aramet]]'s forgotten mortal name.** Gods take new names at apotheosis; deep-history Elfen memory may hold the mortal Elfen name of a goddess now worshipped under her divine one. This rhymes with the hidden-hands reveal — a truth about the divine that was always in the people.
- **The richest arc — intuition before knowledge.** A far-tail ancient Elfen (20,000 years deep, in profound psionic communion) may *sense* the unsourceable wildness and whim in their own kind — too vast to be Mark's hand, too protean to be design — and be unable to **name** it, because Qoxli/Sylph-in-them is knowledge no Elfen has. The gift sensing itself, through the very faculty the gifts made them good at.

## Public-facing breadcrumbs (these MAY appear in [[elfen]], as mystery — never as the answer)

- That a rare, very old Elfen in deep attunement sometimes reports sensing *something in their own people too vast and too changeable to be wholly their maker's hand* — and cannot say what it is. Written as in-world mystery, never resolved.
- That the Elfen worship Qoxli with a devotion that outsiders find disproportionate for a people "merely made by Mark." (The disproportion is the clue; the page must not explain it.)

Do **not** state, hint, or imply on any public page that Qoxli or Sylph contributed to the Elfen's making.
