# Daj

**Summary**: Daj is the Elder God of war — sharpener of blades, strong ally of [[raja|Raja]], and the recipient of [[sylph|Sylph]]'s flirtatious rivalry.

**Sources**: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt, Creation Myth.txt, Gods of Eadras.txt, More corrections.txt

**Last updated**: 2026-04-30

---

## Domain

War, strife, conflict, armies, fortresses, weapons (source: Gods of Eadras.txt).

He was the sixth of the [[creation-myth|ten Elder Gods]] to step forward in the first naming, declaring: *"I am Daj — god of war."* In the great work, **he sharpened the blades** (source: Creation Myth.txt). In the [[lirra|Lirran]] cosmological summary of the Elder Gods, *"Daj roars in battle."*

## Relationships

- **Strong ally of** [[raja|Raja]]
- **Flirtatious rivalry with** [[sylph|Sylph]]

## Celestial aspect — the Red Moon

The **Red Moon** of Eadras is named for Daj. It is the **second-largest** of [[four-moons-of-eadras|Eadras's four moons]], after [[gaia|Gaia]] (the Green Moon) and before the [[shali|Shali]]–[[shani|Shani]] crystal-moon pair. Its red coloration is consistent with Daj's domain of war, blade-sharpening, and conflict; mortal cultures often invoke its position in the sky as an omen for conflict-related divinations.

Like all four moons of Eadras, Daj is **reflected across the Feywild and Shadowdark** — there is a Daj in each plane, in the same way there is a [[gaia|Gaia]] in each plane. (source: More corrections.txt)
