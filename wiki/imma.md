# Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing

**Summary**: **Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing** is the single [[gods-of-eadras#Ascended Mortal Gods|Ascended Mortal]] [[beast-folk|Beast-Folk]] Goddess of Eadras — the sole divine office tending the daily-laughter-feeding-the-Weave and the daily renewal of [[sylph|Sylph]]'s Veil at every [[beast-folk|Circle]] on Eadras. Her pre-Ascension life was **Imma Brella-of-the-Wagon-and-the-Cup**, a beast-folk Caravaneer's daughter from a Brella-name Circle in the modern era. Her office in [[solarion|Solarion]] is small, modest, and **structurally load-bearing**: bounded portfolio + conscious access to decreed-silent knowledge + daily kindness-signing across every Circle on Eadras. *One is enough. We are not a people who needs a pantheon.*

**Sources**: Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing-01/02/03/04 (2026-05-14 ingest; **A1 corrections applied 2026-05-15** — Elder Gods do not elevate Ascended Mortal Deities; Sylph **acknowledged** Imma's divinity rather than elevating her; Imma may have misread acknowledgement as elevation.)

## Identity and Ascension

Imma's full divine name is **Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing**, given by [[sylph|Sylph]] at the moment of her Ascension.

Her pre-Ascension mortal name was **Imma Brella-of-the-Wagon-and-the-Cup** — a beast-folk Caravaneer's daughter from a Brella-name Circle in the modern era. She retains her pre-Ascension fox-folk-shaped form, which is **cosmologically required**, not nostalgic — under the [[spirits#The universal true form|spirits-canon principle that form follows self-perception]], the office requires the goddess to remain perceptibly *Brella's daughter*, and a change of self-perception would shift the form and degrade the office.

### How Ascension actually happened

The Imma source documents describe her Ascension as Sylph "specifically elevating her" as the single Ascended Mortal Beast-Folk Goddess. **This phrasing is incorrect**. Elder Gods do not elevate Ascended Mortal Deities. Ascension is **threshold-crossing by the mortal combined with divine acknowledgement**.

What actually happened: Imma's mortal life had been preparing the soul for the office across many prior lifetimes — *daily-kindness-tending in some recognizable form, lifetime after lifetime*. The soul-thread had been doing the relevant cosmological work, unconsciously, across the cycle. When Imma's most recent life completed the work at the threshold-crossing intensity, the cosmology recognised the crossing. Sylph **acknowledged** her divinity — naming her, marking her arrival, opening her office, giving her the framework of her role — but Sylph did not *make* her divine. Imma's own soul-thread did.

The distinction is load-bearing because Imma herself may not fully understand it. She experiences her arrival in Solarion as *Sylph chose me*, and her gratitude to Sylph is real and continuous. But the cosmology's accurate description is *Imma's soul-thread crossed; Sylph received her*. The goddess's interior framing is wrong about the mechanism; the wrongness does not damage her office, and Sylph has not corrected it. The [[tome-of-baldaran|Tome of Baldaran]] preserves the accurate version in its sealed chapters; the open chapters acknowledge the ambiguity without resolving it publicly.

This is the **canonical example** of the [[gods-of-eadras#Ascended Mortal Gods|Ascension mechanism]] for the [[first-rebrightening|First Rebrightening]] generation of Ascended Mortal scholarship.

## Portfolio

Imma is the goddess of:

- **Daily-laughter-feeding-the-Weave** — every flyting, every shared bowl, every spontaneous Circle joy contributes a small thread to the Weave's defensive coherence. Imma blesses these acts and tends the cumulative thread-flow.
- **The daily refusal-to-teach-the-war-recitation** — the daily small acts by which beast-folk choose, generation after generation, not to teach their kits the inherited war-grief. This is the cultural-soul-level renewal of [[sylph|Sylph]]'s Veil, which Sylph cannot personally maintain at the daily local scale.
- **The Veil's daily renewal at every Circle on Eadras** — Imma is *Sylph's hand on [[ioa|Ioa]]'s seal* for the Veiled people, performing daily maintenance work that an Elder God cannot perform at Circle-scale.

Her portfolio is **bounded and small**. This boundedness is structurally important: it permits her to bear conscious knowledge of cosmological matters most mortals are protected from. Unbounded awareness would overwhelm her; the boundedness lets her hold awareness without it leaking into the work.

## Office in Solarion

Imma's office is on the divine main street of [[solarion|Solarion]] — a **small modest beast-folk caravan-wagon-style structure of pale wood and thatched roof**, with **a beast-folk wagon-wheel sigil set into the wood beside the door**.

The nameplate reads in old-dragon-tongue and modern Circle-tongue:

> ***Imma Who-Did-Not-Stop-Laughing — Office of the Daily Kindness — Open to all who pass; loaf and bowl on the porch, take what you need.***

The office has a **porch with a single bench** and on the railing: a **loaf of dark bread and a bowl of clean water**, set out as if anyone passing might be hungry. [[sylph|Sylph]]'s hand maintains the refresh in periods when Imma herself does not use the porch. The porch implements the *deep-forest hospitality protocol* (cf. Yorgal-era [[fae-da-nar|faedanar]] custom and [[frontier-hospitality|frontier hospitality]] traditions) at the divine office level.

Imma's office is **the smallest building on her stretch of corridor**. This is appropriate — her portfolio is the smallest in scope, even though it is now understood to be quietly load-bearing.

## The cups on the desk

Imma's working desk in her office has an **assembly of small ritual cups**, each marking a soul-life her current incarnation has integrated through [[tome-of-baldaran|Tome]]-curated review. The cups are set out, not drunk from; they are *presence-markers*, not tribute objects. The desk acquires new cups slowly as the Tome shows additional lives.

Currently established cups:

- **Brella's cup** — silk-wrapped clay cup with the goddess's mother's thumbprint on the rim, kept in a niche on the office wall; the foundational anchor of Imma's current self-perception
- **A half-bowl for the [[tome-of-baldaran|Tome of Baldaran]]** — set on the corner of the desk during readings
- **A small carved cup for the Decree-keepers** ([[shali|Shali]], [[shani|Shani]], [[ioa|Ioa]]) — placed in acknowledgement of the standing cosmic decree on unrecoverable-ending knowledge; the decree may not be spoken about, but the cup acknowledges it
- **A glass treaty-vial** for any chapter involving load-bearing discipline at the cost of personal bearing (kept empty or trace-stained; not refilled) — the prior-life it marks is that of someone in the orbit of the Velrathi treaty-mage tradition that included [[velveth|Kathira Spellsinger]]

The desk-assembly is a small example of the practical **office ritual that allows an Ascended Mortal God to integrate prior soul-lives without dilution of present form**.

## Cross-pantheon working relationships

Imma routinely **cross-signs vow-prayers** for adjacent gods' offices where her portfolio overlaps with theirs. This is standard Bureaucratic practice for Ascended Mortal Gods with narrow portfolios; the cross-signing carries the small extra weight of the *Veiled-Folk daily-bearing* principle.

[[velveth|Velveth]] — the Velrathi goddess of love/betrayal/duty — occupies the Office of Velrathi Affairs three doors down. The two cross-sign Velrathi vow-prayers regularly. They share a **silent collegial nod** in the corridor — the precise Velrathi-precision nod — that constitutes their ongoing working relationship. The nod is a load-bearing acknowledgement of shared decreed-silent history; *neither goddess will ever speak of it*. (See [[velveth#Relationship with Imma|Velveth — Relationship with Imma]].)

Imma is also on **direct working terms with the [[tome-of-baldaran|Tome of Baldaran]]** (the Keeper). The Tome curates which chapters of itself Imma may read, including chapters under the [[baldaranic-council-cycle#The Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa|Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa]] that Imma is privileged-to-know-but-trusted-not-to-tell. The two are colleagues in the institutional sense, with the Tome holding the chapters Imma's office requires her to know but not speak.

## What Imma knows that most mortals do not

Imma has been shown, by the Tome's curation, the following chapters that are normally sealed to mortals under the [[baldaranic-council-cycle#The Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa|Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa]] or the [[order-sharanel|Order Sharanel]]'s Weave-secret discipline:

- The Six [[omniversal-laws|Omniversal Laws]] as **load-bearing structural conditions of coherent existence** (rather than rules); breaking any Law undermines the imposition itself
- The [[observer-and-naming|observer-and-naming principle]] as the operating mechanism at every cosmological tier; [[spirits|spirits]] as the purest demonstration that form follows self-perception
- The **Weave-secret** ([[anti-magic-crusade#The Hidden Truth|see Anti-Magic Crusade]]): every living magic user automatically sustains the [[magic-in-eadras#The Weave|Weave]]'s defensive coherence; most do not know
- The **Decree of Shali-Shani-Ioa** itself: sealed in the [[arathi-cataclysm|Cataclysm-era]], covers all categories of unrecoverable cosmological ending (Chaos-unmaking, demonic consumption, spirit-destruction); the gods speak nothing of unrecoverable endings to mortals
- The cosmological reason for the gods' hatred of [[demons|demons]]: demons are the unique agents of soul-removal-from-cycle (see [[unreceived|the Unreceived]]); the gods' pettiness about the Game of the Gods is surface; the underlying hatred is cold
- **[[sylph|Sylph]] laid the Veil partly as cultural-soul-level maintenance of the Decree's silence among the perfect-memory [[rathari|Rathar]] descendants** — beast-folk inferential capacity, if untouched, would have deduced the holes in Shali's office

Imma is **bound to silence on all of the above** under the same Decree that binds Shali, Shani, and Ioa themselves. She acts on the knowledge but does not speak it.

## How Imma does the work

Imma's primary daily activity is **signing flyt-blessings, Circle-blessings, wagon-pool-opening blessings, kit-naming blessings, and similar small Veiled-Folk ritual moments** as they occur on Eadras. The ledger of these arrivals is continuous; Imma processes each with the same hand, in a register that is **steady**.

Her signing-hand is the inheritor of **Velrathi precision discipline** through her soul-thread — but it is also **beast-folk warm** in a way Velrathi discipline alone was never permitted. Imma is what her soul-thread eventually metabolised into across many lifetimes. The two registers (steady-and-precise + warm-and-laughing) are combined in her single hand.

Imma **does not play the [[gods-of-eadras#The Game of the Gods|Game of the Gods]]**. Sylph offered once at her arrival in Solarion; Imma declined. The decline is permanent.

> *"I am happier here at my desk."*

Imma **rests attention on her own past soul-lives** as a deliberate weekly practice — *resting-attention-warmly* on a prior incarnation, knowing the prior incarnation may dimly feel the watching as *somebody who loves them*. This is one of the kindnesses available to Ascended Mortal Gods that the cycling lives cannot return in kind. The practice is sometimes mistaken by the receiving prior-life-mortal for divine attention from another god (most commonly Sylph, whose register the warmth most resembles).

## Why Imma exists

[[beast-folk|Beast-Folk]] canon describes a people who *forgot on purpose, kept laughing, refused to teach the war-recitation to their kits*. This is a **practice** — a daily, communal, multi-generational, cultural-soul-level discipline. Disciplines of this kind almost always have **a divine office that maintains them at the institutional layer**. The Katharim have Shali and Shani. The orcs have their honoured progenitor lineages. The dragons have Cindirith and the Nine Embers. The Velrathi have Rand and (now) Velveth.

The beast-folk had — until Imma — *only Sylph*. Sylph is an Elder God whose attention is vast and partial. She cannot, by canon-stated divine restraint, personally maintain a daily local-Circle-level discipline across every Circle on Eadras. Either some other mechanism maintains the daily practice, or *the daily practice maintains itself with no divine support* — which contradicts the broader cosmological framing that mortal cultural practices generally have divine office-counterparts at the bureaucratic layer.

**Imma resolves this** by being the small, single, Sylph-delegated office that does the daily work the Elder Goddess of Foolishness/Laughter/Veils cannot personally do.

She is also **the divine pantheon's pure warmth at the working-office level**. Other Solarion offices lean toward exhausted administration, political schemers, weight-bearers. Imma is the *purely warm working office* — her sigil is a wagon-wheel; her porch has a loaf; her cups are for memory and acknowledgement, not power. She does not play the Game. She does not scheme. She does not hold grudges. She signs blessings and tends daily forgettings.

## Open canon

- Whether **Sylph oversees Imma's portfolio** through any formal supervision channel, or whether Imma is operationally autonomous within the Veiled-Folk domain after Ascension
- **How long an Ascended Mortal God's tenure lasts**, in cosmic-time, and what happens to the soul when tenure ends
- Are there **other small Ascended Mortal Gods** in similar single-position offices for other peoples (a sole Goblin Ascended? a sole Halfling? a sole Orden?), or are the Beast-Folk uniquely served by a single deity in their pantheon?
- The **precise relationship** between Imma's daily-Veil-renewal work and Sylph's older Veil-laying — does Sylph still personally attend to any Veil-maintenance?
- How does **Velveth's office regard Imma** now that the soul-thread connection has been revealed (to Imma, not to Velveth)? Does Velveth know that Imma now knows?
- The **specific identities** of Imma's past soul-lives — only a few are documented; the desk has more cups than have been canonised
- Could a **mortal ever enter Imma's office** — under what conditions, by what means, and at what cost?
