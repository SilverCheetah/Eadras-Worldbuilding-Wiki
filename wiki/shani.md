# Shani

**Summary**: Shani is one of the twin Elder Goddesses of death, born from the splitting of the tenth Elder God at creation — the cold, ruthless, unforgiving half who drags the wicked to eternal torment, so feared that mortals refuse to speak her name and call her only "Death Itself."

**Sources**: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt, Creation Myth.txt, Gods of Eadras.txt, More corrections.txt, Grok Chat Results 6-2-26.txt (2026-06-02, crystal-moon detail)

**Last updated**: 2026-06-02

---

## Domain

Death and the judgment of souls — specifically, dragging the wicked to eternal torment (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

In the [[creation-myth|first naming]], Shani shoved in right after [[shali|Shali]]: *"No, you're not. I'm Shani — goddess of death."*

## The Split at Creation

Shani and [[shali|Shali]] are **two halves of the same original soul**, split at creation because each half interpreted the divine decree regarding death differently (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt). The tenth Elder God, intended to be one being, came into existence already torn — one half stern, the other gentle.

This split is canonized in the [[creation-myth|Creation Myth]] as *"Death, born torn."*

## Personality

Cold, ruthless, and unforgiving (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt). Constantly bickers with [[shali|Shali]] over whose approach to death is superior — their rivalry creates real tension in the pantheon.

## Mortal Perception

Shani is **so feared that most people refuse to speak her name aloud**, simply calling her **"Death Itself"** (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

### A Quiet Affection

Because her name is uttered so rarely, **every time someone does speak Shani's name, she pays very close attention and quietly appreciates it** (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt). This is the closest thing the Goddess of Eternal Torment has to a soft spot.

This piece of canon has practical implications for any mortal who knows it: invoking Shani by name is not a curse, but a kind of greeting — and one she rewards with attention. Whether that attention is desirable depends entirely on the speaker's circumstances.

## Splitting the First Soul

After the great sacrifice of [[primordial-duo|Evye and Fryr]], Shani and [[shali|Shali]] — *still bickering* — split the first soul: one to light, one to dark (source: Creation Myth.txt).

## Strong Rivalry With Nox

Shani has a **strong rivalry with** [[nox|Nox]] (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt). She sees his liberal use of [[necromancy-and-undeath|undeath and necromancy]] as **meddling with what should be final**. She does not view necromancy as inherently evil — only as a violation of her domain.

## Ioa's Management

[[ioa|Ioa]] frequently assigns Shani and Shali absurd menial tasks to keep them apart and prevent their arguments from disrupting the pantheon (source: More Info on the Elder Gods.txt).

## Celestial aspect — twin crystal moon

One of the two **twin crystal moons** of Eadras is named for Shani. Shani and her twin [[shali|Shali]] are the third- and fourth-largest of [[four-moons-of-eadras|Eadras's four moons]]; they orbit each other while jointly orbiting Eadras, and are seen together in the sky as a coupled pair. The crystal-pair imagery in the heavens echoes the irreducible doubleness of the death-twin domain. The moons are each **roughly the size of Charon** and **made entirely of the crystal used in [[crystalmancy|crystal magic]] and technology**; the pair shines one **blue** and one **purple** (which colour belongs to which goddess is unspecified). (source: Grok Chat Results 6-2-26.txt)

Like all four moons of Eadras, Shani is **reflected across the Wyldglimmer and Glimmerdark** — there is a Shani (and a Shali) in each plane. (source: More corrections.txt)
